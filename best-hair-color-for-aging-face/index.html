Question and Answer

Q: Can I dye my hair at home, or should I visit a professional colorist?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair at home, visiting a professional colorist can ensure that you achieve the best results. They have the knowledge and expertise to analyze your skin tone and eye color and recommend the most flattering shades for you.

Q: Will dyeing my hair damage it?

A: Dyeing your hair can cause some damage, especially if you choose a drastic color change or use harsh chemicals. However, using high-quality hair dye and following a proper hair care routine can minimize damage and keep your hair healthy and vibrant.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: The frequency of touch-ups depends on several factors, including the rate of your hair growth and the type of hair dye used. On average, most people need to touch up their roots every 4-6 weeks to maintain a consistent hair color.

Q: Can I dye my hair if it's already damaged?

A: If your hair is already damaged, it's best to consult with a professional colorist before dyeing it. They can assess the condition of your hair and recommend the best course of action to minimize further damage.
