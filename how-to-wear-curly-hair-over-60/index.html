Recommendations for How to Wear Curly Hair Over 60

1. Find a stylist who specializes in curly hair: A knowledgeable and experienced stylist can help you find the right haircut and provide guidance on how to care for and style your curls.

2. Use products designed for curly hair: Look for products that are specifically formulated to enhance and define curls. These may include shampoos, conditioners, leave-in treatments, and styling products.

3. Experiment with different styling techniques: There are various techniques, such as plopping, diffusing, and finger coiling, that can help enhance and define your curls. Try different methods to find what works best for your hair type.

4. Embrace your natural texture: Instead of trying to straighten or hide your curls, embrace and enhance them. This may involve using products that nourish and define your curls, as well as finding a haircut that complements your natural texture.

How to Wear Curly Hair Over 60: Tips and Tricks

1. Choose the right haircut: Opt for a haircut that enhances your curls and suits your face shape. Consider shorter styles, such as a pixie cut or a bob, that can add volume and definition to your curls.

2. Use a diffuser when drying your hair: Instead of towel-drying your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to gently dry your curls. This helps to minimize frizz and enhance the shape and definition of your curls.

3. Avoid heat styling: Excessive heat can damage your curls and lead to frizz and dryness. Try to minimize the use of heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, and opt for heat-free styling methods instead.

4. Protect your curls while sleeping: Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase or use a silk or satin bonnet or scarf to protect your curls while you sleep. This helps to prevent friction and maintain the shape and definition of your curls.
