To blow dry a bob with thick hair, start by applying a heat protectant spray to your damp hair. This will help protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy. Blow Drying a Bob with Thick Hair: Techniques and Tips

Blow drying a bob with thick hair requires some specific techniques and tips to achieve the best results. Here are a few that I have found helpful:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and secure them with clips.
  3. Use a round brush to lift and dry each section, starting from the roots.
  4. Dry the roots first to create volume.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to set the style and keep it in place.
