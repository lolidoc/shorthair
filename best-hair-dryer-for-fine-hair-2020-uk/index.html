Tips for Using the Best Hair Dryer for Fine Hair 2020 UK

When using the best hair dryer for fine hair in 2020 UK, there are a few tips to keep in mind. Firstly, always start with towel-dried hair to remove excess moisture. Use a heat protectant spray to shield your hair from heat damage. Divide your hair into sections and use a round brush to lift the roots and add volume. Set the hair dryer to a low heat setting and aim the nozzle downwards to prevent frizz. Finally, finish with a blast of cool air to set the style and add shine.

Common Questions About Best Hair Dryer for Fine Hair 2020 UK

Q: Can using a hair dryer damage fine hair?

A: Using a hair dryer with proper techniques and the right heat settings should not damage fine hair. It is important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid excessive heat.

Q: How often should I use a hair dryer on fine hair?

A: It is recommended to limit heat styling on fine hair to two to three times a week to prevent damage. On non-styling days, allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Q: Can a hair dryer add volume to fine hair?

A: Yes, a hair dryer can add volume to fine hair. Using a round brush and lifting the roots while drying can create the illusion of fuller and more voluminous hair.

Q: What is the best way to prevent frizz when using a hair dryer on fine hair?

A: To prevent frizz, it is important to use a hair dryer with ion technology and aim the nozzle downwards while drying. Using a heat protectant spray and finishing with a blast of cool air can also help.
