Question and Answer

Q: Can I pull off a short haircut with a round face?

A: Absolutely! Short haircuts can look fantastic on round faces. The key is to add layers and texture to create movement and dimension. Consider a pixie cut with textured layers or a short bob with face-framing highlights.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules, some hairstyles may not be as flattering for a round face. Avoid blunt and straight-across bangs, as they can make your face appear wider. It's also best to steer clear of chin-length bobs, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Q: How often should I trim my hair if I have a round face?

A: Regular trims are important for maintaining the shape and style of your haircut, regardless of your face shape. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! Wearing your hair up can be a great way to change up your look and show off your face shape. Opt for updos that add height and volume at the crown, such as a messy bun with face-framing tendrils.
