Best Hairstyles for Fine Hair 2021: Adding Volume with Styling Techniques

When it comes to adding volume to fine hair, there are a few styling techniques you can try. One option is to blow-dry your hair upside down using a round brush. This can help create lift and volume at the roots. Another technique is to use hot rollers or a curling iron to add texture and body to your hair.

You can also try backcombing or teasing your hair at the roots to add volume. However, be careful not to damage your hair in the process. Use a fine-toothed comb or a teasing brush and gently backcomb small sections of hair. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the volume in place.

Best Hairstyles for Fine Hair 2021: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear a bob with fine hair?

A: Yes, a bob can be a great option for fine hair. Adding layers to a bob can help create movement and add volume to your hair.

Q: Will a pixie cut make my fine hair look thinner?

A: No, a pixie cut can actually make fine hair appear fuller and more voluminous. The key is to focus on adding texture and movement to the hair.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have fine hair?

A: While there are no specific hairstyles to avoid, it's best to avoid styles that require a lot of heat styling or products that can weigh your hair down. Opt for lightweight products and gentle styling techniques.

Q: How often should I trim my fine hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its health and prevent split ends.
