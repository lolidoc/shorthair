Tips for Short Bob Haircuts for Fine Hair 2020

1. Choose the right length: The length of the bob haircut can vary, but for fine hair, it is best to keep it short to medium length. This helps to add volume and body to the hair.

2. Add layers: Layers are key when it comes to adding volume to fine hair. Ask your hairstylist to add layers throughout the hair to create the illusion of thicker locks.

3. Embrace texture: Embracing texture can help to add volume and movement to your hair. Use styling products such as texturizing sprays or volumizing mousses to enhance the texture of your hair.

4. Regular trims: Regular trims are important to maintain the shape and layers of your bob haircut. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and voluminous.
