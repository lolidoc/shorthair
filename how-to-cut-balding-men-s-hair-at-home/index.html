How to Cut Balding Men's Hair at Home: Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by gathering the necessary tools: a set of quality clippers, a handheld mirror, a comb, and a pair of scissors.

2. Before you begin cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry.

3. Begin by using the clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter one for a blended look.

4. Use a comb to lift the hair on top of your head and trim any excess length with the scissors.

5. Take your time and make small adjustments as needed to achieve your desired look.

6. Once you are finished cutting, use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make any final touch-ups.

7. Clean up any loose hairs and style your hair as desired.
