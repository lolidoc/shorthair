How to Achieve Easy Hairstyles for Over 70

Achieving easy hairstyles for over 70 is simple with the right tools and techniques. Here are a few tips:

- Start with clean, dry hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

- Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or styling cream, to your hair to add volume and texture.

- Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down, using the brush to lift and add volume.

- If desired, use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or smooth out any frizz.

- Finish with a lightweight hairspray to set your style and add shine.
