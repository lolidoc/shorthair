How to Cut Toddler Girl Curly Hair: Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, damp hair: Wet the hair slightly with water or a leave-in conditioner to make it more manageable.

2. Section off the hair: Use hair clips to divide the hair into smaller sections. This will make it easier to work with and ensure you don't miss any spots.

3. Trim the ends: Take small sections of the hair and trim the ends using a pair of sharp scissors. Use a wide-toothed comb to comb through the hair and hold it in place while cutting. This will help prevent any uneven cuts and ensure a more even finish.

4. Repeat the process: Continue trimming the ends of the hair, working your way through each section. Take your time and be patient, as toddler girls may not always sit still during a haircut.

5. Check for any unevenness: Once you have finished cutting the hair, comb through it again to check for any uneven or longer strands. Make any necessary adjustments to achieve a more even and precise cut.
