Are you a fan of the iconic hairstyles from the 60s? Do you want to recreate the vintage look and style your hair like they did back then? Well, you're in luck! In this blog post, we will guide you on how to achieve that classic 60s hairstyle that will make you feel like you've stepped back in time.

The 60s was a decade known for its bold and glamorous hairstyles. From the beehive to the bouffant, there were plenty of popular styles that defined the era. However, recreating these looks can be a challenge, especially if you're not familiar with the techniques and tools used during that time.

To style your hair like they did in the 60s, you'll need to gather a few essential tools and products. A teasing comb, bobby pins, hairspray, and a curling iron will be your best friends in achieving that vintage look. These tools will help you create volume, height, and the signature curls that were characteristic of 60s hairstyles.

In summary, to achieve that 60s look, you'll need to tease your hair at the crown to create height and volume. You can then style your hair into various updos or leave it down with curls. Don't forget to finish off the look with a generous amount of hairspray to ensure that your hairstyle stays in place all day long.

How to Style Your Hair Like the 60s: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by thoroughly brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair at the crown of your head and use the teasing comb to backcomb or tease it. This will create the volume and height that is characteristic of 60s hairstyles.

Step 3: Once you have teased the hair at the crown, gently smooth the top layer of hair to hide the teased section.

Step 4: Depending on the style you want to achieve, you can either leave your hair down or create an updo. For a down hairstyle, you can use a curling iron to create loose curls or waves. For an updo, you can gather your hair into a high ponytail or create a beehive or bouffant.

Step 5: Secure your hairstyle with bobby pins and use hairspray to set everything in place.

Additional Tips for Styling Your Hair Like the 60s

- To add extra volume to your hair, you can use a hairpiece or extensions. This will give your hairstyle that extra oomph and make it look more authentic.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and variations of 60s hairstyles. The 60s was a time of creativity and individuality, so feel free to put your own spin on the classic looks.

- If you're not confident in styling your own hair, consider visiting a professional hairstylist who specializes in vintage hairstyles. They will be able to recreate the 60s look for you and give you expert advice on how to maintain it.

- Lastly, have fun with your hairstyle! The 60s was all about expressing yourself through fashion and hair, so embrace the opportunity to channel your inner retro diva.

Conclusion of How to Style Your Hair Like the 60s

Styling your hair like they did in the 60s may seem daunting at first, but with the right tools, products, and techniques, you can achieve that iconic vintage look. Remember to tease your hair at the crown for added height and volume, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and variations. Whether you're going for a casual down hairstyle or an elegant updo, the key is to have fun and embrace the retro vibe. So go ahead, grab your teasing comb and hairspray, and get ready to rock that 60s hairstyle!

Question and Answer

Q: Can I achieve a 60s hairstyle without using heat tools?

A: Yes, you can achieve a 60s hairstyle without using heat tools. Instead of curling irons, you can try using foam rollers or overnight braids to create curls or waves in your hair.

Q: How long does it take to style your hair like they did in the 60s?

A: The time it takes to style your hair like they did in the 60s will vary depending on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the complexity of the hairstyle. On average, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour to achieve the desired look.

Q: Can I use modern hair products to achieve a 60s hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can use modern hair products to achieve a 60s hairstyle. However, it's important to choose products that will provide the hold and volume needed for the vintage look. Look for products that offer strong hold and are specifically designed for creating volume and texture.

Q: How do I maintain a 60s hairstyle throughout the day?

A: To maintain a 60s hairstyle throughout the day, it's important to use a generous amount of hairspray to set everything in place. You can also carry bobby pins and a travel-sized hairspray with you for touch-ups throughout the day.
