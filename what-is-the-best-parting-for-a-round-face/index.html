Best Parting for a Round Face: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a deep side part work for all hair lengths?

Q: How do I create a zigzag part?

A: To create a zigzag part, use a comb to section your hair into small triangular sections. Alternate the direction of each section, creating a zigzag pattern. Use hairspray or styling products to hold the parting in place.
