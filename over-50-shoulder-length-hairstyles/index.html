Recommendations for Over 50 Shoulder Length Hairstyles

If you are over 50 and considering a shoulder length hairstyle, here are some recommendations to keep in mind:

  1. Consider your face shape: Different hairstyles suit different face shapes, so it is important to choose a style that flatters your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a layered bob or a shaggy cut can help to elongate your face.
  2. Think about your lifestyle: Consider how much time and effort you are willing to put into styling your hair. If you prefer low-maintenance hairstyles, opt for a cut that can be air-dried or styled with minimal heat.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist: A professional hairstylist can help you choose a shoulder length hairstyle that suits your hair type, face shape, and personal style. They can also provide tips and advice on how to maintain and style your new haircut.
  4. Experiment with different styles: Once you have chosen a shoulder length hairstyle, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques. Try adding waves or curls for a more romantic look, or straighten your hair for a sleek and polished style.

Over 50 Shoulder Length Hairstyles: Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect over 50 shoulder length hairstyle:

  • Use volumizing products: To add volume and thickness to your hair, use volumizing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products. These can help to plump up your hair and make it appear fuller.
  • Invest in good quality styling tools: A high-quality hair dryer, straightener, and curling iron can make a world of difference when it comes to styling your shoulder length hair. Look for tools that have adjustable heat settings and ceramic or tourmaline plates for less damage to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment: Shoulder length hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques to find the look that suits you best.
  • Take care of your hair: Regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and protecting your hair from heat and sun damage are essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful shoulder length hair.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear shoulder length hairstyles if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, shoulder length hairstyles can actually make thinning hair appear fuller and more voluminous. Layered cuts and texturizing techniques can add volume and movement to your hair, making it look thicker.

Q: How often do I need to get my shoulder length hair trimmed?

A: It is recommended to get your shoulder length hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Q: Are shoulder length hairstyles suitable for curly hair?

A: Absolutely! Shoulder length hairstyles can be worn by women with curly hair. In fact, the natural texture of curly hair can add an extra dimension and movement to shoulder length hairstyles.

Q: Can I still wear updos with shoulder length hair?

A: Yes, there are plenty of updo options for shoulder length hair. You can try a half-up, half-down style, a messy bun, or a braided updo.
