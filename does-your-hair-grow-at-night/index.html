Hair Growth Basics:

Hair growth is a complex process that occurs in cycles. Each hair follicle goes through a growth phase, a rest phase, and a shedding phase. During the growth phase, new hair cells are produced, and the hair shaft extends. This phase can last anywhere from two to seven years, depending on various factors. After the growth phase, the hair enters a rest phase, where it remains dormant for a few months before shedding and being replaced by a new hair follicle.
