Fun Facts about Hair Color and Personality

Did you know that natural hair color is determined by the amount and type of melanin in the hair follicles? Melanin is a pigment that gives color to our hair, skin, and eyes. The more melanin you have, the darker your hair color will be. Additionally, hair color can change over time due to factors such as aging, hormonal changes, and exposure to the sun. So don't be surprised if your hair color evolves as you get older!
