Tips for Using the Best Chi Flat Iron

Using the best chi flat iron is easy, but there are a few tips and tricks that can help you achieve the best results.

Firstly, always start with clean, dry hair. Applying heat to wet or damp hair can cause damage and breakage. Make sure to thoroughly dry your hair before using the flat iron.

Secondly, use heat protectant spray before styling. This will create a barrier between your hair and the heat of the flat iron, minimizing damage and keeping your hair healthy.

Thirdly, section your hair before styling. This will ensure that every strand of hair is evenly styled and reduces the risk of missing any sections.

Lastly, be mindful of the temperature setting. Fine or damaged hair should be styled at a lower heat setting, while thick or coarse hair can handle higher temperatures. Start with a lower temperature and gradually increase if needed.
