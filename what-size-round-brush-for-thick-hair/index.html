What Size Round Brush for Thick Hair: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use a smaller round brush for thick hair?

Q: Can I use a round brush with synthetic bristles for thick hair?

A: While synthetic bristles can work for some individuals with thick hair, natural bristles are generally recommended. Natural bristles help to reduce frizz and create a smoother finish, making them a better choice for thick hair.

Q: How often should I clean my round brush?

A: It is recommended to clean your round brush at least once a month to remove any product buildup and hair debris. Simply remove the hair from the brush and soak it in warm water with a mild shampoo or brush cleaner. Gently scrub the bristles and rinse thoroughly before allowing it to air dry.

Q: Can I use a round brush on wet hair?

A: It is generally recommended to use a round brush on dry or slightly damp hair. Wet hair is more prone to damage and breakage, so it's best to allow your hair to air dry or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting before using a round brush.
