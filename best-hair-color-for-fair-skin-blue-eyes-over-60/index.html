Question and Answer

Q: Can I dye my hair at home? A: While it is possible to dye your hair at home, it is recommended to consult with a professional stylist for the best results, especially if you're looking for a significant color change.

Q: Will dyeing my hair damage it? A: Dyeing your hair can cause some damage, especially if done repeatedly or with harsh chemicals. It's important to take care of your hair by using quality hair products and avoiding excessive heat styling.

Q: Can I go from dark hair to a lighter shade? A: Going from dark hair to a lighter shade can be a more complex process and may require bleaching. It's best to consult with a professional stylist to ensure the process is done correctly and without causing excessive damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots? A: The frequency of root touch-ups will depend on how fast your hair grows. It's generally recommended to schedule touch-ups every 4-6 weeks to maintain a seamless hair color.
