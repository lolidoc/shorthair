Short Hairstyles for Relaxed African American Hair: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I still wear extensions with a short hairstyle?

A: Yes, extensions can be worn with short hairstyles. There are various types of extensions available, including clip-ins, sew-ins, and tape-ins, that can be easily incorporated into your short hairstyle.

Q: Will cutting my hair short make it grow faster?

A: No, cutting your hair does not make it grow faster. Hair growth is determined by factors such as genetics, diet, and overall health. However, cutting your hair can eliminate split ends and breakage, which can make your hair appear healthier and longer.

Q: How often should I trim my short hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your short hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. Regular trims will also promote healthy hair growth.

Q: Can I still straighten my short hair?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your short hair. However, it is important to use heat protectant products and avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.
