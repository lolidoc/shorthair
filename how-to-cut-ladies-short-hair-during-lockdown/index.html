First, gather all the necessary tools for cutting your hair, including sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Start by washing and drying your hair, then comb through it to remove any tangles. Divide your hair into sections using the clips, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

Next, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the comb as a guide to determine the length you want to cut. Slowly and carefully trim the hair, making small cuts at a time to ensure precision. Repeat this process for each section of hair, always combing through and using previous cuts as a guide for the next.
