Are you tired of the same old hairstyle but unsure of how to switch it up with short hair? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share a step-by-step guide on how to do easy space buns with short hair. Whether you have a bob or a pixie cut, this hairstyle is a fun and trendy way to change up your look.

Short hair can sometimes limit your options when it comes to hairstyles, but space buns are a versatile and stylish choice that works for any hair length. They can be worn for casual outings, special events, or even just as a cute everyday look. Plus, they're super easy to do!

To achieve the perfect space buns with short hair, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, you may want to straighten it using a flat iron for a sleeker look. You can also add some texturizing spray or dry shampoo to give your hair more grip and volume.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Take a comb and create a middle part, dividing your hair into two equal sections. Use hair ties or clips to secure each section, keeping them separate from each other.

Step 3: Create the Buns

Take one section of hair and twist it around itself, creating a small bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Repeat this step with the other section of hair, making sure both buns are symmetrical and secure.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

Once your buns are in place, use a mirror to check if they are even and adjust as needed. You can also pull out some strands of hair near your face for a softer, more effortless look. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray to hold everything in place.

And there you have it – easy space buns with short hair! This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want to add some flair to your look without putting in too much effort. Plus, it's a great way to keep your hair out of your face and off your neck during the warmer months.

Conclusion

In conclusion, space buns are a fantastic hairstyle option for those with short hair. They are easy to do, versatile, and can be worn for any occasion. By following the steps outlined in this blog post, you'll be able to achieve the perfect space buns with your short hair in no time. So go ahead, give it a try, and rock those space buns with confidence!
