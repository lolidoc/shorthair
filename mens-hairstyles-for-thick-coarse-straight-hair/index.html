Mens Hairstyles for Thick Coarse Straight Hair: The Classic Pompadour

The classic pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is characterized by short sides and a long, voluminous top. This hairstyle works well for men with thick, coarse, straight hair as it allows you to showcase your hair's natural texture and volume.

To achieve the classic pompadour, start by applying a volumizing mousse or styling cream to damp hair. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots. Once your hair is dry, use a comb to create a deep side part and comb the longer section of hair back. Finish off with a strong-hold pomade or wax to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Mens Hairstyles for Thick Coarse Straight Hair: The Undercut

The undercut is another popular hairstyle for men with thick, coarse, straight hair. It is characterized by short sides and a longer top that can be styled in various ways. This hairstyle is versatile and can be customized to suit your personal style.

To achieve the undercut, start by shaving the sides of your hair short. Leave the top section longer and style it as desired. You can choose to slick back your hair, create a messy textured look, or even add some volume and height at the front. Use a strong-hold pomade or wax to keep your hair in place and give it a polished finish.

Mens Hairstyles for Thick Coarse Straight Hair: The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that works well for men with thick, coarse, straight hair. It is characterized by a short, uniform length all over the head. This hairstyle is perfect for those who prefer a clean and minimalistic look.

To achieve the buzz cut, start by using a pair of clippers to cut your hair to the desired length. Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut. If you prefer a shorter length, use a lower guard setting on the clippers. If you prefer a slightly longer length, use a higher guard setting. Finish off by trimming any stray hairs and applying a light-hold gel or styling cream to add shine and control to your hair.
