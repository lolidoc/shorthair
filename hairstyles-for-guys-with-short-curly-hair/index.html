Hairstyles for Guys with Short Curly Hair

The Struggle of Styling Short Curly Hair

For guys with short curly hair, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. Curly hair has its own unique texture and characteristics that require special attention. But fear not, as we have compiled a list of stylish and trendy hairstyles that will make you stand out.

The Classic Taper Fade

The classic taper fade is a timeless hairstyle that works well for guys with short curly hair. This style features short sides and back, gradually fading into longer hair on top. This creates a clean and polished look, while still embracing your natural curls.

The Messy Curls

If you prefer a more casual and carefree look, the messy curls hairstyle is perfect for you. This style requires minimal effort and maintenance, as it allows your curls to naturally fall into place. Simply apply a curl-enhancing product and let your curls do the talking.

The Undercut with Curly Fringe

The undercut with curly fringe is a modern and edgy hairstyle that adds a touch of sophistication to your look. This style features shaved sides and back, with longer curly hair on top. The curly fringe adds texture and movement, creating a stylish contrast.

The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that works well with short curly hair. This style involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the hair on top slightly longer. The textured finish adds dimension and depth to your curls.

The Mohawk Fade

If you want to make a bold statement, the mohawk fade is the perfect choice. This hairstyle features a shaved or faded side, with longer curly hair on top that is styled into a mohawk shape. This look is stylish, edgy, and sure to turn heads.

The Side Swept Curls

The side swept curls hairstyle is a classic and elegant option for guys with short curly hair. This style involves sweeping your curls to one side, creating a sleek and polished look. This hairstyle is perfect for formal occasions or when you want to dress up your look.

The Curly Pompadour

The curly pompadour is a stylish and sophisticated hairstyle that works well for guys with short curly hair. This style involves styling your curls upwards and slightly back, creating a voluminous and eye-catching look. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to make a statement.

The Afro Fade

If you embrace your natural curls and want to showcase them, the afro fade is the way to go. This hairstyle features a fade on the sides and back, with a full and voluminous afro on top. This look is bold, confident, and perfect for guys who want to stand out.

The Brushed Back Curls

If you want a polished and suave look, the brushed back curls hairstyle is a great choice. This style involves brushing your curls back away from the face, creating a sleek and sophisticated appearance. This hairstyle is perfect for both formal and casual settings.

The Curly Faux Hawk

The curly faux hawk is a trendy and fashionable hairstyle that adds a touch of attitude to your look. This style involves shaving or fading the sides, while leaving longer curly hair on top. The hair on top is styled upwards and towards the center, creating a faux hawk effect.

With these stylish and trendy hairstyles, guys with short curly hair can embrace their natural texture and make a statement. Whether you prefer a classic, casual, or edgy look, there is a hairstyle that suits your personality and style. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and stylish.
