What is Face Shape?

Face shape refers to the unique structure and proportions of your face. There are several common face shapes, including round, square, oval, and diamond. Each face shape has its own characteristics and features that can be enhanced or balanced with the right hairstyle.

For example, if you have a round face shape, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds angles and lengthens your face. On the other hand, if you have a square face shape, you may want to opt for a hairstyle that softens your features and adds some roundness.

Q: How do I determine my face shape?

A: To determine your face shape, you can measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, compare the measurements to identify whether you have a round, square, oval, or diamond-shaped face.

Q: Can I change my face shape with a different hairstyle?

A: While a hairstyle can create the illusion of a different face shape, it cannot permanently change your face shape. However, choosing a hairstyle that suits your face shape can enhance your features and create a more balanced and flattering appearance.
