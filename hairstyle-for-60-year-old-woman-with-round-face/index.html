Hairstyle for 60 Year Old Woman with Round Face

Tips and Ideas for Flattering Hairstyles

As we age, finding the perfect hairstyle becomes more important than ever. If you have a round face and you're in your 60s, there are plenty of options to choose from that can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident. Here are some tips and ideas to consider:

1. Embrace Shorter Lengths

Shorter hairstyles tend to work well with round faces as they can create the illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer. Consider going for a pixie cut, a bob, or a layered haircut that falls just below your chin. These styles can be low-maintenance and youthful.

2. Add Volume

If you have thinning hair, adding volume can make a significant difference. Opt for layered hairstyles that add texture and movement. You can also try using volumizing products or getting a perm to give your hair more body.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to elongate your face and draw attention away from the roundness. They can be styled to blend seamlessly with your haircut or left longer to create a face-framing effect.

4. Consider Highlights

Adding highlights to your hair can create depth and dimension, making your face appear more angular. Opt for subtle highlights that complement your natural hair color and skin tone.

5. Avoid Excessive Volume at the Sides

While adding volume can be beneficial, be cautious with excessive volume at the sides of your head. This can make your face appear wider. Opt for hairstyles that focus on creating height rather than width.

6. Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut can be a great option for women with round faces. The layers help to add texture and movement, while the shorter length creates the illusion of a longer face shape. It's a chic and modern hairstyle that can take years off your appearance.

7. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is another flattering choice for round faces. It falls just below the chin, elongating the face and creating a more balanced look. Opt for a layered bob that adds volume and movement to your hair.

8. Soft Waves

Soft waves can help to soften the roundness of your face and add a touch of elegance to your hairstyle. You can achieve this look with a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight and then undoing the braids in the morning for natural-looking waves.

9. Classic Updo

For special occasions, a classic updo can be a sophisticated choice. Pulling your hair away from your face can create the illusion of a longer neck and draw attention to your features. Consider adding some loose tendrils or face-framing layers to soften the look.

10. Consult with a Professional

When in doubt, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can assess your face shape, hair texture, and personal style to recommend the best hairstyle for you. Don't be afraid to bring in pictures or discuss your concerns and preferences.
