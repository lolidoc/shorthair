The Best Short Haircuts for Thin Hair 2019

When it comes to choosing the best short haircuts for thin hair in 2019, it's important to consider your personal style and face shape. Here are some popular options to consider:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless and versatile haircut that works well for thin hair. It is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on the top. This haircut adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great option for thin hair. It is a classic haircut that is both stylish and low-maintenance. The bob cut can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, and it adds volume and movement to your hair.

3. Shag Cut

The shag cut is a trendy and edgy haircut that is perfect for thin hair. It features choppy layers and textured ends, creating a messy and effortless look. The shag cut adds volume and movement to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

4. Layered Cut

The layered cut is a versatile and flattering haircut for thin hair. It involves cutting different lengths of hair to create layers and texture. The layered cut adds volume and dimension to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

Question and Answer

Q: Will a short haircut make my thin hair look even thinner?

A: No, a well-executed short haircut can actually make thin hair look thicker and fuller by adding volume and texture.

Q: How do I style my short thin hair to make it look fuller?

A: Use volumizing products, such as mousse or dry shampoo, and try styling techniques like backcombing or using hot rollers to add volume to your hair.

Q: Can I still wear extensions with a short haircut for thin hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear extensions with a short haircut for thin hair. However, it is important to choose extensions that are lightweight and blend seamlessly with your natural hair.

Q: What is the best way to maintain a short haircut for thin hair?

A: Regularly trim your hair to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. Use quality hair products and avoid excessive heat styling to keep your hair healthy and vibrant.
