What Are Short Pixie Haircuts for Thick Hair?

The History and Myth of Short Pixie Haircuts for Thick Hair

Short pixie haircuts have been popular for decades, with icons like Audrey Hepburn and Mia Farrow rocking the style in the 1950s and 1960s. However, the history of pixie haircuts can be traced back even further.

The term "pixie" refers to a mythical creature from folklore, often depicted as a mischievous and playful being. The pixie haircut was named after these creatures due to its short and spiky appearance, which is reminiscent of the pixie's hairstyle.

Over the years, the pixie haircut has evolved and taken on different variations, but it has always remained a symbol of confidence, independence, and individuality. Today, the pixie haircut is a popular choice for those with thick hair who want a stylish and low-maintenance look.
