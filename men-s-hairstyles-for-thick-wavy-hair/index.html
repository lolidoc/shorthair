Men s Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair

Introduction

If you have thick wavy hair, you're in luck! This hair type offers a lot of versatility when it comes to styling. Whether you prefer a short and neat look or want to embrace your natural waves, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we will explore some trendy and stylish hairstyles for men with thick wavy hair in 2023.

1. The Classic Pompadour

The classic pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that works well for men with thick wavy hair. This style features longer hair on top that is swept back and styled with volume. To achieve this look, you'll need to apply some pomade or styling product to your hair and use a comb to create height and shape. This hairstyle adds a touch of sophistication and is perfect for both formal and casual occasions.

2. The Messy Crop

If you prefer a more casual and effortless look, the messy crop is a great choice. This style involves cutting the hair short on the sides and leaving some length on top. To style, simply apply some texturizing product to your hair and use your fingers to tousle and create a messy texture. This hairstyle is low-maintenance and perfect for guys who want a laid-back vibe.

3. The Undercut

The undercut is a popular hairstyle that works well for thick wavy hair. This style features short hair on the sides and back, while the top is left longer and styled with volume. You can choose to slick the top back or create a messy look with some texture. The undercut adds a modern and edgy touch to your overall look.

4. The Long Waves

If you're willing to embrace your natural waves, growing your hair out can lead to a stunning and unique look. Let your hair grow long and use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to create loose and flowing waves. You can also add some styling product to enhance the texture and hold. Long waves are effortlessly cool and perfect for guys who want a more bohemian style.

5. The Slicked Back

The slicked-back hairstyle is a classic choice that never goes out of style. This look involves combing the hair back and using a styling product to create a sleek and polished finish. For thick wavy hair, you may need to use a stronger hold product to keep your waves in place. The slicked-back style exudes confidence and works well for both formal and casual occasions.

6. The Faux Hawk

If you want to make a bold statement, the faux hawk is a stylish and edgy option. This hairstyle features shorter hair on the sides and back, while the center is styled with height and texture. To achieve this look, apply some styling product and use your fingers or a comb to create a spiky effect. The faux hawk is a great choice for guys who want a daring and adventurous look.

7. The Taper Fade

The taper fade is a popular haircut that can complement thick wavy hair. This style involves gradually fading the hair from short to longer as you move up the sides and back. The top can be styled in various ways, such as a pompadour or messy texture. The taper fade adds a clean and polished touch to your overall look.

8. The Textured Quiff

The textured quiff is a modern and stylish hairstyle that works well for thick wavy hair. This look features longer hair on top that is styled with volume and texture. To achieve this style, apply some texturizing product to your hair and use a comb or your fingers to create height and shape. The textured quiff is a versatile option that can be dressed up or down.

9. The Side Swept

If you prefer a more relaxed and casual look, the side-swept hairstyle is a great choice. This style involves brushing the hair to one side and letting it naturally fall into place. You can add some styling product to enhance the texture and hold. The side-swept look is effortless and works well for both formal and casual occasions.

10. The Buzz Cut

If you're looking for a low-maintenance option, the buzz cut is a simple yet stylish choice. This haircut involves shaving the hair very short all over. It's a great way to embrace your natural texture and keep your hair manageable. The buzz cut is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that suits any face shape.

Conclusion

With thick wavy hair, you have a wide range of hairstyles to choose from. Whether you prefer a classic look or want to experiment with something more edgy, there's a style that suits your taste and personality. Consider your face shape, lifestyle, and maintenance preferences when selecting a hairstyle. Don't be afraid to try new things and have fun with your hair!
