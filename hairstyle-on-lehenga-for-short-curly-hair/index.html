Hairstyle on Lehenga for Short Curly Hair

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for short curly hair with a lehenga, there are several options to consider. One popular choice is to go for an updo, such as a bun or a twisted updo. These hairstyles not only keep the hair off your face but also add an elegant touch to your overall look.

To achieve an updo, start by gathering your hair at the nape of your neck and securing it with bobby pins. You can then twist or coil the hair to create a bun or a twisted updo. Finish off the look with some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Another option for short curly hair with a lehenga is to leave the hair down and add some accessories. You can opt for a side part and pin one side of your hair back with a decorative hairpin or a flower crown. This will add a touch of glamour to your look and keep your hair out of your face.
