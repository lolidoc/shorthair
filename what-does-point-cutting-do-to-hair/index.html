How to Point Cut Your Hair

If you're feeling adventurous and want to try point cutting your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair into small, diagonal sections.
  3. Hold the section of hair between your fingers, and point the scissors towards the ends of the hair.
  4. Make small, diagonal cuts into the ends of the hair, removing small sections at a time.
  5. Work your way through the hair, cutting each section at an angle.
  6. When you're finished, shake out your hair to see the texture and movement that you've created.
