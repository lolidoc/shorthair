Short Hairstyles for Round Chubby Faces and Thin Hair: The Perfect Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is both chic and low-maintenance. It is a versatile option that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. The pixie cut is especially flattering for round chubby faces and thin hair, as it creates the illusion of length and adds volume to the hair.

When getting a pixie cut for round chubby faces and thin hair, it's important to consider the length and texture of your hair. Opt for a longer pixie cut that grazes the jawline, as this will help to elongate your face. Adding layers to the hair can also create the illusion of volume and movement.

To style your pixie cut, use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume to your hair. Use a small round brush to lift the roots and create volume at the crown. Finish off with a light-hold hairspray to keep your pixie cut in place.

Short Hairstyles for Round Chubby Faces and Thin Hair: The Trendy Bob

The bob is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair textures. It is a great option for round chubby faces and thin hair, as it adds length and volume to the hair.

When choosing a bob for round chubby faces and thin hair, opt for a longer length that grazes the shoulders. This will help to elongate your face and create a flattering look. Adding layers to the hair can also create the illusion of volume and movement.

To style your bob, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the roots. Finish off with a light-hold hairspray to keep your bob in place.

Short Hairstyles for Round Chubby Faces and Thin Hair: The Edgy Shag

The shag is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that is perfect for round chubby faces and thin hair. It is a versatile option that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. The shag adds texture and volume to the hair, creating a chic and effortless look.

When getting a shag for round chubby faces and thin hair, opt for a length that hits below the chin. This will help to elongate your face and create a flattering look. Adding layers to the hair can also create the illusion of volume and movement.

To style your shag, use a texturizing spray or mousse to add texture and volume to your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, effortless look. Finish off with a light-hold hairspray to keep your shag in place.
