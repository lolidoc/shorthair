Recommendation of Semi Short Haircuts for Guys with Round Faces

If you're looking for a new haircut and have a round face, here are a few recommendations that you might want to consider:

The classic pompadour is a timeless haircut that works well for guys with round faces. This style features shorter sides and longer lengths on top, with the hair swept back and styled into a voluminous quiff. The height at the crown of the head adds some angularity to the face, while the shorter sides help to create a more balanced and proportionate appearance.

2. The Undercut

The undercut is another great option for guys with round faces. This haircut features very short or shaved sides and a longer length on top. The contrast between the shorter sides and longer top adds some angularity and definition to the face, while the longer length on top can be styled in various ways to suit your personal style and preference.

3. The Taper Fade

The taper fade is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your individual needs and preferences. This style features shorter sides that gradually taper into a longer length on top. The gradual fade adds some definition and dimension to the face, while the longer length on top can be styled in different ways to create the desired look.

Q: Can guys with round faces pull off longer haircuts?

A: Yes, guys with round faces can pull off longer haircuts. However, it's important to find a style that complements your face shape and doesn't make it appear even rounder. Long hairstyles with layers and texture can help to add some angularity and definition to a round face.

Q: Are there any specific hairstyles that should be avoided for guys with round faces?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules, there are some hairstyles that may not be as flattering for guys with round faces. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as wide fringes or hairstyles with too much volume on the sides.
