What is a Pixie Cut?

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut close to the head on the sides and back, while leaving slightly longer layers on top. It is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that can be customized to suit different face shapes and personal styles. The pixie cut is known for its ability to add volume and texture to fine hair, making it a popular choice among women with thin or flat hair.

History and Myth of Pixie Cuts

The pixie cut gained popularity in the 1950s when Audrey Hepburn sported the iconic hairstyle in the movie "Roman Holiday". Since then, the pixie cut has become a timeless and classic hairstyle that has been embraced by women all over the world. In mythology, pixies are small, mischievous creatures known for their playful and adventurous nature. The pixie cut is named after these mythical creatures, as it represents a bold and daring hairstyle that requires confidence and a sense of adventure.

The Hidden Secret of Pixie Cuts

The hidden secret of pixie cuts lies in its ability to add volume and texture to fine hair. By cutting the hair short and adding layers, the pixie cut creates the illusion of thickness and fullness. The short length also prevents the hair from being weighed down, allowing it to bounce and move freely. Additionally, the pixie cut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling, making it a convenient option for women with busy lifestyles.

Recommendation of Best Pixie Cuts for Fine Hair 2020

If you're considering getting a pixie cut for your fine hair, here are some recommendations to consider:

1. The Classic Pixie

The classic pixie cut is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that suits all face shapes. It features short layers all around the head, with slightly longer layers on top. This cut adds volume and texture to fine hair, giving it a fuller and more voluminous look.

2. The Textured Pixie

The textured pixie cut is a modern twist on the classic pixie. It features choppy and textured layers that add movement and dimension to fine hair. This cut is perfect for those who want a more edgy and trendy look.

3. The Boyish Pixie

The boyish pixie cut is a short and sleek hairstyle that is reminiscent of a boy's haircut. It features very short layers all around the head, with minimal volume on top. This cut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance and minimalist look.

4. The Long Pixie

The long pixie cut is a slightly longer version of the classic pixie. It features longer layers on top that can be styled in various ways, such as swept to the side or spiked up. This cut is perfect for those who want a bit more versatility in styling their hair.

Tips for Maintaining Pixie Cuts for Fine Hair 2020

1. Use volumizing products: To add more volume and texture to your pixie cut, use volumizing products such as mousse or texturizing spray. Apply them to damp hair and blow-dry with a round brush to create lift at the roots.

2. Embrace texture: Don't be afraid to embrace the natural texture of your hair. Fine hair can often have a naturally smooth and sleek texture, which can be enhanced with the right products and styling techniques.

3. Regular trims: To keep your pixie cut looking fresh and polished, schedule regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.

4. Experiment with styling: One of the best things about a pixie cut is its versatility. Play around with different styling techniques, such as tousling with your fingers, using a curling iron for loose waves, or slicking it back for a sleek look.

Conclusion of Best Pixie Cuts for Fine Hair 2020

Question and Answer

Q: Will a pixie cut work for all face shapes?

A: While a pixie cut can be customized to suit different face shapes, it may not be the best choice for everyone. It is important to consult with a hairstylist who can assess your face shape and recommend the best pixie cut for you.

Q: Can I style my pixie cut in different ways?

A: Absolutely! Despite its short length, a pixie cut offers a lot of versatility in styling. You can play around with different techniques, such as tousling with your fingers, using a curling iron for loose waves, or slicking it back for a sleek look.

Q: How often should I get my pixie cut trimmed?

A: To maintain the shape and freshness of your pixie cut, it is recommended to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will prevent split ends and keep your cut looking polished.

Q: Can I add color to my pixie cut?

A: Absolutely! Adding color to your pixie cut can enhance its overall look and add a touch of personality. Consult with a professional hairstylist to find the best color options for your hair and skin tone.
