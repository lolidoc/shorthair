Low Maintenance Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50: How to Style and Maintain

To style and maintain your low maintenance short hairstyle for round faces over 50, follow these simple tips:

1. Start with clean and dry hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add lift and texture to your hair.

2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a texturizing spray or mousse, to add volume and hold to your hair.

3. Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair. Focus on lifting the roots and creating movement.

4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to set your style and add shine.
