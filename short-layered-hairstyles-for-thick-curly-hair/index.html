Short Layered Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair: Tips

Here are some tips to help you rock your short layered hairstyle for thick curly hair:

1. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curls gently. Avoid using a brush, as it can cause breakage and frizz.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to hydrate and define your curls. This will help to reduce frizz and add shine.

3. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can disrupt the curl pattern and cause frizz. Instead, let your curls air dry or use a diffuser on a low heat setting.

4. Refresh your curls between washes by spritzing them with water and applying a curl refreshing spray or mousse. This will help to revive your curls and reduce frizz.
