Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle this year? Well, you're in luck because there are plenty of exciting hair trends to try in 2020. From bold colors to edgy cuts, there's something for everyone. In this blog post, we'll explore the latest hair trends for 2020 and how you can incorporate them into your own style.

When it comes to hair trends, many people are looking for ways to stand out and express their individuality. Whether you want to make a statement with a vibrant hair color or try out a unique haircut, there are plenty of options to choose from. However, with so many trends to keep up with, it can be overwhelming to know where to start.

The latest hair trends for 2020 include everything from natural textures to bold colors. One of the most popular trends right now is the "money piece" highlight, which involves adding a bright pop of color around the face to frame it and create a focal point. Another trend that's been gaining traction is the shag haircut, a layered and textured style that adds volume and movement to any hair type.

In addition to the money piece and shag haircut, other popular trends for 2020 include the bob haircut, the curtain bangs, and the mullet. The bob haircut is a classic style that never goes out of fashion, and it can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. Curtain bangs, on the other hand, are a softer and more versatile alternative to traditional bangs, and they can be styled in a variety of ways. Lastly, the mullet is making a comeback with a modern twist, featuring a shorter length in the front and longer layers in the back.

The Money Piece Highlight

One of the hottest hair trends for 2020 is the money piece highlight. This trend involves adding a bold, contrasting color around the face to create a frame and draw attention to the features. Whether you choose a vibrant blue or a soft pastel pink, the money piece highlight is a great way to add some personality to your look.

When it comes to the money piece highlight, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, make sure you choose a color that complements your skin tone and eye color. This will help you achieve a more harmonious and flattering look. Second, consider the placement of the highlight. While many people choose to frame their entire face, you can also opt for a more subtle look by adding just a few strands of color.

Overall, the money piece highlight is a fun and versatile trend that allows you to experiment with different colors and styles. Whether you're looking to make a bold statement or simply enhance your natural beauty, this trend is definitely worth trying.

The Shag Haircut

Another popular hair trend for 2020 is the shag haircut. This style is all about adding layers and texture to create a messy, undone look. The shag haircut is perfect for those who want to add volume and movement to their hair.

When it comes to the shag haircut, there are a few different variations to choose from. Some people prefer a shorter shag with layers that start at the chin, while others opt for a longer shag with layers that start at the shoulder. The key is to find a length and layering technique that works best for your hair type and face shape.

In addition to the length and layering, the shag haircut can also be customized with bangs. Whether you choose to go for a full fringe or wispy bangs, adding bangs to your shag haircut can help to frame your face and create a more polished look.

The Bob Haircut

The bob haircut is a timeless and versatile style that never goes out of fashion. Whether you prefer a classic bob or a more modern variation, this haircut is a great option for those looking to switch up their look in 2020.

When it comes to the bob haircut, there are a few different lengths and styles to choose from. Some people prefer a chin-length bob, while others opt for a longer bob that falls below the shoulders. The key is to find a length and style that suits your face shape and personal style.

In addition to the length, the bob haircut can also be customized with layers and bangs. Adding layers to your bob can help to add volume and movement, while adding bangs can help to frame your face and create a more youthful look.

The Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs are a softer and more versatile alternative to traditional bangs. This style involves parting the hair in the middle and allowing the bangs to frame the face on either side, creating a curtain-like effect.

When it comes to curtain bangs, there are a few different ways to style them. Some people prefer to wear them straight and sleek, while others opt for a more tousled and textured look. The key is to find a style that suits your face shape and personal style.

Curtain bangs are a great option for those who want to change up their look without committing to a full fringe. They can be easily grown out and styled in a variety of ways, making them a versatile and low-maintenance option.

The Mullet

The mullet is making a comeback in 2020 with a modern twist. This edgy and unconventional haircut features a shorter length in the front and longer layers in the back, creating a unique and eye-catching look.

When it comes to the mullet, there are a few different variations to choose from. Some people prefer a more subtle and gradual transition between the front and back layers, while others opt for a more dramatic contrast. The key is to find a style that suits your personal style and comfort level.

The mullet is a bold and daring choice that is sure to turn heads. Whether you choose a sleek and polished look or a more tousled and textured style, this haircut is a great way to express your individuality and stand out from the crowd.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the latest hair trends for 2020 offer a wide range of options for those looking to switch up their look. Whether you're interested in trying a bold color or experimenting with a new haircut, there's something for everyone. From the money piece highlight to the shag haircut, the bob haircut to the curtain bangs, and the mullet to the natural textures, these trends are sure to inspire and excite. So go ahead, embrace your individuality and try out one of these trends for yourself!
