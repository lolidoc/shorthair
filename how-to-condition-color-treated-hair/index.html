Common Questions About Conditioning Color Treated Hair

Q: Can I use any conditioner on color treated hair? A: It is best to use a conditioner that is specifically formulated for color treated hair. These conditioners are designed to be gentle and nourishing without stripping the color.

Q: How often should I deep condition my color treated hair? A: It is recommended to deep condition color treated hair once a week to provide it with extra hydration and nourishment.

Q: Can I use hot water to rinse out the conditioner? A: It is best to rinse out the conditioner with cool water, as hot water can strip the color and cause damage.

Q: Should I avoid heat styling tools altogether? A: While it is best to limit the use of heat styling tools, if you do use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage and fading.
