African American Short Natural Hairstyles For Round Faces

When it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face African American women with short natural hair have plenty of options to choose from Short hairstyles can be versatile stylish and flattering for those with round faces In this article we will explore some popular and trendy short natural hairstyles that are perfect for round faces in 2023

Table of Contents

Introduction

When it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face, African American women with short natural hair have plenty of options to choose from. Short hairstyles can be versatile, stylish, and flattering for those with round faces. In this article, we will explore some popular and trendy short natural hairstyles that are perfect for round faces in 2023.

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a timeless and classic short hairstyle that can work wonders for round faces. This short and sleek haircut helps to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. You can experiment with different variations of pixie cuts, such as adding layers or bangs, to suit your personal style.

2. Tapered Afro

A tapered afro is a popular choice among African American women with round faces. This hairstyle features shorter hair on the sides and back, gradually getting longer towards the top. The tapered afro adds height and volume to the crown, making the face appear longer and slimmer.

3. Short Curly Bob

A short curly bob is a chic and stylish option for those with round faces. This hairstyle adds volume and texture to the hair, creating a flattering frame for the face. You can enhance the natural curls by using a curling cream or mousse to define the curls and add bounce.

4. Mohawk

A mohawk is a bold and edgy hairstyle that can complement round faces. This hairstyle involves shaving or cutting the hair on the sides and leaving a strip of longer hair in the middle. The height and length of the mohawk can be customized to suit your preference.

5. Braided Updo

A braided updo is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for both formal and casual occasions. This hairstyle involves braiding the hair and creating an updo by twisting or pinning the braids. A braided updo can add height and elongate the face, making it a great choice for round faces.

6. Afro Puff

An afro puff is a simple yet stylish hairstyle that can be achieved with short natural hair. This hairstyle involves gathering the hair into a high ponytail or puff on the crown of the head. The afro puff adds height and elongates the face, making it a flattering choice for round faces.

7. Faux Hawk

A faux hawk is a trendy and fashionable hairstyle that can be achieved with short natural hair. This hairstyle involves creating a mohawk-like effect by styling the hair in the middle upwards, while keeping the sides shorter. The faux hawk adds height and length to the face, creating a flattering look for round faces.

8. Finger Coils

Finger coils are a popular choice for those with short natural hair. This hairstyle involves twisting small sections of hair around your finger to create defined coils. Finger coils can add texture and dimension to the hair, creating a flattering look for round faces.

9. Side Part Pixie

A side part pixie is a stylish and elegant hairstyle for round faces. This hairstyle involves creating a deep side part and styling the hair towards one side. The asymmetrical look helps to create an illusion of length and slim down the face.

10. Mohawk Braids

Mohawk braids are a trendy and eye-catching hairstyle for round faces. This hairstyle involves braiding the hair on the sides and leaving a strip of longer hair in the middle, similar to a traditional mohawk. Mohawk braids add height and length to the face, creating a flattering look.

Conclusion

These are just a few of the many short natural hairstyles that are perfect for African American women with round faces in 2023. Whether you prefer a sleek pixie cut or a bold mohawk, there is a hairstyle out there that will flatter your face shape and showcase your natural beauty. Experiment with different styles, textures, and accessories to find the perfect short natural hairstyle that suits your personal style and enhances your features.
