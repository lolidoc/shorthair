Tips for Short Hair Making You Look Older

If you have short hair and want to maintain a youthful appearance, here are some tips to keep in mind:

To keep your short hair looking fresh and well-groomed, make sure to schedule regular haircuts. This will prevent your hair from becoming overgrown and losing its shape.
