Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for a new way to style your short hair? Look no further! In this blog post, we will show you how to do low space buns with short hair. This trendy hairstyle is perfect for any occasion and can be done quickly and easily. Whether you have a special event coming up or just want to switch up your everyday look, low space buns are a great choice!

Many people with short hair struggle to find hairstyles that work for them. It can be frustrating when you see all these beautiful updos and buns but feel like you can't achieve them with your short hair. That's where low space buns come in! They are specifically designed for shorter hair lengths and can be done with minimal effort.

To create low space buns with short hair, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a small amount of styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 2: Divide your hair

Using a comb, create a part down the middle of your head. This will divide your hair into two sections.

Step 3: Tie your hair

Take one section of hair and tie it into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Repeat with the other section of hair.

Step 4: Twist and wrap

Take one ponytail and twist it into a tight coil. Wrap the twisted ponytail around its base to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins. Repeat with the other ponytail.

Step 5: Finishing touches

Adjust the buns to make them symmetrical and secure any loose strands with bobby pins. Spray your hair with hairspray to set the style in place.

And there you have it – low space buns with short hair! This hairstyle is versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. It is perfect for a casual day out or a formal event. Plus, it is a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still looking stylish.

Conclusion of how to do low space buns with short hair

Low space buns are a trendy and easy hairstyle for those with short hair. With just a few simple steps, you can achieve a stylish and versatile look. So, why not give it a try and see how low space buns can transform your short hair?
