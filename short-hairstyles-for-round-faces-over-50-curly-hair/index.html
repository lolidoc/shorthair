Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for women with round faces and curly hair. This style adds height to the top of your head, elongating your face, and showcases your curls beautifully. It is also easy to maintain and style on a daily basis. Simply apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair, scrunch it with your hands, and let it air dry for natural, effortless curls.

Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is another great option for women over 50 with round faces and curly hair. This hairstyle frames your face, accentuating your features, and adds volume and movement to your curls. To style, apply a curl-defining product to your damp hair, scrunch it with your hands, and use a diffuser to enhance your curls. Finish with a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

Curly Shag

The curly shag is a trendy and modern hairstyle that is perfect for women over 50 with round faces and curly hair. This style adds height and volume to the top of your head, elongating your face, and showcases your curls beautifully. To style, apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair, scrunch it with your hands, and let it air dry for natural, tousled curls.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I still wear short hairstyles if I have thinning hair?

A: Absolutely! There are many short hairstyles that are perfect for women with thinning hair. A pixie cut or a bob with layers can add volume and movement to your hair, making it appear fuller and thicker.

Q: Can I wear a short hairstyle if I have a round face but straight hair?

A: Of course! While some short hairstyles may be more flattering for women with round faces and curly hair, there are still plenty of options for women with straight hair. Consider a layered bob or a short pixie cut to enhance your features and create a stylish look.

Q: How often should I cut my hair if I have a short hairstyle?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your short haircut. Regular trims will also help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Q: Can I still style my short hair if I have limited mobility?

A: Absolutely! There are many styling techniques and tools available that can make styling short hair easier for individuals with limited mobility. Consider using a lightweight hairdryer, a small round brush, or a styling product that adds texture and hold to your hair.
