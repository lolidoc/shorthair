Popular Hairstyles for Black Teenage Girls

One popular hairstyle for black teenage girls is the box braids. Box braids are a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that can be styled in various ways. They involve sectioning the hair into small, square-shaped sections and braiding each section from the root to the tip. Box braids can be worn long or short, and can be accessorized with beads or other hair accessories.

Another popular hairstyle is the afro. The afro is a bold and iconic hairstyle that celebrates natural hair. It involves allowing the hair to grow out into a full, round shape. The afro can be styled and accessorized in various ways to create different looks.

Cornrows are also a popular choice among black teenage girls. Cornrows involve braiding the hair close to the scalp in a straight line. They can be styled in different patterns and can be combined with other hairstyles, such as box braids or twists.

Dreadlocks are another popular hairstyle that has been embraced by black teenage girls. Dreadlocks involve allowing the hair to naturally mat and form into coils or ropes. They can be styled in various lengths and can be accessorized with beads, charms, or other hair accessories.
