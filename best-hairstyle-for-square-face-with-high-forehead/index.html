Do you have a square face with a high forehead? Are you struggling to find the best hairstyle that flatters your features? Look no further! In this blog post, we will discuss the best hairstyle for square face with high forehead and provide you with some helpful tips and recommendations.

Having a square face with a high forehead can be a challenge when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. You may feel that certain styles make your forehead appear even larger or emphasize the squareness of your face. This can be frustrating and make it difficult to feel confident in your appearance.

The best hairstyle for a square face with a high forehead is one that softens the angles of your face and balances out the proportions. It should draw attention away from your forehead and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. This can be achieved through various haircut and styling techniques.

To achieve the best hairstyle for a square face with a high forehead, consider the following tips:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great option for square faces with high foreheads. The layers add movement and softness to your hair, while the bob length helps to balance out the proportions of your face. This style can be worn straight or curled for added versatility.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another excellent choice for square faces with high foreheads. The diagonal line created by the bangs helps to soften the angles of your face and draw attention away from your forehead. Opt for longer bangs that graze your eyebrows for a flattering look.

3. Wispy Layers

Wispy layers can also be a great option for square faces with high foreheads. These delicate layers add movement and texture to your hair, creating a softer and more feminine look. They can be incorporated into various hairstyles, including long, medium, or short cuts.

4. Side Part

A side part can help to minimize the appearance of a high forehead and create the illusion of a more balanced face shape. Experiment with different parts to find the one that flatters your features the most. Pairing a side part with any of the above hairstyles can result in a stunning look.

Conclusion of best hairstyle for square face with high forehead:

Finding the best hairstyle for a square face with a high forehead can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and styling, you can achieve a flattering and confident look. Consider trying a layered bob, side-swept bangs, wispy layers, or a side part to soften the angles of your face and draw attention away from your forehead. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist who can provide personalized advice based on your specific features and preferences.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I have a square face with a high forehead?

A: Absolutely! Updos can still be a great option for square faces with high foreheads. Consider incorporating some face-framing layers or soft tendrils to soften the look and create a more balanced appearance.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have a square face with a high forehead?

A: While personal style and preferences vary, some hairstyles may emphasize the squareness of your face or make your forehead appear even larger. Avoid blunt bangs, super short cuts, and hairstyles with a lot of volume on top.

Q: Can I experiment with different hair colors if I have a square face with a high forehead?

A: Absolutely! Hair color can be a fun way to express your personality and enhance your features. Consider consulting with a professional colorist to find the best shades and techniques that complement your skin tone and flatter your face shape.

Q: How often should I trim my hair if I have a square face with a high forehead?

A: Regular trims are important for maintaining the health and shape of your hair, regardless of your face shape. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hairstyle looking fresh and polished.
