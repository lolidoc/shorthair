Are you someone with a round face and considering getting a perm? If so, you may be wondering, "Do perms suit round faces?" This is a common question among individuals with round face shapes. In this blog post, we will explore the answer to this question and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.

Having a round face shape can pose certain challenges when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. The goal is to find a hairstyle that complements your face shape and enhances your features. Many people believe that perms can add volume and texture to the hair, which can help create the illusion of a more elongated face shape. However, there are a few factors to consider before deciding if a perm is the right choice for you.

The answer to whether perms suit round faces is not a simple yes or no. It ultimately depends on the individual and their specific features. While perms can add volume and texture to the hair, they can also make the face appear wider. If you have a round face shape and want to create the illusion of length, it may be best to avoid tight curls and opt for looser waves or beachy curls instead. It's important to consult with a professional hairstylist who can assess your face shape and hair texture to determine the best type of perm for you.

In summary, perms can be a suitable hairstyle option for individuals with round faces. However, it's important to consider factors such as the desired outcome, hair texture, and face shape before making a decision. Consulting with a professional hairstylist is recommended to ensure the best results.

Do perms suit round faces and how to choose the right one?

When considering a perm for a round face shape, it's important to choose the right type of perm that will complement your features. Here are a few factors to consider:

1. Face Shape:

As mentioned earlier, round faces can benefit from hairstyles that create the illusion of length. Opt for loose waves or beachy curls that add volume to the hair without making the face appear wider. Avoid tight curls that can emphasize the roundness of the face.

2. Hair Texture:

Consider your hair texture when choosing a perm. Different hair textures respond differently to perms, and it's important to choose a perm that will work well with your natural hair. Fine hair may benefit from a body wave perm, while coarse hair may require a tighter curl pattern.

3. Maintenance:

Perms require regular maintenance to keep the curls looking their best. Consider the time and effort you are willing to put into styling and maintaining your perm. If you prefer low-maintenance hairstyles, a perm may not be the best choice for you.

4. Consultation with a Professional:

Before getting a perm, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can assess your face shape, hair texture, and desired outcome to recommend the best type of perm for you. They can also provide guidance on aftercare and maintenance to ensure long-lasting results.

History and Myth of Perms for Round Faces

Perms have been a popular hairstyle choice for decades. They rose to popularity in the 1980s and have since evolved to include various curl patterns and styles. While there may be myths surrounding perms for round faces, such as them making the face appear wider, the truth is that the right type of perm can actually enhance the features of a round face shape.

Many celebrities with round faces have successfully rocked perms, proving that they can be a flattering hairstyle choice. It's important to remember that everyone's face shape and features are unique, and what works for one person may not work for another.

The Hidden Secret of Perms for Round Faces

The hidden secret of perms for round faces lies in choosing the right type of perm and styling techniques. By opting for loose waves or beachy curls, you can create the illusion of a more elongated face shape. Additionally, using volumizing products and styling techniques can add height to the hair, further enhancing the overall look.

Recommendation for Perms for Round Faces

Before making any decisions, consult with a professional hairstylist who specializes in perms. They can assess your face shape, hair texture, and desired outcome to recommend the best type of perm for you.

3. Use Volumizing Products:

Adding volume to the hair can help enhance the overall look and create the illusion of length. Use volumizing products and styling techniques to achieve this.

Tips for Perms for Round Faces

If you decide to get a perm for your round face shape, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a professional hairstylist who specializes in perms and has experience working with round face shapes.
  • Bring inspiration photos to your consultation to help communicate the desired outcome.
  • Follow the aftercare instructions provided by your hairstylist to ensure long-lasting results.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that suits you best.

Question and Answer

Q: Will a perm make my round face appear wider?

A: The right type of perm can actually enhance the features of a round face shape. Opt for loose waves or beachy curls to create the illusion of a more elongated face shape.

Q: Can I get a perm if I have fine hair?

A: Yes, individuals with fine hair can get perms. However, it's important to choose a perm that will work well with your hair texture. A body wave perm may be a suitable choice for fine hair.

Q: How long do perms typically last?

A: The longevity of a perm can vary depending on factors such as hair texture, aftercare, and maintenance. On average, perms can last anywhere from three to six months.

Q: Can I style my perm in different ways?

A: Yes, perms can be styled in various ways. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that suits you best.
