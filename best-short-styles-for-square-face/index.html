When it comes to square faces, the main pain points are finding hairstyles that soften the strong jawline and create balance. A square face shape is characterized by a wide forehead, angular jawline, and a square or rectangular shape. The right hairstyle can help to soften these features and bring out your best features.

If you are looking for the best short styles for square faces, consider hairstyles that add volume and softness to the hair. Best Short Styles for Square Face: Exploring the Options

When it comes to the best short styles for square faces, there are several options to choose from. One popular style is the layered pixie cut. This hairstyle features short layers throughout the hair, creating texture and movement. The layers soften the strong jawline and add volume to the crown, creating a flattering look for square faces.

Another great option is the asymmetrical bob. This hairstyle is characterized by one side of the hair being shorter than the other. The asymmetry creates a sense of movement and adds softness to the face. It also helps to balance out the strong jawline, making it a perfect choice for square faces.

For those who prefer a shorter length, a textured short crop is a fantastic option. This style involves cutting the hair short all over and adding texture through techniques such as razor cutting or point cutting. The texture adds softness and dimension to the hair, making it a great choice for square faces.

Tips for Best Short Styles for Square Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for your square face, here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Consider your hair texture: Certain short styles work better with specific hair textures. For example, a layered pixie cut works well with both straight and wavy hair, while a textured short crop is ideal for curly or textured hair.

2. Consult with a stylist: A professional stylist can help you choose a short style that flatters your square face shape and suits your individual needs. They can also provide advice on how to maintain and style your new haircut.

3. Play with different lengths: Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths when choosing a short hairstyle. Some square faces may look best with a shorter length, while others may benefit from a longer bob or lob.

4. Consider your lifestyle: When choosing a short style, consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend on styling. If you prefer a low-maintenance look, opt for a style that requires minimal styling and upkeep.

Fun Facts About Best Short Styles for Square Face

How to Achieve the Best Short Styles for Square Face

To achieve the best short styles for square faces, follow these steps:

1. Consult with a stylist: A professional stylist can help you choose a short style that flatters your square face shape and suits your individual needs. They can also provide advice on how to maintain and style your new haircut.

2. Prepare your hair: Before getting a short haircut, make sure your hair is in good condition. Trim any split ends and deep condition your hair to ensure it is healthy and strong.

3. Style your hair: Once you have your new short haircut, experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that suits you best. Use products such as mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to the hair.

4. Maintain your haircut: Regular trims are essential to maintain the shape and style of your short haircut. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your hair looking its best.

What If I'm Not Ready for a Short Style?

If you're not ready to commit to a short style, there are still options for you. Consider trying medium-length hairstyles that can help to soften the strong angles of a square face. Shoulder-length bobs, layered cuts, and side-swept bangs are all great choices that can flatter square faces without sacrificing length.
