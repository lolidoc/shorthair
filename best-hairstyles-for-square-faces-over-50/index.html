Recommendations for Best Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 50

Take into account your hair type when choosing a hairstyle. Different hair textures and thicknesses can affect how a style looks and holds up throughout the day. Consult with your hairstylist to find a style that works well with your hair type and is easy to maintain.

2. Embrace Layers

Layers are a great way to add movement and softness to the hair, especially for square faces over 50. They can create the illusion of volume and help to balance out the angular features of the face. 3. Opt for Softness

Softness is key when it comes to hairstyles for square faces over 50. Avoid harsh lines and angles that can further emphasize the squareness of the face. Instead, opt for styles that incorporate soft waves, curls, or texture to add femininity and create a more balanced appearance.

4. Experiment with Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add softness to a square face shape. Whether you choose side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, or wispy bangs, incorporating this element can make a big difference in how your hairstyle frames your face.
