Best Hairstyles For Coarse Curly Hair

Introduction

Having coarse curly hair can be a blessing and a challenge at the same time. While it adds volume and texture to your hair, it can also be difficult to manage and style. However, with the right hairstyles, you can enhance the beauty of your coarse curly hair and make a fashion statement. In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for coarse curly hair in 2023.

1. Long Layered Curls

If you have long coarse curly hair, consider getting layers to add movement and shape to your locks. This hairstyle works best for those with thick hair as it helps to reduce bulkiness. The layers can be cut to frame your face and highlight your natural curls. To style, use a curl-enhancing product and scrunch your hair to encourage the curls.

2. Curly Bob

A curly bob is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that suits coarse curly hair perfectly. This shorter haircut can be customized to your preference, whether you prefer a chin-length bob or a longer bob. The key is to maintain the natural texture of your curls while keeping the length manageable. Apply a curl-defining product to enhance the curls and create a polished look.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a great option for those with coarse curly hair who want to showcase their curls while keeping their hair out of their face. Gather the top section of your hair and secure it with a clip or hair tie, leaving the rest of your curls cascading down. This hairstyle is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

4. Braided Updo

An elegant braided updo is a fantastic choice for special occasions or when you want to switch up your look. Start by creating two Dutch braids on each side of your head, then gather the remaining hair and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and leave a few curly tendrils loose for a romantic touch. This hairstyle is not only beautiful but also practical, as it keeps your hair off your face.

5. High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a classic and easy hairstyle that works wonders for coarse curly hair. It instantly lifts your face and elongates your neck. To achieve this look, gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie. You can also wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to conceal it and add a stylish touch.

6. Messy Bun

If you're looking for a quick and effortless hairstyle for your coarse curly hair, a messy bun is the way to go. Gather your hair into a loose bun at the back of your head, leaving a few strands loose to frame your face. Use bobby pins to secure the bun and pull out a few curls for a relaxed and carefree look. This hairstyle is perfect for casual outings or when you're on the go.

7. Side Swept Curls

For a romantic and glamorous look, try styling your coarse curly hair into side-swept curls. Part your hair to the side and let your curls fall naturally. You can use a curling iron to enhance the curls and create a more polished look. This hairstyle is perfect for parties, weddings, or any special occasion where you want to make a statement.

8. Fishtail Braid

The fishtail braid is a trendy and chic hairstyle that looks amazing on coarse curly hair. Divide your hair into two sections and take a small strand from the outer side of one section. Cross it over to the other side, incorporating it into the opposite section. Repeat this process until you reach the end of your hair, then secure it with a hair tie. Gently pull on the strands to create a fuller and more voluminous braid.

9. Top Knot

A top knot is a stylish and versatile hairstyle that works well with coarse curly hair. Gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it, and wrap it around the base to create a knot. Secure the knot with bobby pins and leave a few curls loose for a relaxed look. This hairstyle is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

10. Afro

Embrace the natural beauty of your coarse curly hair by rocking an afro hairstyle. This bold and empowering look celebrates your natural curls and adds a unique flair to your style. To maintain your afro, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to keep your curls defined and moisturized.

In conclusion, coarse curly hair can be styled in various ways to enhance its beauty. Whether you prefer long layered curls, a curly bob, or an elegant updo, there are plenty of options to choose from. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for your hair type and personal style. Embrace your natural curls and rock your coarse hair with confidence!
