Best Mousse for Short Straight Hair: How to Use It

Using the best mousse for short straight hair is simple. Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair as usual. Towel dry your hair until it is damp, but not dripping wet. Shake the mousse can well and dispense a small amount of foam into your palm.
