Understanding the Concept of Protective Styles

Protective styles refer to hairstyles that minimize manipulation and protect the ends of your hair. These styles typically involve tucking your ends away and reducing the exposure of your hair to elements that can cause damage, such as heat, friction, and harsh weather conditions. Protective styles can include braids, twists, buns, weaves, wigs, and more.

The idea behind protective styles is to create an environment that allows your hair to grow without experiencing excessive breakage or damage. By reducing manipulation, protecting the ends, and retaining moisture, you can create optimal conditions for hair growth.
