Tips for Using Shampoo for Thick Wavy Hair

Using the right technique when shampooing your thick wavy hair can make a world of difference. Here are some tips to get the most out of your shampoo:

1. Wet your hair thoroughly before applying shampoo. 2. Use a quarter-sized amount of shampoo and lather it in your hands before applying it to your hair. 3. Massage the shampoo into your scalp using gentle circular motions. 4. Focus on cleansing your scalp rather than scrubbing your hair. 5. Rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all traces of shampoo. 6. Follow up with a conditioner to hydrate and nourish your hair. 7. Avoid towel-drying your hair vigorously – instead, gently squeeze out excess water. 8. Style your hair as desired, using products specifically formulated for thick wavy hair.
