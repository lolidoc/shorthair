Tips for Hair Styles for Round Faces 2020

When it comes to styling hair for round faces, there are a few tips to keep in mind. Instead, opt for hairstyles that create vertical lines and add height, such as layered cuts or hairstyles with volume at the crown. Secondly, consider incorporating side-swept bangs into your hairstyle, as they can help to create angles and balance out the width of the face. Lastly, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your round face.

Common Questions about Hair Styles for Round Faces 2020

Q: Can I wear long hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear long hair if you have a round face. However, it is important to choose a hairstyle that adds length and angles to your face. Consider incorporating face-framing layers and side-swept bangs to create a more balanced look.

Q: Are pixie cuts suitable for round faces?

A: Yes, pixie cuts can be incredibly flattering for round faces. The short length helps to create the illusion of length, while the edgy and asymmetrical nature of the cut adds angles to the face.

Q: Should I avoid blunt cuts if I have a round face?

A: Blunt cuts can make a round face appear wider. Instead, consider opting for layered cuts that create movement and dimension. Adding texture to the hair can also help to soften the face and create a more flattering look.

Q: How can I choose the right hairstyle for my round face?
