The 60s Hair Flip: A Vintage Trend Making a Comeback

The 60s hair flip is a timeless hairstyle that has made a comeback in recent years. This iconic look, popularized by fashion icons like Brigitte Bardot and Twiggy, adds a touch of retro glam to any outfit. Whether you're heading to a special event or simply want to add some vintage flair to your everyday style, mastering the art of the 60s hair flip is a must.

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that add volume and texture. Towel dry your hair, gently patting it to remove excess moisture. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots, focusing on the crown area. This will give your hair the lift it needs for the perfect hair flip.

Step 2: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, lifting at the roots to create volume. As you dry each section, roll the hair away from your face and hold it in place for a few seconds to create a slight bend. Continue this technique until all of your hair is dry.

Step 3: Section and Curl

Divide your hair into two sections, starting from the middle part. Take a 1-inch section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron, leaving the ends out for a more natural look. Hold the curling iron vertically and curl away from your face. Repeat this process for each section of hair, alternating the direction of the curls for a more textured finish.

Step 4: Brush and Tease

Gently brush through your curls with a wide-tooth comb to create soft waves. Then, take a teasing comb and backcomb the roots at the crown area to add height. This will give your hair the necessary lift for the perfect hair flip.

Step 5: Flip and Set

Now comes the fun part – the hair flip! Take a fine-tooth comb or a teasing brush and gently comb through the top layer of your hair to smooth out any tangles. Then, flip your hair to one side, allowing it to fall naturally into place. Use bobby pins to secure any loose sections or create extra volume if needed. Finish off with a light mist of hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the 60s Hair Flip

To keep your 60s hair flip looking fabulous all day long, follow these tips:

1. Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is your best friend when it comes to maintaining volume and texture. Spritz some dry shampoo at the roots and massage it in to refresh your hair and add extra lift.

2. Sleep with a Silk Pillowcase

Avoid waking up with flattened hair by sleeping on a silk pillowcase. Silk helps reduce friction and prevents hair breakage, allowing you to maintain your hair flip overnight.

3. Touch Up with a Curling Iron

If your hair starts to lose its curl throughout the day, touch up the ends with a curling iron. This will revive your waves and keep your hair flip looking fresh.

4. Use Hairspray Sparingly

While hairspray is essential for setting your style, using too much can weigh down your hair and make it look stiff. Opt for a lightweight hairspray and apply it sparingly to avoid a crunchy finish.

With these simple steps and maintenance tips, you can effortlessly rock the 60s hair flip in 2023. Embrace the retro vibe and turn heads with this iconic hairstyle that never goes out of style.
