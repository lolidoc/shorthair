How to Style Short Haircuts for Girls 2021

Styling short haircuts for girls in 2021 can be a fun and creative process. Here are some tips to help you style your daughter's short hair:

1. Start with clean and dry hair: Before styling, make sure your daughter's hair is clean and dry. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner suitable for her hair type, and gently towel-dry the hair before applying any styling products.

2. Apply styling products: Depending on the desired look, apply a small amount of styling product to your daughter's hair. For sleek and smooth styles, use a smoothing serum or cream. For textured or voluminous looks, use a texturizing spray or mousse.

3. Use heat tools sparingly: Short haircuts often require minimal heat styling. However, if you want to create curls or waves, use a curling iron or flat iron with a small barrel size. Remember to use heat protectant spray before applying any heat to the hair.

4. Finish with a setting spray: To ensure that the style lasts all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray or setting spray. This will help to hold the hair in place and prevent frizz or flyaways.
