What Makeup Should A 70 Year Old Wear

Table of Contents

Introduction

As we age, our skin goes through various changes, and so does our makeup routine. For a 70-year-old, it is important to adapt the makeup choices to enhance natural beauty and address specific concerns. In this article, we will discuss the essential makeup tips and products for a 70-year-old to look radiant and confident.

Skincare Preparation

Before applying any makeup, it is crucial to have a well-prepared base. Start with a gentle cleanser to remove any impurities and follow it up with a hydrating moisturizer. Mature skin tends to be drier, so opt for a moisturizer that locks in moisture and plumps up the skin. Additionally, don't forget to apply a broad-spectrum sunscreen to protect your skin from harmful UV rays.

Foundation and Concealer

When it comes to foundation, opt for a lightweight formula that provides medium coverage. Avoid heavy and matte foundations as they can settle into fine lines and wrinkles. Instead, choose a dewy or satin finish foundation that gives a natural glow to the skin. Use a concealer to cover any dark spots or under-eye circles, but be gentle while blending to avoid creasing.

Blush and Bronzer

Adding a touch of color to the cheeks can instantly brighten up the face. Opt for a cream or liquid blush as they blend seamlessly into the skin and give a natural flush. Choose warm, peachy tones that complement your skin tone. To add warmth and dimension, lightly dust a bronzer on the temples, cheekbones, and jawline. Avoid heavy contouring, as it can appear unnatural on mature skin.

Eye Makeup

For a 70-year-old, it is best to keep the eye makeup simple and elegant. Start by applying a neutral eyeshadow shade all over the lid to create a subtle definition. Skip shimmery or glittery eyeshadows as they can draw attention to fine lines. Define the eyes with a soft brown or gray eyeliner, and finish with a few coats of mascara to enhance the lashes. Avoid heavy eyeliner and dramatic false lashes.

Lipstick and Lip Liner

Choose lipstick shades that complement your skin tone and add a youthful touch. Opt for creamy or satin finish lipsticks that hydrate the lips and give a plump appearance. Avoid matte lipsticks, as they can make the lips look dry and accentuate fine lines. To prevent feathering, use a lip liner in a similar shade as your lipstick and carefully outline the lips.

Setting the Makeup

To ensure your makeup lasts longer and stays in place, use a setting powder or spray. However, be cautious not to apply too much powder, as it can settle into fine lines and make the skin appear dry. Instead, lightly dust the powder on the T-zone or areas prone to oiliness. Finish by spritzing a setting spray to lock in the makeup and give a natural, dewy finish.

Makeup Removal

At the end of the day, it is essential to remove your makeup thoroughly. Use a gentle makeup remover or micellar water to dissolve the products. Follow it up with a cleanser to ensure all traces of makeup are removed. Finish with a hydrating night cream to nourish the skin while you sleep.

Conclusion

Makeup for a 70-year-old should focus on enhancing natural beauty and addressing specific concerns. By following the tips mentioned above and choosing suitable products, you can achieve a radiant and confident look. Remember, beauty has no age limit, and with the right makeup choices, you can embrace your age gracefully.
