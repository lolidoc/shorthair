How To Get 1960S Hair Volume

Written by Vannesa Apr 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

The 1960s were known for their iconic hairstyles, and the voluminous hair look was one of the most popular trends of that era. Achieving that signature 1960s hair volume may seem challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can recreate this timeless style today. In this article, we will guide you through step-by-step instructions on how to get 1960s hair volume.

1. Start with the Right Cut

The first step to achieving 1960s hair volume is to start with the right haircut. Opt for layers and a slightly shorter length to add volume and texture. Consult with a professional hairstylist who can give you the perfect cut for your hair type and face shape.

2. Use Volumizing Products

To achieve the desired volume, it's crucial to use the right hair products. Look for volumizing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products specifically designed to add volume and lift to your hair. Apply these products while your hair is damp to maximize their effectiveness.

3. Blow-dry with a Round Brush

When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to create lift at the roots. Start at the crown and work your way down, lifting the hair away from the scalp as you go. This technique will help create volume and give your hair that 1960s look.

4. Tease for Extra Volume

To amp up the volume even more, tease your hair at the crown. Take small sections of hair and gently backcomb them towards the roots. Be careful not to damage your hair while teasing and use a fine-toothed comb to avoid tangling.

5. Set with Velcro Rollers

Once your hair is dry and volumized, set it with Velcro rollers. Take small sections of hair and roll them onto the rollers, securing them in place. Leave the rollers in for about 15-20 minutes to allow your hair to set and cool.

6. Brush Out and Style

After removing the rollers, gently brush out your hair with a soft-bristle brush. This will give your hair that smooth, voluminous finish. Style your hair as desired, whether you prefer a sleek updo or loose, bouncy curls.

7. Use Hairspray for Hold

To make your 1960s hair volume last all day, use a strong-hold hairspray. Mist it over your styled hair to set it in place and prevent it from falling flat. Avoid touching your hair too much to maintain the volume throughout the day.

8. Consider Hair Extensions

If you have fine or thin hair and struggle to achieve the desired volume, consider using clip-in hair extensions. These extensions can add instant thickness and length to your hair, creating that perfect 1960s look.

9. Sleep in Pin Curls

If you want to wake up with voluminous hair, try sleeping in pin curls. After washing your hair, twist small sections and secure them with bobby pins. Sleep with the pins in overnight, and in the morning, remove them for gorgeous 1960s-inspired waves.

10. Embrace the Retro Vibes

Lastly, to truly rock the 1960s hair volume, embrace the retro vibes. Pair your voluminous hair with vintage-inspired clothing, bold makeup, and accessories that reflect the era. Have fun experimenting with different looks and channel your inner 1960s diva.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily achieve 1960s hair volume in today's world. Whether you're attending a themed party or simply want to add a touch of retro glamour to your everyday look, this style is sure to turn heads and make you feel fabulous.
