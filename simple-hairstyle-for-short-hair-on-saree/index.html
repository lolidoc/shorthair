Simple Hairstyles for Short Hair on Saree

When it comes to styling short hair on saree, there are several options that you can choose from. Here are some simple hairstyles that will elevate your look:

1. Side Swept Curls

One of the easiest and most elegant hairstyles for short hair on saree is the side swept curls. This hairstyle adds softness to your look and frames your face beautifully. To achieve this style, curl your hair using a curling iron and then sweep it to one side. Secure the curls with bobby pins, and you're ready to go.

2. Half Updo with a Twist

If you want to add some volume and texture to your short hair, try a half updo with a twist. This hairstyle is simple yet stylish and works well with sarees of all colors and designs. To create this look, take a small section of hair from each side of your head and twist them towards the back. Secure the twists with bobby pins, leaving some hair loose for a relaxed look.

3. Braided Crown

A braided crown is a beautiful and feminine hairstyle that looks stunning with a saree. This hairstyle adds a touch of elegance to your overall look and is perfect for both formal and casual occasions. To achieve this style, create a Dutch braid on one side of your head and wrap it around the crown of your head. Secure the braid with bobby pins, and you're good to go.

4. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is effortless to achieve and works well with short hair on saree. To create this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it around the base to form a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins, leaving some loose strands for a messy look. This hairstyle adds a touch of sophistication to your saree ensemble.

5. Pinned Back Waves

If you want to keep your hair away from your face while still looking stylish, try pinned back waves. This hairstyle is perfect for short hair and adds a glamorous touch to your saree look. To achieve this style, curl your hair using a curling iron and then pin back small sections of hair on both sides of your head. Secure the pins with bobby pins, and you're all set.

How to Achieve a Simple Hairstyle for Short Hair on Saree

To achieve a simple hairstyle for short hair on saree, follow these steps:

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair to ensure it is clean and manageable.

2. Blow-dry your hair or let it air dry, depending on your preference.

3. Decide on the hairstyle you want to achieve and gather the necessary tools and accessories.

4. Section your hair and begin styling it according to the chosen hairstyle. Use a comb or brush to smooth out any tangles or knots.

5. Secure your hairstyle in place using bobby pins, hair ties, or other accessories.

6. Apply hairspray or other styling products to hold your hairstyle in place and add shine.

7. Finally, admire your beautiful hairstyle in the mirror and confidently rock it with your saree!
