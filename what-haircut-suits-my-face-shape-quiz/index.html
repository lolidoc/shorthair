Q: How can I determine my face shape?

A: Determining your face shape is easy. Start by pulling your hair back and looking at your face in the mirror. Pay attention to the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face. This will help you identify whether you have a round, square, oval, or heart-shaped face.

Q: Can I pull off any haircut, regardless of my face shape?

A: While it's true that you can experiment with different haircuts, understanding your face shape can help you choose hairstyles that flatter your features. Certain haircuts are more likely to complement specific face shapes, creating a harmonious and balanced look.

Q: Are there any haircuts that suit all face shapes?

A: Yes, there are certain haircuts that can suit multiple face shapes. These versatile haircuts usually focus on creating balance and enhancing your natural features. For example, a layered bob can flatter round, square, and oval faces by adding movement and dimension.
