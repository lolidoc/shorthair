How to Style Short Hairstyles for 50 Year Old Woman with Thick Wavy Hair

Styling short hairstyles for 50 year old woman with thick wavy hair is relatively easy. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or foam to enhance your natural waves and add texture.
