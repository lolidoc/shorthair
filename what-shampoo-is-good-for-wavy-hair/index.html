How to Use Shampoo for Wavy Hair

To use shampoo for wavy hair, start by wetting your hair thoroughly. Apply a small amount of shampoo to your hands and work it into a lather. Gently massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair, focusing on the roots. Rinse the shampoo out thoroughly with lukewarm water.

After shampooing, follow up with a conditioner to help detangle the hair and provide extra moisture. Apply the conditioner to the mid-lengths and ends of your hair, avoiding the roots. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water.
