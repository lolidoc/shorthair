Q: Can I still have long hair over 60?

Q: How often should I get my haircut?

A: The frequency of haircuts depends on your individual needs and preferences. Some people prefer to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain their hairstyle, while others may go longer between haircuts. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best schedule for your hair.

Q: Can I color my hair if I'm over 60?

A: Yes, you can color your hair if you're over 60. Many women choose to color their hair to cover gray or to enhance their natural color. Consult with a professional hairstylist who can guide you in choosing the best hair color for your individual needs.
