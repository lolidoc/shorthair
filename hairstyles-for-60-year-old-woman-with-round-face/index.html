Hairstyles for 60 Year Old Woman with Round Face: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear my hair long if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair long if you have a round face. However, it is important to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of your head, such as long layers or a layered shag cut. This will help to create the illusion of length and balance out the fullness of your face.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: If you have a round face, it is best to avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face. This includes blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and hairstyles with excessive volume on the sides. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head, while keeping the sides sleek and close to your face.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. Regular trims also help to promote healthy hair growth and keep your hairstyle looking fresh and polished.

Q: Can I color my hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can color your hair if you have a round face. Adding highlights or lowlights can help to add dimension and depth to your hair, creating the illusion of length and structure. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hair color options that will complement your skin tone and enhance your features.
