If you have a round face, the key is to choose a haircut that adds angles and structure to your face. Avoid hairstyles that make your face look even rounder, such as full fringes or long, straight hair. Instead, opt for hairstyles that create the illusion of a more elongated face shape.

One of the best short haircuts for round-faced guys is the classic buzz cut. This hairstyle is low-maintenance and looks great on almost everyone. The buzz cut helps to minimize the roundness of your face and adds a masculine edge to your overall look.

Another great option is the undercut. This hairstyle features short sides and a longer top, which creates contrast and adds dimension to your face. You can style the top in various ways, such as slicking it back or adding some texture with a pomade.

The fade haircut is also a popular choice for round-faced guys. This haircut gradually tapers the hair from the sides to the top, creating a clean and sharp look. You can pair the fade with a short crop or a textured top for added style.
