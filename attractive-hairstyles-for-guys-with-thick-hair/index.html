Are you a guy with thick hair? Are you looking for attractive hairstyles that will suit your hair type? Look no further! In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for guys with thick hair that will make you look stylish and confident.

Having thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it gives you a full and voluminous look, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Many guys with thick hair struggle to find hairstyles that work well with their hair type. They often face challenges such as frizz, unruly strands, and difficulty in achieving desired styles.

If you are one of those guys, don't worry! There are plenty of attractive hairstyles that can enhance the natural texture and thickness of your hair. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there are options that will suit your personal style and complement your features.

Let's take a closer look at some of the best hairstyles for guys with thick hair:

1. The Classic Pompadour

The classic pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. It involves keeping the hair on the sides short and the hair on top long, which is then styled upwards and back. This hairstyle works exceptionally well with thick hair as it adds extra volume and creates a bold, masculine look.

To achieve the classic pompadour, start by applying a generous amount of volumizing mousse to damp hair. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume. Once dry, use a comb to slick back the sides and back, and then use a pomade or wax to style the hair on top upwards and back.

2. The Undercut

The undercut is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your personal style. It involves keeping the hair on the sides and back very short or shaved, while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a striking contrast and accentuates the thickness of your hair.

To achieve the undercut, visit a professional barber who can give you a clean and precise cut. The length on top can vary depending on your preference, but it's best to keep it long enough to style into a quiff, slick back, or messy textured look.

3. The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern and trendy hairstyle that works exceptionally well with thick hair. It involves keeping the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the hair on top longer and textured. This creates a messy and effortless look that is perfect for guys who prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle.

To achieve the textured crop, ask your barber for a short fade on the sides and back, leaving about 2-3 inches of length on top. Style the hair on top using a texturizing product such as a sea salt spray or a matte clay. Use your fingers to create a messy and tousled look, and you're good to go!

4. The Long Wavy Hair

If you have thick hair and prefer a longer hairstyle, the long wavy hair is a great option. This hairstyle embraces the natural texture and volume of your hair, creating a rugged and effortlessly cool look.

To achieve the long wavy hair, let your hair grow out to a medium or long length. Embrace your natural waves by using a curl-enhancing product and scrunching your hair while it's damp. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance the waves. Finish off with a light-hold hairspray to keep the waves in place throughout the day.

Conclusion of attractive hairstyles for guys with thick hair

There you have it! These are just a few examples of attractive hairstyles for guys with thick hair. Remember, the key is to embrace your natural texture and volume, and find a style that suits your personal taste and lifestyle.

Experiment with different hairstyles, products, and techniques to find the perfect look for you. Whether you prefer a classic or modern hairstyle, there are endless options that will make you look stylish and confident.
