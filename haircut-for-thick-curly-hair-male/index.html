Q: Can I straighten my thick curly hair?

A: While it is possible to straighten thick curly hair using heat tools, it is important to proceed with caution. Heat can cause damage to the hair, especially when used frequently or at high temperatures. If you decide to straighten your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature of your styling tools as low as possible. It is also important to deep condition and moisturize your hair regularly to prevent dryness and breakage.

Q: How often should I get a haircut for thick curly hair?

A: The frequency of haircuts for thick curly hair can vary depending on your personal preference and the length of your hair. On average, it is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair. However, if you prefer a longer length or are growing out your hair, you may be able to stretch the time between haircuts to 10-12 weeks.
