Best Hair Style For Oval Face Over 60

Table of Contents

Introduction

As we age, our hair goes through changes, and finding the perfect hairstyle becomes even more important. For women over 60 with an oval face shape, there are plenty of stylish options to choose from. In this article, we will explore the best hair styles that flatter oval faces and enhance the natural beauty of women over 60.

1. Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut is a timeless and low-maintenance hairstyle that suits women of all ages. For women with an oval face shape, a short pixie cut can accentuate your facial features and bring out your best attributes. This hairstyle adds volume to the crown area and creates a youthful and energetic look.

2. Layered Bob

A layered bob is another fantastic option for women over 60 with an oval face shape. This versatile hairstyle offers movement and texture, making it perfect for those who want to add volume and body to their hair. The layers can be customized to flatter your facial features and create a soft and flattering look.

3. Shoulder-Length Waves

If you prefer longer hair, shoulder-length waves are an excellent choice for oval-faced women over 60. This hairstyle adds softness and femininity to your look while framing your face beautifully. To achieve this hairstyle, use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves, and finish with a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Adding side-swept bangs to any hairstyle can instantly transform your look and flatter an oval face shape. Bangs help to shorten the face and draw attention to your eyes. Whether you have short hair or long locks, incorporating side-swept bangs can add a touch of elegance and sophistication to your hairstyle.

5. Classic Bob

The classic bob is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. For oval-faced women over 60, a classic bob that hits just below the chin can create a youthful and fresh look, while adding volume and movement to your hair.

6. Long Layers

If you prefer to keep your hair long, consider adding long layers to create dimension and movement. Long layers can add volume and softness to your hair, making it look more youthful and vibrant. This hairstyle is easy to maintain and can be styled in various ways to suit any occasion.

7. Short Shag

A short shag haircut is a trendy and stylish option for oval-faced women over 60. This haircut adds texture and volume to your hair, creating a chic and edgy look. The layers in a short shag haircut can be tailored to flatter your facial features and enhance your natural beauty.

8. Curly Bob

If you have naturally curly hair, a curly bob is an excellent choice for oval-faced women over 60. This hairstyle embraces your natural curls and adds bounce and movement to your hair. It is a low-maintenance option that can be styled with a diffuser and some curl-enhancing products to define your curls and keep them looking fabulous.

9. Updo with Face-Framing Layers

For special occasions or when you want to dress up, an updo with face-framing layers can be a stunning choice. This hairstyle pulls your hair away from your face while adding soft layers that frame your features beautifully. It creates an elegant and sophisticated look that is perfect for formal events or a night out.

10. Sleek and Straight

For a sleek and polished look, opt for straight hair that is smooth and shiny. This hairstyle can be achieved with a flat iron and some heat protectant spray. Straight hair can make your face appear longer, so it is perfect for oval-faced women over 60 who want to elongate their features. Finish with a glossing serum for added shine and frizz control.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle is crucial for enhancing your natural beauty and boosting your confidence. For oval-faced women over 60, these hair styles offer a range of options that are both flattering and stylish. Whether you prefer short or long hair, curly or straight, there is a perfect hairstyle out there for you. Consult with your hairstylist to find the one that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style the best. Embrace your age and rock a hairstyle that makes you feel beautiful and confident!
