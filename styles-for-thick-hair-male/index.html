Styles For Thick Hair Male In 2023

Written by Vannesa Jul 15, 2023 · 5 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Thick hair is a blessing that many men desire. It provides volume, texture, and endless styling possibilities. However, finding the right hairstyle for thick hair can be a challenge. In this article, we will explore the top styles for men with thick hair in 2023. Whether you prefer a short, medium, or long haircut, we've got you covered.

1. Short and Textured

For those who prefer a low-maintenance look, a short and textured hairstyle is perfect. This style involves keeping the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving some length on top. The hair is then styled with a texturizing product to add definition and movement. This style works well for thick hair as it reduces bulk and allows for easy styling.

2. Undercut with Fringe

The undercut with fringe is a trendy hairstyle that has been popular in recent years and continues to be in 2023. This style involves shaving the sides and back very short, while leaving the top long. The hair on top is then styled into a fringe, which can be swept to the side or worn straight down. Thick hair adds volume and texture to this style, making it look even more stylish and edgy.

3. Medium Length Waves

If you have thick, wavy hair, embracing your natural texture is a great option. Growing your hair to a medium length allows the waves to shine and creates a relaxed and effortless look. To enhance the waves, use a curl-enhancing product and scrunch your hair gently. This style is perfect for those who love a laid-back and beachy vibe.

4. Long and Layered

For those who prefer longer hair, a long and layered style is the way to go. This style involves growing your hair out and adding layers throughout to reduce bulk and add movement. Thick hair provides a great base for this style, as it adds volume and dimension. To keep the hair looking healthy and shiny, make sure to use a good conditioner and avoid excessive heat styling.

5. Pompadour Fade

The pompadour fade is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. This style involves keeping the hair longer on top and gradually fading it shorter on the sides and back. The hair on top is then styled into a voluminous pompadour by using a comb and some pomade. Thick hair adds extra height and fullness to this style, making it look even more impressive.

6. Buzz Cut

For those who prefer a low-maintenance and masculine look, a buzz cut is a great option. This style involves cutting the hair very short all over, creating a clean and sharp appearance. Thick hair adds texture and dimension to this style, making it look even more stylish and modern.

7. Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. This style involves keeping the hair shorter on the sides and longer on top, creating a strip of hair that runs from the front to the back. The hair on top is then styled upwards to create the signature "hawk" look. Thick hair adds volume and texture to this style, making it look even more impressive.

8. Slicked Back

The slicked back hairstyle is a timeless and sophisticated look that never goes out of style. This style involves combing the hair back and using a gel or pomade to keep it in place. Thick hair provides a great base for this style, as it adds volume and hold. This style is perfect for formal occasions or when you want to look polished and put together.

9. Mohawk

If you're feeling bold and daring, a mohawk is a great option for thick hair. This style involves shaving the hair on the sides very short, while leaving a strip of longer hair in the middle. The hair in the middle is then styled upwards to create the signature "mohawk" look. Thick hair adds volume and drama to this style, making it look even more striking.

10. Taper Fade

The taper fade is a popular hairstyle that involves gradually fading the hair shorter from the top to the sides and back. This style creates a clean and polished appearance while still maintaining some length on top. Thick hair adds texture and dimension to this style, making it look even more stylish and modern.

In conclusion, there are numerous stylish options for men with thick hair in 2023. Whether you prefer a short and textured look, a medium length style, or a long and layered haircut, there is a style that suits your preferences. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and stylish.
