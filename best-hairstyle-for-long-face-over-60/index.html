What is the Best Hairstyle for Long Face over 60?

The best hairstyle for a long face over 60 is one that adds width and softness to the face, while also balancing out the length. Here are some key factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle:

1. Layers:

Adding layers to your hair can help to create movement and volume, which can balance out a long face shape. Opt for shorter layers around the face to add softness and frame your features.

2. Volume at the Crown:

Adding volume at the crown of your head can help to create the illusion of width and balance out a long face shape. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to lift the roots and create volume.

3. Short or Medium-Length Styles:

Short or medium-length hairstyles tend to work best for long face shapes, as they help to proportion the face and create a more balanced look. Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can elongate the face even more.

4. Softening Angles:

Softening the angles of your face can help to make a long face shape appear more youthful and vibrant. Opt for hairstyles with soft layers and curves, rather than sharp angles and straight lines.
