Recommendations for Short Hair for Round Faces

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, it's important to consider your individual features and preferences. Here are some recommendations to keep in mind:

3. Layered Cut: Adding layers to your short haircut can create movement and texture, adding volume and height to the hair.

4. Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help to create angles and draw attention away from the width of the face.
