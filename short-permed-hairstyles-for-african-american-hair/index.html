Question and Answer

Q: How long does a short perm last?

A: The duration of a short perm can vary depending on your hair type and how well you maintain it. On average, a perm can last between three to six months.

Q: Can I style my short permed hair with heat?

A: It is generally recommended to avoid using heat styling tools on permed hair, as this can cause damage and weaken the curl pattern. However, if you do choose to use heat, be sure to use a heat protectant and keep the temperature low to minimize damage.

Q: Can I color my short permed hair?

A: It is possible to color permed hair, but it is important to consult with a professional stylist before doing so. They will be able to assess the condition of your hair and recommend the best course of action to avoid damage.

Q: Can I wash my short permed hair every day?

A: It is generally recommended to wash permed hair every two to three days to maintain the curl and prevent dryness. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and personal preference.
