Layered cuts are a great option for round faces because they add movement and texture. By creating layers at different lengths, you can add angles to your face and make it appear longer. This style is versatile and can be worn straight or curled for different looks.

Long bobs, or "lobs," are another popular choice for round faces. This cut typically falls just above the shoulders and adds length to the face. You can opt for a straight bob or add layers for added movement. This style is low-maintenance and works well for both casual and formal occasions.

Angled bobs are a more dramatic option for those who want to make a statement. This cut features a shorter back and longer front, creating an angled effect. The shorter back adds volume and height, while the longer front helps to elongate the face. This style is bold and edgy, perfect for those who want to stand out.

Best Hair Cut for Round Face 2020: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a short cut make my round face look even rounder?

A: Not necessarily. While it's true that some short cuts can emphasize the roundness of a face, there are many short styles that can actually be quite flattering for round faces. Q: Are there any specific hairstyles to avoid for round faces?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules, some hairstyles may not be as flattering for round faces. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt bangs or a one-length cut that falls right at the jawline. Instead, opt for styles that add height and length to create a more elongated look.

Q: Can I still wear my hair curly if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! Curly hair can be incredibly flattering for round faces. The key is to add layers and create volume at the crown to elongate the face. Embrace your natural texture and experiment with different curly styles to find the look that suits you best.
