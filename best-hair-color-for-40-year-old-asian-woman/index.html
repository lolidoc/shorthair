Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: While it is possible to dye your hair at home, it is recommended to consult with a professional hair stylist for the best results. They have the knowledge and expertise to choose the right color and technique that suits your individual needs.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: The frequency of touch-ups depends on the type of hair color and the rate of hair growth. On average, touch-ups are recommended every 4-6 weeks to maintain the color's vibrancy and freshness.

Q: Will coloring my hair damage it?

A: Coloring your hair can cause some damage, especially if not done properly or if harsh chemicals are used. However, with proper care and maintenance, such as using color-safe products and regular deep conditioning treatments, you can minimize the damage and keep your hair healthy.
