Listicle of Easy Braided Hairstyles for Short African American Hair

Here is a listicle of easy braided hairstyles for short African American hair to inspire your next look:

1. Cornrow Updo: Create a sleek and sophisticated updo by braiding small cornrows along the sides of your head and gathering them into a bun at the back.

2. Box Braids: Add length and volume to your short hair with box braids. These braids can be styled in various ways, such as ponytails or updos.

3. Halo Braid: Create a crown-like effect by braiding a small section of hair around your head and securing it with bobby pins.

4. Twist Out: Achieve a defined and voluminous look by twisting small sections of hair and allowing them to set overnight.

5. Faux Hawk: Create a bold and edgy look by braiding the sides of your hair and leaving the center section loose, resembling a mohawk.
