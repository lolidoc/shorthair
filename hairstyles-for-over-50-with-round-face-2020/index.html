The Best Hairstyles for Over 50 with Round Face 2020

Are you a woman over 50 with a round face? Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you look youthful and stylish? If so, you're not alone. Many women in their 50s and beyond face the challenge of finding a hairstyle that suits their round face and enhances their natural beauty. But fear not, because in this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for over 50 with a round face in 2020 and help you find the perfect look that will make you feel confident and beautiful.

When it comes to hairstyles for over 50 with a round face, the key is to create the illusion of length and angles. This means opting for hairstyles that add height and volume to the top of the head, as well as hairstyles that have layers and angles to create the appearance of a more elongated face shape. Additionally, hairstyles that frame the face and draw attention away from the roundness can also be flattering for women over 50 with round faces.

So what are the best hairstyles for over 50 with round face in 2020? Let's take a look:

1. Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut is a great option for women over 50 with round faces. This hairstyle is low-maintenance, stylish, and can help create the illusion of a longer face shape. The short layers and angles of a pixie cut can add height to the crown of the head, making the face appear longer and more balanced. Plus, a pixie cut can also showcase your facial features and highlight your natural beauty.

2. Bob with Side Swept Bangs

A bob with side swept bangs is another excellent choice for women over 50 with round faces. This hairstyle adds angles and movement to the face, creating the illusion of length. The side swept bangs can also help to frame the face and draw attention away from the roundness. A bob can be customized to suit your preferences, whether you prefer a chin-length bob or a longer bob that skims the shoulders.

3. Layered Shag

A layered shag is a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit different face shapes, including round faces. The layers in a shag haircut can add texture and volume, creating the appearance of a longer and more angular face shape. The layers can also help to frame the face and draw attention away from the roundness. Whether you prefer a short or long shag, this hairstyle can give you a youthful and trendy look.

4. Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, opt for long layers. Long layers can help to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. This hairstyle can be versatile and can be worn straight, wavy, or curly, depending on your preferences. Long layers can also add movement and texture to the hair, giving you a youthful and vibrant look.

Conclusion of Hairstyles for Over 50 with Round Face 2020

In conclusion, there are many flattering hairstyles for women over 50 with round faces in 2020. Whether you prefer a short pixie cut, a bob with side swept bangs, a layered shag, or long layers, there is a hairstyle that can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and stylish. Consider experimenting with different hairstyles and consulting with a professional hairstylist to find the perfect look for you. Remember, age is just a number, and with the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best at any age.
