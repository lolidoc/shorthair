What Is The Best Styling Gel For Natural Hair In 2023

Table of Contents

Introduction

When it comes to styling natural hair, finding the right products can be a challenge. With so many options available in the market, it's important to choose a styling gel that not only provides the desired hold but also nourishes and protects your hair. In this article, we will explore the best styling gel for natural hair in 2023.

Factors to Consider

1. Ingredients

One of the key factors to consider when choosing a styling gel for natural hair is the ingredients. Look for gels that are free from harsh chemicals such as sulfates, parabens, and alcohol. Instead, opt for products that contain natural ingredients like aloe vera, shea butter, and essential oils.

2. Hold

The hold of a styling gel determines how well it keeps your hair in place. For natural hair, a medium to strong hold gel is recommended to help define and maintain your curls or coils throughout the day. Avoid gels that leave your hair stiff or crunchy.

3. Moisture and Nourishment

Natural hair tends to be dry, so it's important to choose a styling gel that provides moisture and nourishment. Look for gels that contain hydrating ingredients like glycerin and coconut oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

4. Versatility

Consider how versatile the styling gel is in terms of styling options. Look for gels that can be used for various hairstyles like wash and go, twist outs, or braid outs. A versatile gel allows you to experiment with different looks without having to invest in multiple products.

Top Recommendations

1. Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curling Gel Souffle

This gel is a favorite among natural hair enthusiasts due to its natural ingredients and ability to define curls without leaving them sticky or greasy. It provides a medium hold and helps to reduce frizz, leaving your hair with a healthy shine.

2. Cantu Shea Butter for Natural Hair Moisturizing Twist & Lock Gel

Cantu is a well-known brand in the natural hair community, and this gel is a popular choice for twist outs and braid outs. It provides a strong hold, helps to moisturize and nourish your hair, and leaves it with a soft and shiny finish.

3. Eco Styler Olive Oil Styling Gel

This budget-friendly gel is loved for its strong hold and versatility. It works well for various hairstyles and provides moisture to your hair with the added benefits of olive oil. It also does not leave a residue or build-up on your hair.

4. Aunt Jackie's Don't Shrink Flaxseed Elongating Curling Gel

If you're looking to elongate your curls and add definition, this gel is a great option. It contains flaxseed and wheat proteins, which help to nourish and strengthen your hair while providing a medium hold. It also reduces frizz and adds shine.

Conclusion

Choosing the best styling gel for natural hair is essential to achieve the desired look while keeping your hair healthy. Consider the ingredients, hold, moisture, and versatility of the gel before making a purchase. The recommended gels mentioned above are popular choices in 2023 and offer great results for natural hair styling. Experiment with them to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style!
