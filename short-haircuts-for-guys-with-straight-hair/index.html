Short Haircuts for Guys with Straight Hair

One popular short haircut for guys with straight hair is the classic crew cut. This timeless hairstyle features short hair on the sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top. It is a versatile option that can be styled in various ways, making it suitable for both formal and casual occasions.

Another trendy option for guys with straight hair is the undercut. This haircut features short sides and back, with longer hair on top. The contrast between the short sides and longer top creates a bold and edgy look. The undercut can be styled with a side part, slicked back, or left tousled for a more casual vibe.

A popular short haircut for guys with straight hair is the buzz cut. This ultra-short hairstyle is achieved by cutting the hair very close to the scalp with clippers. The buzz cut is a low-maintenance option that requires minimal styling and is perfect for guys who prefer a no-fuss look.

For those who prefer a slightly longer haircut, the textured crop is a great option. This hairstyle features short hair on the sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top. The top is styled with texture and volume for a messy and effortless look. The textured crop is a versatile option that can be styled in various ways, making it suitable for any occasion.
