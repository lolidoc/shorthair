African American Short Hair Cuts 2020 - Latest Trends and Styles

The Latest Trends and Styles for African American Short Hair Cuts

African American short hair cuts are stylish, versatile, and low-maintenance. In 2020, there were several trendy hairstyles that gained popularity among African American women. Whether you prefer a bold and edgy look or a more classic and elegant style, there's a short haircut for everyone. Let's explore some of the top African American short hair cuts of 2020:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless and chic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is perfect for women who want to showcase their beautiful facial features and highlight their natural beauty. The pixie cut can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair textures. It can be worn sleek and polished or tousled and textured for a more relaxed look.

The Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short hairstyle among African American women. It is versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair textures. The bob can be worn straight and sleek, curly and voluminous, or with added layers for extra dimension. It is a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that can be easily styled for any occasion.

The Afro

The afro is a bold and iconic hairstyle that celebrates natural hair texture. It is a statement-making look that exudes confidence and individuality. The afro can be worn in various sizes and shapes, from a small and compact afro to a larger and more voluminous one. It requires regular maintenance and proper care to keep it healthy and well-defined.

The Tapered Cut

The tapered cut is a modern and stylish short haircut that features shorter hair on the sides and back, gradually getting longer towards the top. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn curly, straight, or with added texture. The tapered cut is perfect for women who want a low-maintenance yet trendy look.

The Mohawk

The Mohawk is a bold and edgy short hairstyle that showcases the perfect balance between femininity and fierceness. It involves shaving or cutting the sides of the head very short while leaving a strip of longer hair in the center. The Mohawk can be styled in various ways, such as braided, twisted, or simply worn up in a faux hawk style.

The Short Natural Haircut

For women who prefer to embrace their natural hair texture, short natural haircuts are a great choice. These haircuts showcase the beauty of natural curls, coils, and kinks. Short natural haircuts can be customized to suit individual preferences, whether it's a TWA (teeny weeny afro), a curly fro, or a defined twist-out.

The Protective Style

Protective styles are hairstyles that help protect the hair from damage and promote healthy growth. These styles include braids, twists, and updos. African American women often opt for short protective styles to give their hair a break from daily styling and manipulation. Short braids or twists can be adorned with stylish accessories to add a touch of flair.

The Shaved Head

The shaved head is a bold and daring choice for women who want to make a statement with their short hair. It involves completely shaving off all the hair on the head, creating a sleek and clean look. The shaved head requires minimal maintenance and can be styled with accessories or left bare for a minimalist look.

The Undercut

The undercut is a trendy and versatile short haircut that involves shaving or cutting the hair on one side or the back of the head shorter than the rest. It adds a touch of edginess and uniqueness to any hairstyle. The undercut can be hidden or showcased by styling the longer hair on top in various ways.

The Curly Pixie

For women with naturally curly hair, the curly pixie is a stylish and low-maintenance option. It embraces and enhances the natural curl pattern while keeping the hair short and manageable. The curly pixie can be styled with lightweight products to enhance the curls and add definition.
