What is the Best Haircut for Over 50 with a Round Face?

The best haircut for over 50 with a round face is one that balances out the roundness and adds height and length to the face. Here are some key features to look for in a haircut:

Layered Cuts:

Layered cuts can add movement and texture to the hair, creating a more youthful and dynamic look. By removing weight and bulk from the sides of the face, layered cuts can help slim down the appearance of a round face.

Long Bangs:

Long bangs can be a great option for round faces as they can help create the illusion of a longer face shape. By sweeping the bangs to the side or parting them in the middle, you can draw attention away from the roundness of the face.

Volume at the Crown:

Adding volume at the crown of the head can help create height and elongate the face. This can be achieved through teasing or using volumizing products. By lifting the hair at the crown, you can create a more balanced and proportionate look.

Shorter Lengths:

While longer hairstyles can work for round faces, shorter lengths can also be flattering. A short bob or pixie cut can help showcase the features of a round face and add a touch of sophistication.

Best Haircut for Over 50 with a Round Face: Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best haircut for over 50 with a round face:

Consider Your Hair Texture:

Take into account your hair texture when choosing a haircut. Fine hair may benefit from layered cuts to add volume, while thick hair may require weight removal to prevent the face from appearing wider.

Face-Framing Layers:

Opt for face-framing layers that start at the chin or below to create the illusion of a slimmer face shape.

Avoid Heavy Bangs:

Avoid heavy, straight-across bangs as they can make a round face appear shorter and wider. Instead, opt for side-swept or wispy bangs that soften the features and add dimension.

Play with Length:

Experiment with different lengths to find what works best for you. While shorter lengths can be flattering, longer hairstyles can also be customized to flatter a round face shape.
