So what exactly is a low taper fade haircut? A taper fade is a type of haircut that gradually fades from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back. The "taper" refers to the gradual blending of the hair lengths, creating a seamless transition from one length to another. The term "low" in the low taper fade refers to where the fade begins, typically around the ear line or just above it.
