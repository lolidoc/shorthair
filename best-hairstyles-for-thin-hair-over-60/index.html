Question and Answer

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have thin hair over 60?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have thin hair. However, it's important to choose the right cut and styling techniques to add volume and dimension to your hair.

Q: Should I avoid heat styling tools if I have thin hair?

A: It's best to avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair. However, you can still use them occasionally, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray and set the tools to a lower temperature.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that can make thin hair look thicker?
