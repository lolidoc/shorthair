Short Afro Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Michael May 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Afro hairstyles have always been a popular choice and they continue to dominate the fashion scene in 2023 If you have a round face finding the right short Afro hairstyle can enhance your features and bring out your natural beauty In this article we will explore some trendy and flattering short Afro hairstyles that are perfect for round faces

Table of Contents

Introduction

Afro hairstyles have always been a popular choice, and they continue to dominate the fashion scene in 2023. If you have a round face, finding the right short Afro hairstyle can enhance your features and bring out your natural beauty. In this article, we will explore some trendy and flattering short Afro hairstyles that are perfect for round faces.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can beautifully complement a round face. This short hairstyle features cropped sides and back, with slightly longer layers on top. The pixie cut adds height to the crown, elongating the face and creating a more balanced look.

Tips for Styling:

To style the pixie cut, apply a small amount of styling gel or mousse to damp hair and comb it through. Use your fingers to tousle the top layers for a textured look. You can also add a side-swept fringe to further elongate the face.

The Tapered Cut

The tapered cut is another fantastic option for round faces. This hairstyle involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and back while leaving more length on top. The tapered cut adds dimension and creates a slimming effect, making it an excellent choice for those with round faces.

Tips for Styling:

To style the tapered cut, you can use a small amount of styling cream or wax to add definition and shine. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to create texture and shape. You can also experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look.

The Afro Puff

If you have naturally thick and voluminous hair, the Afro puff is a fabulous option for showcasing your curls and adding height to your round face. This hairstyle involves gathering your hair into a high ponytail or bun, creating a stylish and chic look.

Tips for Styling:

To create the perfect Afro puff, start by moisturizing and detangling your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gather your hair at the crown of your head. Secure it with a hair tie or scrunchie and fluff out your curls for added volume. You can also accessorize with headbands or hairpins for a more personalized touch.

The Mohawk

If you're feeling bold and adventurous, the Mohawk is a striking hairstyle that can beautifully complement a round face. This edgy look involves shaving or cutting the hair short on the sides while leaving a strip of longer hair in the center.

Tips for Styling:

To style the Mohawk, you can use a strong-hold styling gel or wax to spike up the center strip of hair. Use your fingers or a comb to create texture and height. You can also experiment with different designs and patterns on the sides for added flair.

The Afro Bob

The Afro bob is a stylish and low-maintenance option for those with round faces. This hairstyle features a short, rounded bob that frames the face and highlights your natural curls.

Tips for Styling:

To style the Afro bob, use a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to define your curls. Use your fingers to scrunch and shape your hair for a more voluminous look. You can also add a deep side part or accessorize with a colorful headband to add flair to your style.

The Buzz Cut

If you're looking for a bold and daring hairstyle, the buzz cut is a fantastic option for round faces. This ultra-short hairstyle involves shaving the hair close to the scalp, creating a sleek and modern look.

Tips for Styling:

With the buzz cut, styling is minimal. Simply keep your scalp clean and moisturized to maintain a healthy appearance. You can also experiment with different hair accessories, such as hats or headscarves, to add a touch of personality to your look.

Conclusion

When it comes to short Afro hairstyles for round faces, there are plenty of options to choose from. Whether you prefer a pixie cut, tapered cut, Afro puff, Mohawk, Afro bob, or buzz cut, these hairstyles can enhance your features and showcase your natural beauty. Experiment with different styles, products, and accessories to find the perfect short Afro hairstyle that suits your personality and flatters your round face shape.
