Best Haircuts for Round Faces Over 60: Recommendations

Here are some recommended haircuts for round faces over 60:

1. Layered Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

This haircut adds height and volume on top, creating the illusion of a longer face shape. The side-swept bangs help to soften the features and add a touch of elegance.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and low-maintenance hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape. Opt for a longer pixie cut with textured layers to add height and volume on top.

3. Angled Bob

An angled bob is a versatile haircut that can be tailored to suit your face shape. Opt for an asymmetrical bob with longer layers in the front to create a slimming effect.

4. Medium-Length Layered Cut

A medium-length layered cut is a great option for those who prefer longer hair. The layers help to add movement and texture to the hair, while the length helps to elongate the face.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces Over 60: Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a round face over 60:

1. Consider your face shape

While round faces share certain characteristics, each face is unique. Take the time to analyze your face shape and consult with a professional hairstylist to find a haircut that suits you.

2. Add height and volume on top

Creating the illusion of a longer face shape can be achieved by adding height and volume on top. This can be done through layered cuts, textured styles, or hairstyles with side-swept bangs.

3. Embrace your natural texture

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, embrace it! Curly and wavy hair can add dimension and movement to your hairstyle, making it more flattering for a round face shape.

4. Consider your lifestyle

Choose a haircut that suits your lifestyle and personal preferences. If you prefer low-maintenance styles, opt for a shorter haircut. If you enjoy styling your hair, a medium-length or longer haircut may be more suitable.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces Over 60: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear long hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear long hair if you have a round face. However, it's important to add layers and volume on top to create a more elongated and balanced look.

Q: Are bangs suitable for round faces?

A: Bangs can be suitable for round faces, but it's important to choose the right style. Side-swept bangs or longer bangs that graze the eyebrows can help to soften the features and create a slimming effect.

Q: Are there any hairstyles to avoid if I have a round face?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules when it comes to hairstyles for round faces, some styles may not be as flattering. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt bobs or straight-across bangs.

Q: How often should I get my haircut?

A: The frequency of haircuts will depend on your hair type and personal preference. On average, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.
