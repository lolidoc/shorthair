Are you a woman over 50 with a round face and wear glasses? Finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge, but don't worry, we've got you covered! In this blog post, we'll explore short hairstyles that are perfect for round faces over 50 with glasses.

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose its volume. This can be especially frustrating for women with round faces, as they often have fuller cheeks. Glasses can also add extra width to the face, making it even more important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and balances out the proportions.

So, what exactly is the target of short hairstyles for round faces over 50 with glasses? The goal is to find a hairstyle that creates the illusion of length and slimness, while also complementing your glasses and enhancing your natural beauty. With the right hairstyle, you can look and feel confident at any age.

In summary, short hairstyles for round faces over 50 with glasses should aim to create the illusion of length and slimness, while also complementing your glasses and enhancing your natural beauty. Now, let's dive deeper into some specific hairstyles that are perfect for women in this category.

Short Bob with Side Swept Bangs

One of the most flattering hairstyles for round faces over 50 with glasses is a short bob with side swept bangs. This hairstyle creates the illusion of length by elongating the face, while the side swept bangs draw attention to the eyes and add a touch of elegance. It's a versatile and timeless look that suits women of all ages.

The key to pulling off this hairstyle is to keep the bob just below the chin and add layers for movement and texture. The side swept bangs should be long enough to cover the forehead but not too heavy, as this can overwhelm the face. Pair this hairstyle with a pair of stylish glasses that complement your facial features.

Pixie Cut with Wispy Bangs

If you're looking for a low-maintenance yet stylish hairstyle, consider a pixie cut with wispy bangs. This short and edgy haircut can work wonders for round faces, as it adds height and dimension to the overall look. The wispy bangs soften the face and draw attention to the eyes, making it a perfect choice for women with glasses.

To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist for a short pixie cut with textured layers. The bangs should be cut at an angle and slightly longer on one side to create a flattering asymmetrical effect. Don't forget to style your hair with a texturizing product for added volume and hold.

The Shaggy Bob

The shaggy bob is a trendy and youthful hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. This haircut features layers and texture, which can help to elongate the face and add movement to the hair. The shaggy bob is versatile and can be customized to suit your personal style and preferences.

To get the shaggy bob look, ask your hairstylist for a bob haircut with layers throughout. The layers should be feathered and texturized to create a soft and tousled effect. This hairstyle works well with glasses, as it adds a touch of sophistication and complements the facial features.

Conclusion of Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50 with Glasses

In conclusion, finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face over 50 with glasses can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident at any age. Consider trying a short bob with side swept bangs, a pixie cut with wispy bangs, or a shaggy bob to flatter your features and complement your glasses. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape and personal style. With a little experimentation, you'll find a hairstyle that makes you look and feel amazing!

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with glasses if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! A pixie cut can be a great choice for women with round faces, as it adds height and dimension to the overall look. Pair it with stylish glasses that complement your facial features, and you'll have a chic and trendy hairstyle.

Q: What type of glasses should I choose for a round face?

A: When choosing glasses for a round face, opt for frames with angles and geometric shapes to add definition and balance. Avoid round or oval frames, as they can make the face appear even rounder.

Q: Are there any specific hair products that can help enhance my short hairstyle?

A: Yes! Look for volumizing products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to add lift and body to your hair. Additionally, a lightweight styling cream can help to define and separate the layers.

Q: How often should I trim my short hairstyle?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your short haircut. Regular trims will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and polished.

Conclusion of Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50 with Glasses

In conclusion, finding the perfect short hairstyle for a round face over 50 with glasses is all about creating balance and enhancing your natural beauty. Whether you opt for a short bob with side swept bangs, a pixie cut with wispy bangs, or a shaggy bob, make sure to consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape and personal style. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair – after all, age is just a number!
