Haircuts for Long Wavy Hair 2021: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I get a haircut for my long wavy hair?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut for long wavy hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I straighten my long wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your long wavy hair using a flat iron. However, it's important to use heat protectant spray and not to apply too much heat to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How can I reduce frizz in my long wavy hair?

A: To reduce frizz in your long wavy hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, and use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush.

Q: Can I dye my long wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your long wavy hair. However, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best color and technique for your hair type and desired result.
