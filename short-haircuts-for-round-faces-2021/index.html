Are you tired of the same old hairstyles that don't flatter your round face? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will explore the top short haircuts for round faces in 2021. These trendy and stylish haircuts will enhance your features and give you a fresh new look.

One of the biggest pain points for those with round faces is finding a haircut that complements their face shape. Round faces tend to have softer features and lack definition, so it is important to choose a haircut that adds structure and angles. Short haircuts can help create the illusion of a more elongated face and add dimension to your look.

So, what are the best short haircuts for round faces in 2021? Let's dive in and find out! First, the pixie cut is a popular choice for round faces. This short and cropped hairstyle adds height and volume to the crown, making your face appear longer. Another great option is the bob haircut, particularly the angled bob. This haircut creates angles and frames the face, giving it a more oval shape.

In summary, the top short haircuts for round faces in 2021 are the pixie cut and the angled bob. These haircuts add structure and angles to round faces, creating a more elongated and defined look. Now, let's explore each of these haircuts in more detail.

Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2021: Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. For round faces, a pixie cut with added volume on top is ideal. This creates height and elongates the face. You can also opt for side-swept bangs to add angles and definition to your features.

The pixie cut is low-maintenance and easy to style. It is perfect for those who want a chic and edgy look without spending too much time on their hair. With the right styling products, you can achieve a sleek and polished look or a tousled and textured style.

Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2021: Angled Bob

The angled bob is another great option for round faces. This haircut features longer layers in the front and shorter layers in the back, creating an angled silhouette. The longer layers in the front help to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more oval shape.

There are many variations of the angled bob, such as the asymmetrical bob and the inverted bob. These variations add more interest and uniqueness to the haircut. You can also play around with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening, to create different looks.

Now that you know the top short haircuts for round faces in 2021, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look. Remember, the key is to choose a haircut that adds structure and angles to your face, creating a more defined and elongated appearance. With the right haircut, you can enhance your features and boost your confidence!

Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2021: Tips for Styling

When styling short haircuts for round faces, there are a few tips to keep in mind. First, use volumizing products to add height and lift to the crown. This will help create the illusion of a longer face shape. Second, consider adding layers to your haircut. Layers can add movement and texture, making your hair look more dynamic. Finally, experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening, to create different looks and add variety to your hairstyle.

Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2021: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I pull off a pixie cut with a round face?

A: Absolutely! A pixie cut with added volume on top can actually flatter a round face by creating height and elongating the face shape.

Q: Will an angled bob make my face look longer?

A: Yes, an angled bob can help create the illusion of a longer face shape by adding angles and definition to the features.

Q: Are there any short haircuts to avoid for round faces?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules, it is generally recommended to avoid very short and cropped hairstyles that lack volume and height on top. These hairstyles can make the face appear rounder.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut for a short hairstyle?

A: The frequency of haircuts depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain your hairstyle. On average, short haircuts require maintenance every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh and well-maintained.

Conclusion of Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2021

In conclusion, short haircuts for round faces in 2021 can help enhance your features and give you a fresh new look. The pixie cut and angled bob are two popular choices that add structure and angles to round faces, creating a more defined and elongated appearance. With the right styling techniques and products, you can rock a short haircut that flatters your face shape and boosts your confidence. So, go ahead and embrace the short hair trend for round faces!
