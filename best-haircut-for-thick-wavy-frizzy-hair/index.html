Question and Answer

Q: Can I get a short haircut if I have thick, wavy, frizzy hair?

A: While a short haircut can work for some people with thick, wavy, frizzy hair, it may not be the best choice for everyone. Short haircuts can create more volume and make your hair appear even thicker. It is important to consult with a stylist to determine the best haircut for your hair type and desired outcome.

Q: Can I straighten my hair if I have thick, wavy, frizzy hair?

A: Straightening treatments can temporarily tame frizz and make your hair appear straighter. However, these treatments can be damaging to your hair and may not be a long-term solution. It is important to use heat protectant products and avoid excessive heat styling to maintain the health of your hair.

Q: How often should I get a trim with a long layered cut?

A: To maintain the shape of your long layered cut, it is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking fresh.
