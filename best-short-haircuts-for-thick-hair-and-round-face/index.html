What are the Best Short Haircuts for Thick Hair and Round Face?

When it comes to choosing the best haircut for thick hair and a round face, there are several options to consider. Some popular choices include:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and edgy haircut that can add definition to your face and make your features stand out. This haircut works well for thick hair as it adds texture and volume. To create a more elongated look, consider adding side-swept bangs.

2. Bob with Layers

A bob with layers is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. The layers help to add movement and texture to your hair, while the bob length frames your face and creates a more defined look.

3. Shaggy Lob

The shaggy lob is a longer version of the bob haircut that works well for thick hair. The layers and choppy ends add texture and movement to your hair, while the length helps to elongate your face. This haircut is low maintenance and can be styled in various ways.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Adding side-swept bangs to any short haircut can help to create a more elongated look. The bangs frame your face and draw attention away from the roundness. This is a great option for those who want to keep their hair longer but still want to add definition to their face.

5. Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut is a bold and trendy choice for those with thick hair and a round face. This haircut features one side longer than the other, creating an asymmetrical look that adds interest and dimension to your hair. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair.

Best Short Haircuts for Thick Hair and Round Face: Question and Answer

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. The key is to choose a haircut that adds definition and length to your face. By adding volume and texture to your hair, you can create the illusion of a more elongated face.

Q: Can I still style my short hair in different ways?

A: Absolutely! Short haircuts can be versatile and can be styled in various ways. You can use styling products, such as mousse or gel, to add volume and texture to your hair and create different looks.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut?

A: The frequency of haircuts will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your haircut. On average, it is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Q: Can I color my short hair?

A: Yes, you can color your short hair. However, it is important to consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure that the color suits your skin tone and complements your haircut.
