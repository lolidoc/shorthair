Men's Haircuts for Thick Hair 2021: Exploring the Options

When it comes to men's haircuts for thick hair in 2021, there are several options to consider. One popular choice is the undercut, which features short sides and a longer top. This style works well with thick hair as it provides contrast and allows you to showcase your hair's natural texture.

Another trendy option is the textured crop, which involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. This haircut adds texture and volume to thick hair, making it easier to style and maintain.

If you prefer a longer hairstyle, the long layered cut is a great choice. This haircut involves adding layers to the hair to reduce bulk and create movement. It's a versatile option that can be worn straight or styled with waves or curls.
