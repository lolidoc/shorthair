Question and Answer

Q: Will layering my fine wavy hair make it more prone to frizz?

A: Layering can potentially increase frizz in fine wavy hair. To minimize frizz, use products specifically designed for your hair type and consider using a diffuser when drying your hair.

Q: How often should I get my fine wavy hair layered?

A: The frequency of layering depends on how quickly your hair grows and your desired look. On average, it is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your layers.

Q: Can layering fine wavy hair make it appear thinner?

A: Layering can remove some of the weight from the hair, which can make it appear thinner. However, the added volume and movement created by the layers can counteract this effect.

Q: Can I still straighten my fine wavy hair after layering?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your fine wavy hair after layering. The layers will add texture and movement to your hair, but you can easily straighten it using heat styling tools.
