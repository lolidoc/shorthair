Question and Answer about the Best Hair Color for Blonde over 60

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: While it is possible to dye your hair at home, it is recommended to visit a professional hairstylist, especially if you are making a significant change in hair color.

Q: Will dyeing my hair damage it?

A: Dyeing your hair can cause some damage, especially if you are using bleach or lightening products. It is important to follow proper hair care practices and use conditioning treatments regularly to minimize damage.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: The frequency of root touch-ups will depend on the rate of your hair growth and the color contrast between your natural hair color and the dyed hair. It is generally recommended to touch up roots every 4-6 weeks.
