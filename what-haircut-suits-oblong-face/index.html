What is an Oblong Face Shape?

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape, with the forehead, cheeks, and jawline all being relatively similar in width. This face shape is often referred to as rectangular, and it is longer than it is wide.

People with oblong face shapes often have prominent foreheads and angular jawlines. The goal when choosing a haircut for an oblong face is to create balance and soften the angles of the face.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear my hair straight with an oblong face?

A: Absolutely! Straight hair can look great on an oblong face shape. Just be sure to add some volume and width to the sides to create balance.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid with an oblong face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add too much height at the crown, as this can elongate the face even further. Also, steer clear of center partings, as they can make the face appear longer.

Q: Can I wear a bob with an oblong face?

A: Yes, a bob can be a great option for an oblong face shape. Opt for a layered bob that adds volume to the sides and creates the illusion of width.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut with an oblong face?

A: The frequency of haircuts will depend on your individual hair type and desired style. On average, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.
