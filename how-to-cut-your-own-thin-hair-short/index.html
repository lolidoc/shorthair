How to Cut Your Own Thin Hair Short: A Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Dampen your hair with water using a spray bottle.

3. Separate a small section of hair near the back of your head.

4. Use the comb to comb through the section and remove any tangles.

5. Hold the scissors parallel to the hair shaft and make small, precise cuts.

6. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you have achieved the desired length.

7. Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.

8. Style your hair as desired and enjoy your new shorter look!
