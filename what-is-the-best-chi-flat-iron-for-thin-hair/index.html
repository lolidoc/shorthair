Tips for Using a Chi Flat Iron on Thin Hair

When using a Chi flat iron on thin hair, it's important to take a few precautions to prevent damage. First, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using the flat iron. Thin hair is more susceptible to heat damage, so using a lower temperature is key.
