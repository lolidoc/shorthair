Thick Haircuts for Round Faces

Introduction

In 2023, the latest trend in haircuts for round faces is all about embracing your natural thickness. Round faces are characterized by soft, curved features and a lack of sharp angles. Those with thick hair often struggle to find the perfect haircut that flatters their face shape. However, with the right style and length, you can enhance your round face and showcase your voluminous locks. In this article, we will explore some of the best thick haircuts for round faces in 2023.

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round-faced individuals with thick hair. This haircut adds movement and dimension to your hair, making your face appear slimmer. The layers also help to reduce bulk and provide a lighter feel. Opt for layers that start below the chin to elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

2. Textured Bob

A textured bob is a versatile haircut that suits various face shapes, including round faces with thick hair. This style adds texture and volume to your hair, while the shorter length helps to create the illusion of a more elongated face. Ask your hairstylist to incorporate layers and choppy ends for a modern and edgy look.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

If you have a round face and thick hair, consider adding side-swept bangs to your haircut. This style helps to create angles on your face and draw attention away from the roundness. The bangs should be longer on one side and gradually blend into the rest of your hair, creating a flattering and asymmetrical effect.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut may seem daring, but it can be a fantastic option for round faces with thick hair. This short haircut accentuates your facial features and highlights your natural thickness. Opt for a slightly longer pixie cut with textured layers to add movement and dimension. The key is to create height and volume on top to elongate your face shape.

5. Shoulder-Length Shag

A shoulder-length shag is a trendy and low-maintenance haircut for round faces with thick hair. The shaggy layers add texture and volume, while the length falls just below the shoulders, elongating your face. This haircut works well with natural waves or curls, enhancing the overall look and creating a carefree vibe.

6. Blunt Cut with Face-Framing Layers

A blunt cut with face-framing layers is a classic choice for round faces with thick hair. The blunt ends provide a clean and polished look, while the face-framing layers add softness and definition. This haircut helps to create angles and balance out the roundness of your face, making it a flattering option.

7. Bob with Wispy Ends

If you prefer a shorter haircut, consider a bob with wispy ends. This style adds texture and movement to your hair, while the wispy ends soften the overall look. The bob should be slightly longer in the front and shorter in the back to create an angled effect. This haircut works well for both straight and wavy hair.

8. Layered Lob

A layered lob, also known as a long bob, is a popular choice for round faces with thick hair. This haircut falls just above or below the shoulders, creating a sleek and modern look. The layers add volume and movement, while the length elongates your face shape. Style it straight or add loose waves for a more relaxed vibe.

9. Asymmetrical Pixie

An asymmetrical pixie is a bold and stylish choice for round faces with thick hair. This haircut features longer pieces on one side, creating an asymmetrical effect that adds angles to your face. The shorter length on the other side accentuates your facial features and showcases your natural thickness. This edgy haircut is perfect for those who want to make a statement.

10. Curly Bob

For round faces with thick and curly hair, a curly bob is an excellent choice. This haircut embraces your natural curls and adds volume and bounce. The bob should be cut just above the shoulders to create a balanced look. Opt for layers to prevent your hair from becoming too heavy and to enhance your curls' definition.

Conclusion

When it comes to thick haircuts for round faces in 2023, there are plenty of options to choose from. Whether you prefer long layers, a textured bob, or a short pixie cut, the key is to find a style that enhances your natural thickness and flatters your face shape. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the perfect one. Embrace your thick hair and rock your round face with confidence!
