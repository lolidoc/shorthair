What is Thin Hair and Why Does it Lack Volume?

Thin hair refers to hair that has a smaller diameter or fewer individual strands compared to thick hair. It can be genetic or caused by factors such as aging, hormonal changes, or damage from styling and heat. Due to its smaller diameter, thin hair tends to lie flat against the scalp, making it appear limp and lacking in volume. But fear not, there are ways to overcome these challenges and create the illusion of fuller, more voluminous hair.
