Q: Can I wear bangs with my glasses?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear bangs with your glasses. However, it's important to consider your face shape and glasses style when choosing the right type of bangs. For example, if you have a round face, opt for side-swept bangs that will help to elongate your face.

Q: How often do I need to get my short hairstyle trimmed?

A: The frequency of your hair trims will depend on the style and how fast your hair grows. On average, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your short hairstyle.

Q: Can I color my gray hair with a short hairstyle?

A: Absolutely! Short hairstyles can be a great canvas for experimenting with different hair colors. Whether you want to embrace your natural gray hair or add some highlights, there are plenty of options to choose from.

Q: How can I add volume to my short hairstyle?

A: To add volume to your short hairstyle, you can use styling products like root lifters or volumizing sprays. You can also try blow drying your hair upside down to create more lift at the roots.
