Discover Your Perfect Natural Hair Color with Buzzfeed

Are you tired of your current hair color and looking for a change? Do you want to experiment with a natural shade that complements your skin tone? Look no further! Buzzfeed has the ultimate guide to help you find the perfect natural color to dye your hair in 2023.

Understanding Your Skin Tone

Before diving into the world of hair dye, it's important to understand your skin tone. Buzzfeed's experts recommend determining whether you have warm, cool, or neutral undertones. This will help you choose a hair color that enhances your natural beauty.

If you have warm undertones, you're likely to have a golden or peachy complexion. Hair colors like honey blonde, copper, or auburn will complement your skin tone beautifully. On the other hand, if you have cool undertones, your skin may have pink or blue undertones. Shades like ash brown, platinum blonde, or cool black will enhance your features.

For those with neutral undertones, lucky you! You can pull off a wide range of colors, from warm to cool tones. Experiment with chocolate brown, dark red, or even a caramel blonde to find your perfect match.

Consider Your Eye Color

When choosing a natural hair color, it's essential to take your eye color into account. For those with blue eyes, shades like strawberry blonde or light brown can make your eyes pop. If you have green eyes, try deep red or warm brown shades to enhance their natural beauty.

Brown-eyed beauties can experiment with almost any hair color, but warm tones like chestnut brown or golden blonde can bring out the richness in their eyes. Hazel-eyed individuals can opt for shades like caramel or honey to accentuate the different colors in their eyes.

Work with Your Natural Hair Color

If you're not ready to make a drastic change, Buzzfeed suggests working with your natural hair color and enhancing it. For example, if you have naturally dark brown hair, adding caramel highlights can create a subtle yet stunning effect. Balayage or ombre techniques can also be used to add dimension and depth to your hair.

On the other hand, if you have lighter hair, you can experiment with darker shades like chocolate brown or even try a vibrant red for a bolder look. Remember, it's all about finding a color that complements your skin tone and makes you feel confident.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a natural hair color, consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance you're willing to commit to. Some colors may require more frequent touch-ups, while others can grow out more seamlessly. If you have a busy schedule, opt for low-maintenance colors like a soft balayage or natural-looking highlights.

Alternatively, if you're ready to embrace a high-maintenance hair color, go for it! Vibrant reds, platinum blondes, or bold fashion colors can make a statement and show off your personality. Just be prepared to invest time and effort into maintaining your new look.

Consult a Professional

While Buzzfeed's guide provides helpful tips and suggestions, it's always a good idea to consult a professional hairstylist. They can assess your hair type, texture, and condition to recommend the best natural hair color for you. They can also ensure that the dyeing process is done safely and without damaging your hair.

Whether you're looking for a subtle change or a dramatic transformation, Buzzfeed's guide to finding the perfect natural hair color has got you covered. Take the time to explore different options, consider your skin tone, eye color, and lifestyle, and don't be afraid to experiment. With the right hair color, you'll feel confident, beautiful, and ready to take on the world!
