Are you a woman over 50 with a square face shape? Finding the right hairstyle can be challenging, but don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for square face over 50, including tips, recommendations, and fun facts.

As we age, our face shape may change, and certain hairstyles that once looked great may not be as flattering anymore. Square face shapes are characterized by a strong jawline and equally wide cheekbones and forehead. The goal is to find a hairstyle that softens the angles of the face and enhances your natural beauty.

If you have a square face shape and are over 50, it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and makes you feel confident. The right hairstyle can help to minimize the strong jawline and create a more balanced and youthful appearance.

So, what are the best hairstyles for square face over 50? Here are some key points to keep in mind:

1. Consider a Soft, Layered Cut

A soft, layered cut can help to soften the angles of a square face shape. Opt for layers that frame the face and create movement. This will add softness and reduce the emphasis on the jawline. Avoid blunt cuts that can make the face appear more square.

2. Try Bangs or Side-Swept Fringe

Bangs or side-swept fringe can be a great option for square faces over 50. They can help to create the illusion of a longer face and soften the forehead. Opt for longer, wispy bangs or side-swept fringe that graze the eyebrows.

3. Embrace Volume and Texture

Adding volume and texture to your hair can help to balance out a square face shape. Consider using volumizing products, such as mousse or texturizing sprays, and try hairstyles that add height and volume at the crown. This will draw attention away from the jawline.

4. Consider Shorter Hairstyles

Shorter hairstyles can work well for square faces over 50. Pixie cuts, bobs, or short shaggy styles can help to create a more balanced and youthful look. Opt for styles with layers and movement to soften the angles of the face.

5. The Importance of Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color can also make a difference in how flattering a hairstyle is for a square face shape. Consider opting for highlights or lowlights that add dimension and soften the features. Avoid harsh, solid colors that can make the face appear more angular.

6. Consult with a Professional Stylist

When in doubt, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional stylist. They can assess your face shape, hair texture, and personal style to recommend the best hairstyles for you. They can also provide guidance on how to maintain and style your chosen hairstyle.

Conclusion of Best Hairstyles for Square Face Over 50

Finding the right hairstyle for a square face over 50 can be a fun and empowering experience. By considering factors such as soft layers, bangs, volume, and hair color, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance. Remember, it's all about finding a style that makes you feel fabulous and showcases your unique features.
