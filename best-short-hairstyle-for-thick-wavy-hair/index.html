Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with thick, wavy hair?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can work well with thick, wavy hair. It helps to reduce the volume and weight of your hair, making it more manageable.

Q: Will a bob haircut make my thick, wavy hair easier to style?

A: Yes, a bob haircut can help to tame your thick, wavy hair and make it easier to style. It reduces the overall volume and weight of your hair, making it more manageable.

Q: How often should I get my short, thick, wavy hair trimmed?

A: It is recommended to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your short, thick, wavy hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I straighten my thick, wavy hair if I have a short hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can straighten your thick, wavy hair if you have a short hairstyle. However, it is important to use heat protectant products and not apply excessive heat to prevent damage to your hair.
