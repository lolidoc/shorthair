Medium Hairstyles for 40 Year Old Woman 2020: How to Achieve

To achieve a medium hairstyle for 40 year old woman 2020, follow these steps:

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.

2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

3. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to create volume and shape in your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to lift and curl the hair.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to create your desired style. For a sleek and polished look, straighten your hair. For a more textured and tousled look, create loose waves or curls.

5. Finish off your medium hairstyle with a hairspray or styling product to hold the style in place and add shine and definition to your hair.
