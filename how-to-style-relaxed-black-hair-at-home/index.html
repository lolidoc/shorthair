How To Style Relaxed Black Hair At Home In 2023

Table of Contents

Introduction

Styling relaxed black hair at home can be a fun and empowering experience. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of stunning looks without having to visit a salon. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style your relaxed black hair at home in 2023.

1. Preparing Your Hair

Before you start styling, it's important to properly prepare your hair. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair using products specifically formulated for relaxed hair. This will help to moisturize and nourish your strands, making them more manageable.

2. Detangling

Next, gently detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots to minimize breakage. If your hair is prone to tangles, apply a leave-in conditioner or detangling spray to make the process easier.

3. Choosing the Right Style

Now that your hair is prepped and detangled, it's time to decide on the style you want to achieve. Whether you're going for a sleek straight look, bouncy curls, or an updo, make sure to choose a style that complements your face shape and personal style.

4. Straightening

If you prefer a straight hairstyle, you can use a flat iron to achieve sleek and shiny locks. Apply a heat protectant spray to shield your hair from heat damage, then use the flat iron to straighten small sections of hair at a time. Remember to set your flat iron to an appropriate temperature for relaxed hair to avoid excessive heat.

5. Curling

If you want to add curls or waves to your hair, there are several methods you can try. You can use flexi rods, perm rods, or even a curling wand to achieve different types of curls. Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or mousse, to enhance the curl definition and hold.

6. Updo Hairstyles

Updo hairstyles are a great option for special occasions or when you want to keep your hair off your face and neck. You can create a sleek bun, a high ponytail, or even a braided updo. Secure your updo with bobby pins, hair ties, or a hair accessory of your choice.

7. Protective Styling

Protective styling is important to maintain the health and length of your relaxed black hair. Consider options such as braids, twists, or weaves to protect your hair from daily manipulation and environmental stressors.

8. Adding Accessories

To elevate your hairstyle, don't forget to add some accessories. From headbands and hair clips to scarves and turbans, there are endless options to choose from. Experiment with different accessories to add a personal touch to your look.

9. Maintaining Your Style

Once you've achieved your desired hairstyle, it's essential to maintain it. Protect your hair at night by wrapping it with a satin or silk scarf or using a satin pillowcase. Refresh your style with a light mist of water and a bit of leave-in conditioner to combat dryness.

10. Embracing Your Natural Texture

While styling your relaxed black hair can be fun, it's also important to embrace and celebrate your natural texture. Take breaks from heat styling and allow your hair to breathe. Experiment with different hairstyles that enhance the beauty of your natural curls or coils.

By following these tips and techniques, you can confidently style your relaxed black hair at home in 2023. Remember to prioritize the health and well-being of your hair and have fun exploring different styles and looks.
