Haircuts for Long Wavy Hair and Round Face: Explained

When it comes to haircuts for long wavy hair and round faces, there are a few key points to consider. Firstly, it is important to choose a haircut that adds height and volume to the crown of the head. This can be achieved through layered cuts or styles that incorporate curls or waves.

Lastly, softening the roundness of the face can be achieved through adding layers that frame the face, opting for a side part, or choosing a hairstyle that falls below the chin.
