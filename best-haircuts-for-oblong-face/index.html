Are you tired of searching for the perfect haircut that will flatter your oblong face shape? Look no further! In this blog post, we will discuss the best haircuts for oblong face shapes and how to choose the right one for you.

Having an oblong face shape can be a challenge when it comes to finding the right haircut. Many hairstyles can make your face appear even longer and narrower, which is not the most flattering look. But fear not, there are plenty of haircuts that can enhance your features and balance out your face shape.

So, what exactly is an oblong face shape? An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape, with a more elongated forehead and chin. The goal of choosing a haircut for this face shape is to add width and volume to the sides of the face, while minimizing the length.

Here are some of the best haircuts for oblong face shapes:

1. Long Bob

A long bob, also known as a lob, is a great haircut option for oblong face shapes. The length of the bob falls just below the chin, which helps to create the illusion of width. Adding layers to the bob can also help to add volume and movement to the hair, further balancing out the face shape.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are a perfect addition to any haircut for oblong face shapes. They help to break up the length of the face and create the illusion of width. Whether you have long hair or a shorter bob, side swept bangs can be a game changer for your oblong face shape.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold and want to go for a shorter haircut, a pixie cut can be a great option for oblong face shapes. The short length of the hair helps to create the illusion of width, while the layers add volume and texture. This haircut is not only stylish, but it also complements the oblong face shape perfectly.

4. Layered Shoulder Length

A layered shoulder-length haircut is another great option for oblong face shapes. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, while the length falls at the shoulders, creating the illusion of width. This versatile haircut can be styled in many different ways, making it a perfect choice for those with oblong face shapes.

Conclusion:

Choosing the right haircut for an oblong face shape is all about creating balance and adding width to the face. The best haircuts for oblong face shapes include the long bob, side swept bangs, pixie cut, and layered shoulder length haircuts.
