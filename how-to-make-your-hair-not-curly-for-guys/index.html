How to Make Your Hair Not Curly for Guys

Having curly hair can be a challenge for many guys who prefer a straighter look. While embracing your natural curls is always an option, there are ways to temporarily make your hair look straighter. In this article, we will explore various techniques and products that can help you achieve the desired hairstyle. Just remember that these methods are not permanent and require regular maintenance.

1. Blow Drying

Blow drying your hair is one of the easiest and quickest ways to make it appear straighter. Start by washing your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair gently, leaving it slightly damp. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the blow dryer.

Using a round brush, pull the hair taut while blow drying it. Direct the airflow from the roots to the ends, following the brush movement. This technique helps to smooth out the hair cuticles, resulting in a straighter appearance. Finish off with a cool shot of air to set the style.

2. Flat Iron

If you want a more pronounced straight look, a flat iron can be a great tool. Apply a heat protectant spray before using the flat iron to minimize damage. Divide your hair into sections and start straightening from the roots to the ends. Be sure to move the flat iron in a smooth and controlled manner to avoid unnecessary heat exposure.

Remember to set the flat iron to an appropriate temperature based on your hair type and thickness. Fine or damaged hair requires lower heat settings, while thicker hair may need higher temperatures. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the straightened style in place.

3. Hair Relaxer

If you're looking for a longer-lasting solution, a hair relaxer can help. This chemical treatment breaks down the protein bonds in your hair, resulting in a straighter texture. However, it's important to note that hair relaxers can be damaging and should be used with caution.

Consider consulting a professional hairstylist who specializes in chemical treatments to ensure the process is done safely. They will choose the right relaxer formula for your hair type and apply it correctly. Regular touch-ups are necessary to maintain the straightened look.

4. Keratin Treatment

A keratin treatment is another popular option for achieving straight hair. This semi-permanent treatment infuses keratin protein into the hair, smoothing out the cuticles and reducing frizz. It can last for several months, depending on your hair type and aftercare routine.

During the treatment, a hairstylist will apply a keratin solution to your hair and use a flat iron to seal it in. After the treatment, avoid washing your hair for a couple of days and use sulfate-free products to maintain the results. Regular touch-ups are required as the treatment gradually wears off.

Conclusion

While making your hair not curly for guys may require some effort and regular maintenance, there are various methods to achieve the desired straight look. Whether you prefer a temporary solution like blow drying or flat ironing, or are open to chemical treatments like hair relaxers or keratin treatments, consult with a professional hairstylist to find the best option for your hair type and desired style.
