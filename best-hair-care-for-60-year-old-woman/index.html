Question and Answer

Q: How often should I wash my hair as a 60-year-old woman?

A: It is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent excessive dryness.

Q: Can I still color my hair at 60?

A: Absolutely! Many women color their hair well into their 60s. Just make sure to choose gentle, ammonia-free hair dyes.

Q: How can I add volume to my thinning hair?

A: There are several ways to add volume to thinning hair, including using volumizing shampoos, using a root-lifting spray, and getting a layered haircut.

Q: Can I use heat styling tools on my hair?

A: Yes, you can use heat styling tools, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low to prevent damage.
