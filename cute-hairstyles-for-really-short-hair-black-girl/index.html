Cute Hairstyles For Really Short Hair Black Girl

Introduction

Short hair can be just as versatile and stylish as long hair, especially for black girls. Whether you're rocking a TWA (teeny weeny afro) or a pixie cut, there are plenty of cute hairstyles that can enhance your natural beauty and make a statement. In this article, we will explore some trendy and easy-to-maintain hairstyles for black girls with really short hair in 2023.

1. Finger Coils

Finger coils are a great way to define your natural curls and add volume to your short hair. To achieve this style, apply a styling product to damp hair and twist small sections around your finger. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser for quicker results. Finger coils create a stunning texture and can last for several days with the right care.

2. Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a bold and edgy hairstyle that can be achieved with short hair. Simply gather the hair in the middle and secure it with bobby pins or hair clips to create the illusion of a mohawk. You can leave the sides sleek or add some extra flair with shaved designs. This hairstyle is perfect for those looking to make a statement.

3. Twist-Out

A twist-out is a classic hairstyle that works well on short natural hair. Start by dividing your hair into small sections and twisting each section from the roots to the ends. Allow the twists to set overnight or use a hooded dryer for faster results. Once the twists are dry, unravel them to reveal beautiful, defined curls. This hairstyle is versatile and can be worn for various occasions.

4. Mohawk with Shaved Sides

If you're feeling bold and adventurous, a mohawk with shaved sides is a trendy option. Shave the hair on the sides of your head and leave the center section longer. Style the longer section into a faux hawk or create cornrows for added texture and dimension. This hairstyle is fierce and will definitely turn heads.

5. Afro Puffs

Afro puffs are a cute and easy hairstyle for black girls with short hair. Simply gather the hair on the top of your head and secure it with a hair tie or a cute hair accessory. You can leave the rest of your hair down or create two puffs for a playful look. This hairstyle is perfect for everyday wear and can be dressed up or down.

6. Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are a popular protective hairstyle that looks amazing on short hair. Divide your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly. Wrap the twisted section around itself to create a small knot and secure it with a bobby pin. Leave the knots in overnight or until they are completely dry. When you unravel them, you'll have beautiful, defined curls. Bantu knots are versatile and can be worn in various styles.

7. Head Wraps

Head wraps are a stylish and versatile accessory that can instantly elevate any short hairstyle. Choose a colorful or patterned head wrap that complements your outfit and wrap it around your head. You can leave your hair as is or tuck it into the head wrap for a sleeker look. Head wraps not only protect your hair but also add a touch of sophistication to your overall style.

8. Curly Pixie Cut

A curly pixie cut is a chic and low-maintenance hairstyle for black girls with short hair. This style features short, curly hair that frames your face and accentuates your natural beauty. To maintain this hairstyle, simply apply a curl defining product to damp hair and let it air dry or use a diffuser. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a stylish look without much effort.

9. Braided Crown

A braided crown is a stunning and elegant hairstyle for special occasions. Divide your hair into two sections and create two French braids going towards the back of your head. Once you reach the nape of your neck, secure the braids with bobby pins. This hairstyle is not only beautiful but also keeps your hair off your face, making it perfect for hot summer days or formal events.

10. Tapered Cut

If you're looking for a bold and trendy hairstyle, consider a tapered cut. This style features shorter hair on the sides and back, gradually getting longer towards the top. You can leave the top section curly or straighten it for a sleeker look. A tapered cut is a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that allows you to showcase your unique style.

Conclusion

Having really short hair doesn't limit your hairstyle options as a black girl. From finger coils to braided crowns, there are plenty of cute and trendy hairstyles that can enhance your natural beauty and make a statement. Experiment with different styles and find the ones that suit your personality and lifestyle. Embrace your short hair and rock it with confidence!
