How to Style Layers for Thick Straight Hair

Styling layers for thick straight hair can be a breeze with the right techniques and products. Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.

2. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

3. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair. Blow dry your hair in sections, starting from the roots and working your way down.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or a curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair. This will add movement and dimension to your layers.

5. Finish off with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place without weighing it down.
