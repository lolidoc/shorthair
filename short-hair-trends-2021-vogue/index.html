Short Hair Trends 2021 Vogue and How to Achieve Them

Short hair trends 2021 vogue can be achieved with the help of a skilled hairstylist and some styling products. Here are a few popular short hairstyles and how to achieve them:

1. Pixie Cut: The pixie cut is a short and cropped hairstyle that requires regular trims to maintain its shape. To achieve a sleek and polished look, apply a styling cream or gel to damp hair and blow-dry with a round brush. Finish off with a texturizing spray for added volume and texture.

2. Bob: The bob is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your personal style. To achieve a sleek and straight bob, apply a heat protectant spray to dry hair and use a flat iron to straighten the hair. Finish off with a shine serum for a glossy finish. For a tousled and textured bob, apply a sea salt spray to damp hair and scrunch with your fingers.

3. Shag Haircut: The shag haircut is all about texture and movement. To achieve a tousled and effortless look, apply a texturizing spray to damp hair and blow-dry with a diffuser. Use a curling wand to add loose waves and finish off with a flexible hold hairspray for a natural-looking finish.
