Understanding Grey Hair and Its Associations

Grey hair occurs when the pigment cells in the hair follicles stop producing melanin. This can happen naturally with age or as a result of certain medical conditions. Despite the common perception, grey hair is not a sign of poor health or lack of vitality. It is simply a natural part of the aging process.

Grey hair has long been associated with wisdom and experience. In many cultures, it is revered as a symbol of maturity and respect.
