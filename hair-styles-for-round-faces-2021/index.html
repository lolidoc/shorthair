Hair Styles for Round Faces 2021: Enhancing Your Features

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for a round face, it's important to consider the length, texture, and volume of your hair. Here are some popular hair styles for round faces 2021:

1. Long Layers: Long layers can help to elongate the face and create the illusion of length. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair.

2. Side Swept Bangs: Side swept bangs can help to create angles and soften the roundness of the face. They can be paired with a variety of hair lengths and textures.

3. Pixie Cut: A pixie cut can be a bold and stylish choice for round faces. The short length can help to create the illusion of height and elongate the face.

4. Bob Cut: A bob cut is a versatile option that can be tailored to suit your face shape. Opt for a longer bob that ends just below the chin to create the illusion of length.

5. Updo: Updos can be a great option for special occasions or when you want to change up your look. Try a high bun or a sleek ponytail to add height and elongate the face.

6. Beachy Waves: Beachy waves can add texture and movement to your hair, creating the illusion of volume and angles. Use a curling iron or sea salt spray to achieve this look.

7. Layered Bob: A layered bob can help to add texture and movement to your hair, creating the illusion of volume and angles. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair.

8. Half Up, Half Down: A half up, half down hairstyle can create the illusion of length and angles, while still allowing you to show off your hair's natural texture.

9. Sleek Straight Hair: Sleek straight hair can help to elongate the face and create a more angular look. Use a flat iron and a heat protectant spray to achieve this style.

10. Braided Crown: A braided crown can add a whimsical touch to your hairstyle, while also creating the illusion of length and angles. This style works well with both short and long hair.

Hair Styles for Round Faces 2021: Debunking Myths

There are many myths surrounding hair styles for round faces, but it's important to separate fact from fiction. Here are a few common myths and the truth behind them:

Myth 1: Short hair is the only option for round faces. Truth: While short hair can be a flattering choice for round faces, there are plenty of other options to choose from. The key is to find a style that creates the illusion of length and angles.

Myth 2: Bangs are a no-go for round faces. Truth: Bangs can actually be a great option for round faces, as long as they are styled correctly. Side swept bangs can help to create angles and soften the roundness of the face.

Myth 3: Round faces should avoid volume. Truth: Adding volume to your hair can actually help to balance out the roundness of your face. Just make sure to add volume in a way that flatters your features, such as through layers or curls.

Myth 4: Round faces should avoid updos. Truth: Updos can actually be a great option for round faces, as long as they are styled in a way that adds height and elongates the face. Try a high bun or a sleek ponytail for a chic and flattering look.

Hair Styles for Round Faces 2021: Hidden Secrets
