Introduction

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. While it adds a unique charm and personality to your overall look, it can also be challenging to style and manage. If you have medium thick curly hair, you're in luck! In 2023, there are plenty of trendy and stylish hairstyles that are perfect for your hair type. Whether you prefer a short, medium, or long look, we've got you covered. Read on to discover some of the best hairstyles for medium thick curly hair in 2023.

1. The Taper Fade

The taper fade is a classic and versatile hairstyle that works well for all hair types, including medium thick curly hair. This haircut features short sides and back, gradually blending into longer hair on top. It gives you a clean and polished look while allowing your curls to shine. To style, simply apply a curl-enhancing product and use your fingers to shape your curls.

2. The Messy Curls

If you prefer a more relaxed and carefree look, the messy curls hairstyle is perfect for you. This style embraces the natural texture of your hair and requires minimal effort. Simply let your curls air dry or use a diffuser to enhance their shape. You can also apply a lightweight styling cream to add definition and hold to your curls.

3. The Curly Pompadour

The curly pompadour is a modern and stylish hairstyle that adds height and volume to your curly hair. To achieve this look, keep your hair longer on top and style it upwards and back using a curl-enhancing product. This hairstyle works well for medium thick curly hair as it allows your curls to create a statement.

4. The Undercut

The undercut is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that works well with medium thick curly hair. This haircut features short or shaved sides and back, with longer hair on top. It creates a bold contrast and allows your curls to stand out. To style, apply a curl-defining product and use your fingers to shape your curls.

5. The Long and Loose

If you prefer a longer hairstyle, the long and loose look is a great choice for medium thick curly hair. This style allows your curls to flow freely and creates a romantic and effortless vibe. To maintain your curls, use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner and apply a curl-enhancing product to define and control your curls.

6. The Afro

If you're looking to embrace your natural curls and make a bold statement, the afro is the way to go. This iconic hairstyle works well for medium thick curly hair and adds volume and texture to your overall look. To style, apply a leave-in conditioner and use a wide-tooth comb to shape your curls. You can also add a headband or accessories to enhance your afro.

7. The Side Part

The side part is a classic and timeless hairstyle that works well for medium thick curly hair. This style adds a touch of sophistication and elegance to your overall look. To achieve this look, simply create a deep side part and use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls. You can also use a comb or your fingers to shape your curls.

8. The Mohawk

The mohawk is a daring and bold hairstyle that works well for medium thick curly hair. This style features shaved or short sides and a strip of longer hair in the middle. It adds an edgy and rebellious vibe to your overall look. To style, apply a strong-hold gel or wax and use your fingers to shape your curls into a mohawk shape.

9. The Half-Up Half-Down

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish hairstyle, the half-up half-down look is a great choice for medium thick curly hair. This style allows you to showcase your curls while keeping them out of your face. Simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. You can also leave some strands loose to frame your face.

10. The Slicked Back

If you're attending a formal event or want a polished and sleek look, the slicked back hairstyle is perfect for medium thick curly hair. This style requires some extra effort but creates a sophisticated and elegant appearance. To achieve this look, apply a strong-hold gel or pomade and use a comb to slick your curls back. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Conclusion

With the right haircut and styling techniques, medium thick curly hair can be a great asset. In 2023, there are plenty of trendy and stylish hairstyles that are perfect for your hair type. Whether you prefer a short, medium, or long look, there's a hairstyle out there that will complement your curls and enhance your overall look. Experiment with different styles and products to find the one that works best for you. Embrace your natural curls and rock your medium thick curly hair with confidence!
