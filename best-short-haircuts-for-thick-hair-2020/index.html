Having thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's great to have a full head of hair, it can also be time-consuming and challenging to style. Thick hair tends to be heavy, making it difficult to hold a style for long periods. It can also become frizzy or unmanageable if not properly maintained. These pain points can be frustrating and make it hard to find a haircut that works for you.

The best short haircuts for thick hair in 2020 are designed to address these pain points and make styling easier. These haircuts typically involve cutting the hair shorter, which reduces weight and helps to manage the thickness. They also often incorporate layers or texture to add movement and create a more flattering shape. With the right short haircut, you can transform your thick hair into a stylish and manageable look.

As someone with thick hair, I understand the struggle of finding the perfect haircut. After years of dealing with heavy, unmanageable hair, I decided to try a short haircut. I opted for a pixie cut, which involved cutting my hair short all over and adding layers for texture. The transformation was incredible. What is the Best Short Haircut for Thick Hair 2020?

The best short haircut for thick hair in 2020 is subjective and depends on personal preference and face shape. However, there are several popular options that work well for many people with thick hair. These include the pixie cut, bob, lob (long bob), and shag haircut. Each of these haircuts is designed to add movement and reduce weight, making them ideal for thick hair. It's important to consult with a professional hairstylist who can assess your hair type and face shape and recommend the best short haircut for you.

The History and Myth of Best Short Haircuts for Thick Hair 2020

Short haircuts have been popular for centuries, with different trends and styles emerging throughout history. In the 1920s, the bob haircut became a symbol of freedom and rebellion for women. In the 1960s, the pixie cut gained popularity, thanks to style icons like Audrey Hepburn. Today, short haircuts continue to be a popular choice for those with thick hair who want a stylish and low-maintenance look. While there may be myths that short haircuts are not flattering for those with thick hair, this is simply not true. With the right cut and styling techniques, short haircuts can enhance the natural beauty of thick hair.

The Hidden Secret of Best Short Haircuts for Thick Hair 2020

The hidden secret of the best short haircuts for thick hair in 2020 is the power of layers and texture. By incorporating layers into a short haircut, you can remove excess weight and create movement in the hair. This not only makes the hair easier to style but also adds dimension and volume. Texture can also be added through techniques such as razor cutting or point cutting, which create softness and add interest to the hair. The combination of layers and texture is the secret to achieving a stylish and manageable look with short thick hair.

Recommendation of Best Short Haircuts for Thick Hair 2020

They will be able to assess your hair type, face shape, and personal style to recommend the best short haircut for you. They can also provide tips on how to style and maintain your new haircut to ensure it looks its best. Don't be afraid to try something new and embrace the ease and style of a short haircut for thick hair.

Best Short Haircuts for Thick Hair 2020: Tips for Styling and Maintenance

Once you've found the perfect short haircut for your thick hair, it's important to learn how to style and maintain it properly. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Use the right products: Invest in good quality styling products that are specifically designed for thick hair. These products can help add texture, control frizz, and hold your style in place.

2. Don't skip the conditioner: Even though your hair is short, it's still important to use conditioner regularly. This will help keep your hair hydrated and prevent it from becoming dry or brittle.

3. Embrace air-drying: Short haircuts for thick hair often look best when air-dried. Instead of relying on heat styling tools, try letting your hair dry naturally for a more effortless and tousled look.

4. Schedule regular trims: To keep your short haircut looking its best, make sure to schedule regular trims with your hairstylist. This will help prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

5. Experiment with different styling techniques: Don't be afraid to try different styling techniques with your short haircut. Play around with texture, volume, and accessories to create different looks for different occasions.

Conclusion of Best Short Haircuts for Thick Hair 2020

Short haircuts for thick hair in 2020 are a great option for those looking to manage their hair more easily and achieve a stylish look. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can transform your thick hair into a manageable and fashionable hairstyle. Consult with a professional hairstylist to find the best short haircut for your hair type and face shape, and don't forget to experiment and have fun with your new look.
