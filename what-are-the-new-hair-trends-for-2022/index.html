The History and Myth of the New Hair Trends for 2022

While the shag haircut and curtain bangs may seem like new trends, they actually have a rich history. The shag became popular in the 1970s and was famously worn by iconic rock stars like Mick Jagger and David Bowie. It was a rebellious, anti-establishment look that symbolized the counterculture movement of the time. Fast forward to 2022, and the shag is making a comeback, with a modern twist. It's all about embracing your individuality and expressing yourself through your hair.

Curtain bangs, on the other hand, have been popularized by celebrities like Brigitte Bardot and Farrah Fawcett. They became synonymous with the glamorous, carefree style of the 1960s and 1970s. Today, curtain bangs are experiencing a resurgence, with many people embracing their face-framing effect and versatility.

More Details on the New Hair Trends for 2022
