Question and Answer

Q: Can using a flat iron damage your hair?

A: When used correctly and with the right products, a flat iron should not damage your hair. However, using a flat iron that is too hot or using it too frequently can cause damage and breakage. It is important to use a heat protectant spray or serum and to not apply too much heat to your hair.

Q: How often should I use a flat iron on my thick curly hair?

A: It is best to limit your use of a flat iron to no more than a few times a week. Using a flat iron too frequently can cause damage and breakage, so it is important to give your hair time to recover between uses.

Q: Can a flat iron make my thick curly hair straight for the whole day?

A: While a flat iron can help to straighten your hair, the results may not last all day, especially if you have thick curly hair. Factors such as humidity and sweat can cause your hair to revert back to its natural state. However, using a high-quality flat iron and applying a finishing serum or oil can help to prolong the straightness of your hair.

Q: Can I use a flat iron on wet hair?

A: It is not recommended to use a flat iron on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. It is best to wait until your hair is completely dry before using a flat iron.
