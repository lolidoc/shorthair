Are you tired of struggling to find the right parting for your square face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the different parting options that suit a square face and help you enhance your features.

Having a square face shape can be challenging when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. The wrong parting can make your face appear wider and boxier. It's important to find a parting that complements your face shape and brings out your best features.

So, what parting suits a square face? The best parting for a square face shape is a deep side part. This helps to soften the angles of your face and creates a more balanced and flattering look. By shifting the parting to the side, you can create the illusion of a longer face and add more dimension to your hairstyle.

In summary, the deep side part is the most flattering parting for a square face shape. It helps to soften the angles of the face and create a more balanced look. By shifting the parting to the side, you can create the illusion of a longer face and add more dimension to your hairstyle.

What Parting Suits a Square Face: Explained

Now let's dive deeper into why the deep side part is the best choice for a square face shape. The deep side part creates asymmetry, which helps to break up the strong, angular lines of a square face. It also draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones, which are often the most attractive features of a square face.

Historically, the deep side part has been a popular choice for women with square faces. It has been used to create a more feminine and elegant look, while also adding a touch of sophistication. Many celebrities with square faces, such as Angelina Jolie and Keira Knightley, have been known to rock the deep side part and look stunning.

One hidden secret of the deep side part is that it can also be customized to suit different hair types and textures. For example, if you have curly hair, you can create a deep side part that follows the natural pattern of your curls. This adds more volume and movement to your hairstyle, while still maintaining the flattering effect of the deep side part.

Recommendations for Parting a Square Face

If you have a square face shape and want to try out the deep side part, here are a few recommendations to keep in mind:

1. Start with clean, dry hair: It's easier to create a defined parting when your hair is clean and free from any product buildup.

2. Use a tail comb: A tail comb is a great tool for creating a precise parting. Use the pointy end to section off your hair and create a straight line.

3. Experiment with different angles: While the deep side part is the most flattering for a square face, you can also try out different angles to see what works best for you. Some people may find that a slightly off-center parting looks better on them.

4. Add some texture: To enhance the effect of the deep side part, you can add some texture to your hair. Use a curling iron or texturizing spray to create waves or curls, which will add more movement and dimension to your hairstyle.

What Parting Suits a Square Face: Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a parting for a square face:

- Avoid a center parting: A center parting can make a square face appear wider and more boxy. Stick to side partings to create a more balanced and flattering look.

- Consider your hair length: The deep side part works well with both short and long hair. Just make sure to adjust the placement of the parting to suit your hair length and face shape.

- Consult with a hairstylist: If you're unsure about which parting suits you best, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can assess your face shape and recommend the most flattering parting for you.

What Parting Suits a Square Face: Fun Facts

- The deep side part has been a popular choice for many decades. It was particularly popular in the 1920s and 1930s, when women embraced more glamorous and feminine hairstyles.

- The deep side part is often associated with Old Hollywood glamour. Many iconic movie stars, such as Marilyn Monroe and Audrey Hepburn, were known for their signature deep side partings.

- The deep side part can be a versatile choice for different occasions. It can work well for both casual and formal events, depending on how you style your hair.

Conclusion of What Parting Suits a Square Face

If you have a square face shape, the deep side part is your best bet for a flattering and stylish look. It helps to soften the angles of your face and adds more dimension to your hairstyle. Experiment with different angles and textures to find the perfect deep side part for you. Consult with a hairstylist for personalized advice and rock your new look with confidence!

Question and Answer

Q: Can I still try other partings if I have a square face shape?

A: While the deep side part is the most flattering for a square face, you can still experiment with other partings to see what works best for you. Just keep in mind that the deep side part tends to be the most flattering and balanced option.

Q: How do I create a deep side part?

A: To create a deep side part, start by using a tail comb to section off your hair. Then, use the comb to create a straight line from your hairline to the crown of your head. You can also use the comb to add some volume at the roots for a more dramatic effect.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a square face shape?

A: Absolutely! Bangs can be a great addition to a square face shape. Opt for soft, side-swept bangs or wispy fringe to complement the angles of your face.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that I should avoid with a square face shape?

A: While the deep side part is the most flattering option, there are no hard and fast rules when it comes to hairstyles. Experiment with different looks and find what makes you feel confident and beautiful.
