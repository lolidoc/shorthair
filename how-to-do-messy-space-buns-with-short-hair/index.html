Have you ever wanted to rock the trendy messy space buns hairstyle, but felt like your short hair wouldn't allow it? Well, I'm here to tell you that you can absolutely achieve the perfect messy space buns look even with short hair! In this blog post, I will guide you through the steps of how to do messy space buns with short hair and share some tips and tricks to make them look fabulous.

Many people with short hair struggle to find hairstyles that suit their length. Messy space buns are a popular choice, but it can be challenging to figure out how to create them without enough hair to work with. This can be frustrating and may discourage people from trying out this trendy hairstyle.

But fear not! With a few simple techniques and the right tools, you can achieve the perfect messy space buns look even with short hair. Here's how:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by applying a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add some grip and volume to your hair. This will help create a messy and textured look for your space buns. You can also tease your hair at the roots to add extra volume.

Step 2: Divide your hair

Using a comb or your fingers, part your hair down the middle to create two equal sections. This will be the base for your space buns. If you prefer a more asymmetrical look, you can create an off-center part instead.

Step 3: Create mini buns

Take one section of your hair and twist it tightly to create a mini bun at the back of your head. Secure it with bobby pins or hair ties. Repeat this step with the other section of your hair.

Step 4: Mess it up

Now it's time to make your space buns look messy and effortless. Gently pull at the buns to loosen them and create a more voluminous look. You can also tug at some strands of hair around your face to frame it. Don't be afraid to play around and make it as messy as you like!

Step 5: Set it in place

To ensure that your space buns stay in place all day, use a hairspray or texturizing spray to set the style. This will help keep your hair in place while still maintaining that messy look.

Extra Tips for Perfect Messy Space Buns

- If your hair is too short to create full buns, you can try using mini hair donuts or padding to add volume and create the illusion of larger buns.

- Adding accessories like hair clips, ribbons, or flowers can elevate your messy space buns and make them look even more stylish.

- Don't worry if your space buns aren't perfectly symmetrical. The beauty of this hairstyle is in its messy and effortless look, so embrace the imperfections!

- If you're struggling to create the messy look, try using dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add some grit and texture to your hair.

Conclusion of How to Do Messy Space Buns with Short Hair

Short hair shouldn't limit your hairstyle options. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve the perfect messy space buns look even with short hair. Follow the steps outlined above and don't be afraid to experiment and make it your own. Embrace the messy and effortless vibe of this hairstyle and rock those space buns with confidence!

Question and Answer

Q: Can I do messy space buns with shoulder-length hair?

A: Absolutely! Shoulder-length hair is perfect for messy space buns. You may need to use some bobby pins or hair ties to secure the buns in place, but it's definitely doable.

Q: How long does it take to create messy space buns with short hair?

A: The time it takes to create messy space buns with short hair depends on your hair length and skill level. On average, it can take anywhere from 5 to 15 minutes.

Q: Can I create messy space buns with layered short hair?

A: Yes, you can! Layered short hair can add texture and dimension to your space buns. You might need to use some extra bobby pins or hair ties to secure any loose layers.

Q: Can I wear messy space buns for special occasions?

A: Absolutely! Messy space buns are versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. You can add accessories like sparkly pins or beads to make them more festive.
