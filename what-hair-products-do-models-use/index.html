What Hair Products Do Models Use

Written by Exel Feb 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

When it comes to achieving flawless hair, models are often the best source of inspiration. They always seem to have perfect locks that are shiny, voluminous, and full of life. But what hair products do models use to achieve such stunning results? In this article, we will explore the top hair products that models swear by in 2023.

1. Shampoo and Conditioner

Models understand the importance of a good foundation, and that starts with using the right shampoo and conditioner. Many models opt for sulfate-free formulas that are gentle on their hair and scalp. These products help to maintain the natural oils in the hair, leaving it hydrated and healthy-looking.

2. Hair Oil

To achieve that enviable shine, models rely on hair oils. These oils help to nourish and moisturize the hair, leaving it smooth and frizz-free. Models often choose lightweight oils that won't weigh down their hair, such as argan oil or coconut oil.

3. Heat Protectant

Models are no strangers to heat styling tools, but they always prioritize protecting their hair from damage. Heat protectant sprays or serums are essential in their haircare routine. These products create a barrier between the hair and the heat, preventing breakage and minimizing the impact of styling tools.

4. Texturizing Spray

For that effortless, tousled look, models turn to texturizing sprays. These products add volume and texture to the hair, creating a lived-in, beachy vibe. Models often use texturizing sprays to add grip and hold to their hairstyles, whether it's for a runway show or a photoshoot.

5. Dry Shampoo

Models are constantly on the go, and they don't always have time for a full wash and blowout. That's where dry shampoo comes to the rescue. This hair product absorbs excess oil and refreshes the scalp, giving the hair a clean and voluminous appearance. Models rely on dry shampoo to extend the life of their hairstyles between washes.

6. Hairspray

To ensure their hairstyles stay in place all day long, models reach for a trusty hairspray. Whether it's a sleek updo or loose waves, hairspray helps to set the style and keep it intact. Models prefer lightweight hairsprays that provide a strong hold without leaving the hair stiff or sticky.

7. Hair Masks

Models often undergo various styling treatments and constant heat exposure, which can take a toll on their hair. To restore and rejuvenate their locks, models indulge in regular hair masks. These deep-conditioning treatments help to repair damage, hydrate the hair, and improve overall hair health.

8. Volumizing Mousse

To achieve that coveted volume and body, models use volumizing mousse. This product adds lift and fullness to the hair, creating a glamorous and runway-ready look. Models apply the mousse to damp hair before blow-drying for maximum impact.

9. Leave-In Conditioner

Models often have long and luscious locks, and keeping their hair well-moisturized is crucial. Leave-in conditioners provide an extra layer of hydration, making the hair soft, manageable, and tangle-free. Models use leave-in conditioners to maintain their hair's health and prevent breakage.

10. Serum

To add the finishing touch to their hair, models use serums. These lightweight products help to smooth frizz, add shine, and tame flyaways. Models apply a small amount of serum to their palms and run it through their hair for a polished and glossy look.

Conclusion

Models have access to the best hair products on the market, and they know the importance of using the right products for their hair. From shampoo and conditioner to serums and hairsprays, these are the top hair products that models rely on for their flawless locks. Incorporating some of these products into your haircare routine can help you achieve the same stunning results.
