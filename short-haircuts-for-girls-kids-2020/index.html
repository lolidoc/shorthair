Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut is a popular choice for girls/kids who want a bold and edgy look. This haircut is characterized by short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. It can be styled in various ways, including messy and textured or sleek and polished. A short pixie cut is perfect for girls/kids with thick hair, as it helps to reduce bulk and add movement to the hair.

Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic and timeless option for girls/kids. It can be tailored to suit different face shapes and hair types. A short bob is perfect for girls/kids with fine hair, as it adds volume and creates the illusion of thicker hair. On the other hand, a long bob, also known as a lob, is a great choice for girls/kids with thick hair, as it helps to reduce bulk and make the hair more manageable.

Short Shaggy Cut

A short shaggy cut is a trendy and modern option for girls/kids. This haircut is characterized by layers and texture, giving the hair a messy and lived-in look. It can be styled in various ways, including tousled and beachy or sleek and polished. A short shaggy cut is perfect for girls/kids with curly or wavy hair, as it enhances the natural texture and adds movement to the hair.

Question and Answer

Q: Can my child have a short haircut if they have thin hair?

A: Yes, a short haircut can actually make thin hair appear thicker and fuller. A bob or a pixie cut with layers can add volume and texture to thin hair.

Q: How often should I trim my child's short haircut?

A: It is recommended to trim a short haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Q: Are short haircuts suitable for all face shapes?

A: Short haircuts can be tailored to suit different face shapes. For example, a bob or a pixie cut can complement a round face, while a shaggy cut or a lob can flatter an oval face.
