Tips for Using the Best Hair Brush for Thick Frizzy Hair UK

Here are some tips for using the best hair brush for thick frizzy hair in the UK:

  1. Start by gently detangling the ends of your hair before moving upwards.
  2. Use long, fluid strokes to brush through your hair, avoiding quick and jerky movements.
  3. Brush in sections, working from the bottom layers to the top layers.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair serum before brushing to provide extra slip and reduce frizz.
  5. Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as it is more prone to breakage.

Q: How often should I brush my thick frizzy hair?

A: It is best to brush your thick frizzy hair once or twice a day. Avoid excessive brushing, as it can cause breakage and damage to the hair. Focus on gently detangling and smoothening the hair using long, fluid strokes.
