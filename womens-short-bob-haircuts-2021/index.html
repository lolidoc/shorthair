How to Style Women's Short Bob Haircuts 2021

Styling a women's short bob haircut can be quick and easy. Here are a few simple steps to achieve a stylish look:

1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

2. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create your desired style. For a sleek and polished look, straighten your hair using a flat iron. For a more textured and tousled style, use a curling iron to create loose waves.

3. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to add definition and hold to your hairstyle.

4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to set your style and add shine.
