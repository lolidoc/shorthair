Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your long face? Look no further! In this article, we will be exploring the best short hairstyles for long faces that will enhance your features and make you look absolutely stunning. Whether you have a round face, a heart-shaped face, or an oval face, these hairstyles are sure to flatter your long face shape.

When it comes to long faces, one of the biggest pain points is finding a hairstyle that doesn't elongate the face even more. Many hairstyles can make a long face appear even longer, which can be frustrating for those with this face shape. But fear not, as there are plenty of short hairstyles that are perfect for long faces and can help balance out your features.

So what exactly are the best short hairstyles for long faces? Well, there are a few key factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle for a long face. First, you want to look for styles that add width to the face to create the illusion of a more balanced shape. This can be achieved through layers, curls, and volume at the sides of the face. Second, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as these can further elongate the face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that fall just below the chin or above the shoulders.

In summary, the best short hairstyles for long faces are those that add width and balance to the face. These styles should be medium in length, with layers and volume at the sides of the face. By following these guidelines, you can find the perfect short hairstyle that flatters your long face shape and enhances your natural beauty.

Best Short Hairstyles for Long Faces: The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on almost everyone, but it is especially flattering for those with long faces. The bob is typically cut to just below the chin, which adds width to the face and helps balance out the length. This hairstyle can be worn straight, curly, or wavy, depending on your personal preference and hair type. The bob is versatile and can be customized to suit your individual style.

When choosing a bob for a long face, consider adding layers or bangs to soften the look and add dimension. These elements can help break up the length of the face and create a more balanced appearance. Additionally, styling the bob with curls or waves can add volume to the sides of the face and create a fuller, more rounded shape.

Best Short Hairstyles for Long Faces: The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is another great option for those with long faces. This short and chic hairstyle is perfect for those who want to make a bold statement and show off their facial features. The pixie cut is typically cut close to the head and can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and polished to tousled and textured.

When choosing a pixie cut for a long face, consider adding layers or fringe to soften the look and add dimension. These elements can help break up the length of the face and create a more balanced appearance. Additionally, styling the pixie cut with volume at the crown can add height and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

Best Short Hairstyles for Long Faces: The Shag

The shag is a trendy and effortless hairstyle that is perfect for those with long faces. This layered cut adds texture and movement to the hair, which can help balance out the length of the face. The shag is typically cut with choppy layers and can be styled in a variety of ways, from messy and undone to sleek and polished.

When choosing a shag for a long face, consider adding layers throughout the hair to create volume and dimension. These layers can help break up the length of the face and create a more balanced appearance. Additionally, styling the shag with waves or curls can add even more volume and create a fuller, more rounded shape.

Best Short Hairstyles for Long Faces: The Lob

The lob, or long bob, is a versatile and flattering hairstyle that is perfect for those with long faces. This medium-length cut falls just above the shoulders and can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to tousled and textured. The lob is a great option for those who want a shorter hairstyle without fully committing to a bob or pixie cut.

When choosing a lob for a long face, consider adding layers or bangs to soften the look and add dimension. These elements can help break up the length of the face and create a more balanced appearance. Additionally, styling the lob with waves or curls can add volume to the sides of the face and create a fuller, more rounded shape.

Conclusion

When it comes to choosing the best short hairstyles for long faces, it's all about adding width and balance to the face. The bob, pixie cut, shag, and lob are all great options that can flatter your long face shape and enhance your natural beauty. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a more tousled and textured style, there is a short hairstyle out there that is perfect for you. So go ahead, embrace your long face shape and rock one of these fabulous short hairstyles!
