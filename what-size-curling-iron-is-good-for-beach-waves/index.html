What Size Curling Iron is Good for Beach Waves: Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect beach waves with the right size curling iron:

  1. Start with dry, clean hair. If your hair is damp or wet, the curls may not hold as well.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using a curling iron to minimize heat damage.
  3. Section your hair and curl small sections at a time for more defined waves.
  4. Wrap the hair around the curling iron barrel away from your face for a more natural look.
  5. After curling, gently tousle your hair with your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more relaxed look.
