The History and Myth of Short Hair

Throughout history, short hair has been associated with strength, power, and rebellion. In ancient Egypt, both men and women often wore their hair short as a symbol of cleanliness and social status. In the 1920s, the iconic flapper style became popular, with women cutting their hair short to rebel against societal norms and express their newfound independence.
