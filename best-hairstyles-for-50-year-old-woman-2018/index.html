Medium Length Layers

If you prefer a longer style, consider a medium length cut with layers. This style is versatile and can be worn straight, wavy, or curly, depending on your mood and the occasion. Layers can add movement and dimension to your hair, making it appear fuller and more voluminous. Additionally, a medium length cut can help to frame your face and highlight your best features.

When choosing a medium length layered cut, consider your face shape and hair texture. If you have a heart-shaped face, opt for longer layers that start below the chin to balance out your features. If you have a square face, choose shorter layers that start at the cheekbones to soften your jawline. Additionally, if you have thin or fine hair, ask your stylist to add layers throughout your hair to create the illusion of thickness and volume.
