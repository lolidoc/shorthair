How to Cut Short Hair to Make It Look Thicker

One of the best ways to make short hair look thicker is by adding layers. Layers can add movement and volume to your hair, making it appear fuller. Start by sectioning your hair and combing it out to remove any tangles or knots. Then, take small sections of your hair and cut them at an angle, starting from the top and working your way down. This will create layers that add texture and volume to your hair.

Another technique to make short hair look thicker is by opting for a shorter length. Shorter hair carries less weight, making it easier to create volume and texture. Consider cutting your hair to a length that is above your shoulders or even shorter, depending on your preference. This will create the illusion of thickness and make your hair appear fuller.
