Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear my hair up if I have a square face shape and a big forehead?

A: Absolutely! Updos can be a great option for special occasions or when you want to change up your look. Consider adding soft curls or face-framing tendrils to soften the overall appearance.

Q: Are bangs suitable for a square face shape and a big forehead?

A: Bangs can be a fantastic choice for minimizing the appearance of a larger forehead. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are particularly flattering for square face shapes.

Q: Should I avoid short hairstyles if I have a square face shape and a big forehead?

A: Not necessarily! Short hairstyles can look stunning on square face shapes. Consider adding layers or texture to create softness and movement.

Q: How can I determine my face shape?

A: To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Step back and observe the shape you've drawn. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.
