Best Hairstyle for Guys with Thick Hair

Introduction

Having thick hair can be a blessing for men as it provides plenty of opportunities to experiment with different hairstyles. However, it can also be challenging to find the right haircut that suits thick hair. In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for guys with thick hair in the year 2023.

1. Short Textured Crop

A short textured crop is a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle for guys with thick hair. This style involves keeping the sides and back short while leaving some length on top. The hair is then textured and styled with a matte pomade or wax for a messy yet stylish look.

2. Undercut with Long Comb Over

The undercut with a long comb-over is a versatile hairstyle that works well for thick hair. The sides and back are shaved or cut very short, while the top is left longer. The longer hair on top can be styled with a comb-over to create a sleek and polished look.

3. Medium Length Side Part

If you prefer a more classic and timeless look, a medium length side part is a great option. This hairstyle involves keeping the hair medium length on top and creating a deep side part. The hair can be styled with a pomade or wax to add shine and hold.

4. Messy Quiff

The messy quiff is a popular hairstyle for guys with thick hair. This style involves keeping the hair longer on top and styling it upwards and back for a voluminous and textured look. A matte paste or clay can be used to add texture and hold to the hair.

5. Long Wavy Hair

If you have naturally wavy hair, embracing the waves and growing your hair long can create a stylish and effortless look. Long wavy hair can be styled with a sea salt spray or texturizing cream to enhance the waves and add definition.

6. Taper Fade with Faux Hawk

A taper fade with a faux hawk is a bold and edgy hairstyle for guys with thick hair. The sides are faded or tapered, while the hair on top is styled into a faux hawk by pushing it towards the center. This hairstyle requires regular maintenance to keep the fade sharp.

7. Buzz Cut

If you prefer a low-maintenance and practical hairstyle, a buzz cut is a great option for guys with thick hair. This style involves cutting the hair very short all over, creating a clean and minimalistic look.

8. Slicked Back

The slicked-back hairstyle is a classic and sophisticated choice for guys with thick hair. This style involves applying a styling gel or pomade to comb the hair back and create a sleek and polished appearance.

9. Curly Top with Fade

For guys with thick and curly hair, a curly top with a fade is a stylish and trendy option. The hair on top is left longer and styled into defined curls, while the sides and back are faded or tapered for a clean and sharp look.

10. Mohawk

A mohawk is a daring and bold hairstyle for guys with thick hair. This style involves shaving or cutting the hair on the sides very short while leaving a strip of longer hair on top. The longer hair can be styled into a spike or kept textured for a unique look.
