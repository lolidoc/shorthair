The Best Hair Part for a Square Face: How To

Now that you know the best hair part for a square face, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect part:

  1. Start by combing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Decide on the desired part location (e.g., left or right side).
  3. Using a fine-tooth comb, create a clean and straight part from your hairline to the crown of your head.
  4. For a more natural look, use your fingers to gently tousle your hair and soften the part.
  5. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray to hold your part in place.

Question and Answer: What is the Best Hair Part for a Square Face?

Q: Can I wear a middle part with a square face?

A: While a middle part can work for some square faces, it can also accentuate the angles of your face. It's best to opt for a side part or a deep side part to soften your features and create a more balanced look.

Q: How do I know if a side part is right for me?

A: The best way to determine if a side part is right for you is to experiment with different parts and see which one flatters your features the most. Take photos from different angles and seek feedback from friends or a hairstylist to help you make an informed decision.

Q: Can I wear a center part with a square face?

A: While a center part can work for some square faces, it can also make your face appear wider and more angular. If you're unsure, it's best to consult a hairstylist for professional advice and guidance.

Q: How can I make my hair part look more natural?

A: To make your hair part look more natural, use your fingers to gently tousle your hair and soften the part. You can also add some texture or volume to your hair to create a more effortless and lived-in look.
