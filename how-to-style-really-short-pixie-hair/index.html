What is Really Short Pixie Hair?

Really short pixie hair refers to a haircut that is characterized by its short length and cropped style. It typically involves cutting the hair very close to the scalp, resulting in a bold and edgy look. Really short pixie hair is often chosen by individuals who want to make a statement and showcase their confidence and individuality.

One of the key features of really short pixie hair is its versatility. Despite its short length, there are numerous ways to style and personalize this haircut. From adding texture and volume to creating waves or curls, there are endless possibilities when it comes to styling really short pixie hair.

Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a messy and tousled style, really short pixie hair can be adapted to suit your unique taste and preferences. It's a hairstyle that allows you to express your creativity and embrace your individuality.

The History and Myth of Really Short Pixie Hair

The history of really short pixie hair dates back to the early 20th century when women started to challenge societal norms and embrace more unconventional hairstyles. The iconic flapper look of the 1920s, characterized by its short bob and finger waves, paved the way for the popularity of short haircuts among women.

However, it wasn't until the 1950s that the pixie haircut became a mainstream trend. The legendary actress Audrey Hepburn popularized the pixie cut with her iconic role in the film "Roman Holiday." Her short and gamine hairstyle inspired women around the world to embrace the bold and daring look.

Over the years, the pixie haircut has continued to evolve and adapt to changing trends and styles. It has become a symbol of empowerment and self-expression for many women. Today, really short pixie hair is not only a fashion statement but also a way for women to break free from societal expectations and embrace their true selves.

The Hidden Secret of Styling Really Short Pixie Hair

The hidden secret to styling really short pixie hair lies in the products you use. Finding the right products that work for your hair type and desired style can make all the difference in achieving the perfect look.

One product that is essential for styling really short pixie hair is a good quality texturizing spray or powder. This product helps to add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to create different styles and looks. Simply spray or sprinkle the product onto your roots and use your fingers to tousle and scrunch your hair for added volume and texture.

Another secret to styling really short pixie hair is using a small amount of wax or pomade. These products are great for defining and separating individual strands, creating a more polished and structured look. Simply warm up a small amount of wax or pomade between your palms and apply it to your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that need extra definition.

Lastly, don't forget the power of a good hairspray. A lightweight and flexible hairspray can help to hold your style in place without weighing down your hair. Look for a hairspray that provides a strong hold without leaving your hair stiff or crunchy.

Recommendations for Styling Really Short Pixie Hair

When it comes to styling really short pixie hair, there are a few recommendations that can help you achieve the best results:

1. Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair

Clean and conditioned hair provides a good base for styling and helps to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for short hair to enhance its natural texture and volume.

2. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair

Gently comb through your hair with a wide-toothed comb or use your fingers to remove any knots or tangles. This will make it easier to style and prevent breakage or damage to your hair.

3. Experiment with different techniques and products

Don't be afraid to try out different techniques and products to find what works best for your hair type and desired style. From texturizing sprays to waxes and pomades, there are endless options to choose from.

4. Embrace your natural texture

One of the great things about really short pixie hair is that it allows you to embrace your natural texture. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there are ways to enhance and work with your natural texture to create unique and personalized styles.

Tips for Styling Really Short Pixie Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your really short pixie hair like a pro:

1. Add volume with a texturizing spray or powder

Apply a texturizing spray or powder to your roots to add volume and texture. Use your fingers to tousle and scrunch your hair for added volume and lift.

2. Define and separate individual strands with wax or pomade

Warm up a small amount of wax or pomade between your palms and apply it to your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that need extra definition. Use your fingers to separate and define individual strands for a more polished look.

3. Create soft waves with a small curling iron

Wrap small sections of hair around a small curling iron and hold for a few seconds before releasing. This will create soft and natural-looking waves that add dimension and movement to your short hair.

4. Use a lightweight hairspray to hold your style in place

Finish off your look with a lightweight and flexible hairspray to hold your style in place without weighing down your hair. Look for a hairspray that provides a strong hold without leaving your hair stiff or crunchy.
