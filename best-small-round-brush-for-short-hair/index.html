Tips for Using the Best Small Round Brush for Short Hair

When using the best small round brush for short hair, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Start with clean, dry hair: It is easier to style short hair when it is clean and dry. Remove any excess moisture before styling.

2. Use heat protectant: Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

3. Section your hair: Divide your hair into small sections before styling. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that each section gets the attention it needs.

4. Use the brush to create volume: To add volume to your short hair, use the brush to lift the roots and create lift at the crown.

5. Curl or straighten as desired: Depending on the look you are going for, use the brush to create curls or straighten your hair. Wrap small sections of hair around the brush and use a blow dryer to set the style.
