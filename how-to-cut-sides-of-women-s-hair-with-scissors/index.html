The first step in cutting the sides of women's hair with scissors is to section off the hair. Start by parting the hair down the middle, creating two equal sections. Then, use hair clips to keep the sections separate. This will make it easier to work with one side at a time and ensure an even cut.

Next, take one section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to trim the ends. It's important to start with small cuts and gradually work your way up, as you can always trim more if needed. Make sure to keep the scissors parallel to the hair shaft to achieve a straight and even cut.

In summary, cutting the sides of women's hair with scissors requires proper technique and practice. Start by sectioning off the hair and then proceed to trim the ends of each section. Remember to start with small cuts and gradually work your way up. With time and practice, you'll be able to achieve a beautiful and professional-looking result.
