Best Way to Wear Hair for Round Face: Explained

The best way to wear hair for round face is to choose hairstyles that add vertical length and create angles. This can be achieved through various techniques such as:

1. Adding Layers

Adding layers to your hair can create movement and texture, which helps to break up the roundness of your face. Opt for long layers that start around the chin area to add vertical length and create the illusion of a more elongated face shape.

2. Incorporating Asymmetry

Adding asymmetry to your hairstyle can create angles and draw attention away from the roundness of your face. Consider opting for a side-swept fringe or an off-center part to add asymmetry to your look.

3. Height at the Crown

Adding height at the crown of your head can create the illusion of length and balance out the roundness of your face. Try teasing the hair at the crown or opting for a hairstyle with volume and height to achieve this effect.

4. Avoiding Excess Width

Avoid hairstyles that add extra width to your face, such as blunt cuts or hairstyles with too much volume at the sides. Instead, opt for hairstyles that are more streamlined and elongating.
