How to Style Short Pixie Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 50

Styling short pixie hairstyles for thin hair over 50 is relatively simple and requires minimal effort. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair:

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before styling to ensure that it is clean and free from any product buildup.

2. Apply Volumizing Products:

Apply a volumizing mousse or root-lifting spray to the roots of your hair to add volume and body.

3. Use a Round Brush:

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair, lifting the roots to create volume. Focus on the crown area to add height and fullness.

4. Add Texture:

Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray or wax to add texture and definition to your pixie cut. Scrunch the product into your hair and use your fingers to create a tousled and textured look.

5. Finish with Hairspray:

Finish off your style with a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place and add shine.
