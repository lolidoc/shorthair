Tips for Using the Chi G2 Ceramic and Titanium Hairstyling Iron

1. Start with clean, dry hair: It's important to begin with clean, dry hair to achieve the best results. Remove any excess moisture before using the flat iron to prevent damage.

2. Use heat protectant: Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before using the flat iron. This will create a barrier between your hair and the heat, minimizing damage.

3. Section your hair: Divide your hair into small sections before flat ironing. This will ensure that each strand receives equal heat and results in a smoother finish.

4. Adjust the temperature: Fine hair requires lower heat settings to prevent damage. Start with a lower temperature and gradually increase if needed.

5. Glide the flat iron smoothly: Slowly glide the flat iron down each section of hair, using smooth and even pressure. Avoid holding the flat iron in one spot for too long to prevent heat damage.
