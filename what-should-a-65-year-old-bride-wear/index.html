What Should A 65 Year Old Bride Wear

Written by Alexa Mar 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Getting married is a magical experience at any age, and age should never be a barrier to looking and feeling beautiful on your wedding day. If you're a 65-year-old bride-to-be, you might be wondering what to wear to your special day. In this article, we will explore some fabulous options and provide helpful tips to ensure you feel confident and radiant on your wedding day.

Consider Your Personal Style

When selecting your wedding attire, it's important to consider your personal style. Whether you prefer a classic, elegant look or a more contemporary and trendy style, there are plenty of options available. Take some time to browse wedding magazines, Pinterest boards, or consult with a bridal stylist to gather inspiration and find the style that resonates with you.

Opt for a Flattering Silhouette

Choosing the right silhouette is crucial to ensure you feel comfortable and confident on your wedding day. A-line dresses, empire waistlines, and sheath dresses are popular choices for older brides as they flatter the figure and provide a timeless and elegant look. Consider trying on different styles to see which one enhances your best features and makes you feel incredible.

Embrace Color

While traditional white or ivory gowns are always a classic choice, don't be afraid to embrace color on your wedding day. Soft pastels, champagne, or even a bold statement color can be a stunning choice. Consider your skin tone and choose a hue that complements your natural beauty. Remember, it's your day, and you can wear whatever makes you feel radiant.

Choose the Right Fabric

The fabric of your wedding dress plays a crucial role in its overall look and comfort. Opt for fabrics that drape well and feel luxurious against the skin. Chiffon, lace, and silk are excellent choices for mature brides as they create a soft and elegant look. Avoid heavy fabrics that can weigh you down or add bulk to your figure.

Accessorize Thoughtfully

The right accessories can elevate your wedding day look and add a touch of personal style. Consider adding a beautiful veil, a statement necklace, or a pair of elegant earrings. However, be mindful not to overdo it. Choose one or two key accessories that complement your dress and enhance your overall look without overwhelming it.

Comfort is Key

On your wedding day, you want to feel comfortable and at ease. Opt for a dress that allows you to move freely and enjoy every moment of your special day. Consider the weather and venue when choosing the length and style of your dress. If you're having an outdoor wedding in the summer, a lightweight and breathable fabric will keep you cool and comfortable.

Consider a Two-Piece Ensemble

If you're not keen on wearing a traditional wedding gown, consider a chic two-piece ensemble. A tailored blouse paired with a skirt or pants can be a modern and stylish option for a mature bride. This choice allows you to mix and match fabrics, colors, and styles to create a unique and personalized look.

Customize Your Look

Your wedding day is all about you, so don't be afraid to customize your look to reflect your personality. Add unique touches such as a brooch inherited from a loved one, a flower crown, or a special embroidered detail. These personal touches will make your wedding attire even more meaningful and memorable.

Conclusion

As a 65-year-old bride, you have earned the right to feel beautiful and confident on your wedding day. Remember to choose a dress that reflects your personal style, flatters your figure, and makes you feel comfortable. Embrace color, select the right fabric, and accessorize thoughtfully to create a look that is uniquely yours. Whether you choose a traditional gown or a modern ensemble, the most important thing is to feel radiant and celebrate your love in a way that feels authentic to you.
