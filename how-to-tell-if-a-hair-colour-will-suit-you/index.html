How To Tell If A Hair Colour Will Suit You

Introduction

Choosing a new hair colour can be both exciting and daunting. While you may be tempted to try out a trendy shade, it's important to consider whether it will actually suit you. After all, the wrong hair colour can make you look washed out or even older. In this article, we will share some tips to help you determine if a hair colour will suit you or not.

Understanding Your Skin Tone

One of the key factors in determining whether a hair colour will suit you is your skin tone. There are generally two main categories of skin tones: warm and cool. Warm skin tones tend to have yellow or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones. To figure out your skin tone, look at your veins. If they appear blue, you likely have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you likely have a warm skin tone.

Matching Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, hair colours with golden or coppery undertones will suit you best. Think shades like honey blonde, caramel brown, or auburn. These colours will enhance your natural warmth and give you a healthy glow. Avoid cool tones like ash blonde or platinum, as they can make your skin look sallow.

Matching Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, go for hair colours with ashy or cool undertones. Platinum blonde, ash brown, and cherry red are great options. These colours will complement your skin tone and bring out your natural radiance. Warm shades like golden or coppery tones can clash with your cool undertones and make your skin look flushed.

Considering Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour also plays a role in determining which hair colour will suit you. Certain combinations can create a striking contrast and make your eyes pop.

Blue Eyes

If you have blue eyes, try hair colours in the cool spectrum like icy blonde or ash brown. These colours will bring out the blue in your eyes and create a captivating look.

Brown Eyes

For those with brown eyes, warm hair colours like caramel or chocolate brown can enhance your eye colour and add depth to your overall appearance.

Green or Hazel Eyes

If you have green or hazel eyes, you have the flexibility to experiment with a range of hair colours. Shades like copper, auburn, or even a bold red can make your eyes stand out.

Consider Your Personality and Lifestyle

While skin tone and eye colour are important, it's also essential to consider your personality and lifestyle when choosing a hair colour. If you prefer a low-maintenance routine, opt for a shade that requires minimal upkeep. If you're more adventurous and love experimenting, try out bolder or trendier colours.

Consulting a Professional

If you're unsure about which hair colour will suit you, it's always best to consult a professional hairstylist. They have the expertise and knowledge to assess your features and recommend the most flattering hair colour for you. They can also help you achieve the desired shade while minimizing damage to your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair colour can make a significant difference in your appearance. By considering factors like your skin tone, eye colour, personality, and lifestyle, you can find a hair colour that enhances your natural beauty and boosts your confidence. Remember, if in doubt, seek the advice of a professional hairstylist who can guide you towards the perfect hair colour for you.
