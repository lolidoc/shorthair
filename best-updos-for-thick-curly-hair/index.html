Do you have thick curly hair and struggle to find the perfect updo that suits your hair type? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore the best updos for thick curly hair, providing you with inspiration and tips to achieve a stunning hairstyle that will make you feel confident and beautiful.

Thick curly hair can be both a blessing and a challenge. While it adds volume and texture to your hair, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Finding updos that work well with your hair type is essential to ensure your hairstyle lasts all day without frizz or unruly curls.

So, what are the best updos for thick curly hair? The answer to this question may vary depending on your personal style and preferences. However, there are a few tried and true updos that tend to work well with thick curly hair.

Classic Updo

The classic updo is a timeless hairstyle that works well with all hair types, including thick curly hair. This updo involves gathering your hair at the nape of your neck and securing it with bobby pins or a hair tie. You can leave a few tendrils loose to frame your face or opt for a sleek and polished look by smoothing down any flyaways.

To achieve the classic updo, start by applying a styling product to control frizz and enhance your natural curls. Then, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a bun or knot. Secure with bobby pins or a hair tie and use a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a versatile option for thick curly hair. It allows you to showcase your curls while still keeping your hair out of your face. To achieve this look, simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. You can leave your curls loose or add some twists or braids for added interest.

This hairstyle works well for both casual and formal occasions. It can be dressed up with accessories such as flowers or hairpins, or kept simple for a more relaxed look.

Braided Updo

Braids are a great way to add texture and visual interest to your updo. For thick curly hair, a braided updo can be a stunning choice. You can opt for a single braid or multiple braids, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

To create a braided updo, start by sectioning your hair and braiding each section. Then, gather the braids at the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins. You can leave some curls loose for a softer look or tuck them into the braids for a more polished finish.

Conclusion of Best Updos for Thick Curly Hair

When it comes to updos for thick curly hair, the options are endless. Whether you prefer a classic updo, a half-up half-down style, or a braided updo, there is a hairstyle out there that will perfectly complement your hair type and personal style.

Experiment with different updos to find the one that makes you feel the most confident and beautiful. Don't be afraid to add your own personal touch with accessories or variations on the classic styles. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a stunning updo that will make you stand out from the crowd.

So, embrace your thick curly hair and try out some of these best updos for a gorgeous and stylish look!
