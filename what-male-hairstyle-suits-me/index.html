Q: How do I determine my face shape?

A: To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Compare the measurements to determine if you have a square, round, oval, or heart-shaped face.

Q: Can I change my face shape with a hairstyle?

A: While a hairstyle can't change your face shape, it can create the illusion of a different shape. For example, a hairstyle with volume on top and shorter sides can make a round face appear more elongated.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut to maintain my hairstyle?

A: The frequency of haircuts depends on the length and style of your hair. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hairstyle.
