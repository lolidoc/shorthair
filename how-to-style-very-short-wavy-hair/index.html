Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle for your short and wavy hair:

1. Use a curl-enhancing product

To enhance your natural waves, start by using a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or curl cream. Apply the product to damp hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Gently scrunch your hair to encourage the natural wave pattern.

2. Try a diffuser

If you're using a hairdryer, opt for a diffuser attachment. This will help to distribute the heat more evenly and reduce frizz. Use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser to enhance your waves.

3. Embrace the messy look

Short and wavy hair looks great with a messy, tousled look. Embrace the natural texture of your hair and don't be afraid to create a slightly undone hairstyle. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and add some texture, and finish off with a texturizing spray for added hold.
