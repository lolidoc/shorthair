Short Pixie Cuts for Thick Hair Over 50: Styling Tips

When it comes to styling your short pixie cut for thick hair over 50, here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Use a lightweight and volumizing mousse or foam to add texture and lift to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Use a flat iron or curling wand to add curls or waves for a more textured and playful look.
  • Apply a small amount of styling wax or pomade to define and separate your hair.
  • Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.
