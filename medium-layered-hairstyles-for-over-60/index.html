Recommendation of Medium Layered Hairstyles for Over 60

If you are considering a medium layered hairstyle, here are some recommendations to keep in mind:

  • Consult with a professional hairstylist who specializes in medium layered hairstyles for over 60. They can assess your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to recommend a style that suits you.
  • Choose a length that flatters your face shape and complements your features. For example, if you have a round face, opt for longer layers that elongate your face. If you have a heart-shaped face, go for shorter layers that soften your features.
  • Consider your lifestyle and maintenance level when choosing a medium layered style. If you prefer low-maintenance hairstyles, opt for a style that requires minimal styling and upkeep.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques and products to achieve your desired look. Use volumizing products to add lift and texture to your hair, and use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls.

Tips for Styling Medium Layered Hairstyles for Over 60

Here are some tips to help you style your medium layered hairstyle:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  • Use a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair. Lift the roots as you blow dry to create lift and height.
  • For a sleek and polished look, run a flat iron through your hair to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  • To add texture and movement, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls. Hold the iron vertically and wrap sections of hair around the barrel, leaving the ends out for a more relaxed look.
  • Finish with a light-hold hairspray to set your style and add shine.

Question and Answer

Q: Are medium layered hairstyles suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, medium layered hairstyles can be adapted to suit different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. A professional hairstylist can customize the layers to work with your hair texture and create a style that enhances your natural beauty.

Q: How often should I trim my medium layered hairstyle?

A: It is recommended to trim your medium layered hairstyle every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. Regular trims also promote healthy hair growth and keep your locks looking fresh and vibrant.

Q: Can I still wear my medium layered hairstyle in an updo?

A: Absolutely! Medium layered hairstyles can be styled in various updos, such as a messy bun, a braided updo, or a twisted updo. The layers add texture and interest to your updo, making it look more intricate and stylish.

Q: How can I add volume to my medium layered hairstyle?

A: To add volume to your medium layered hairstyle, use volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, at the roots. Blow dry your hair upside down or use a round brush to lift the roots as you dry. You can also tease the crown area gently for added height and volume.
