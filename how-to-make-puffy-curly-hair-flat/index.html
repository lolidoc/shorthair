How To Make Puffy Curly Hair Flat

Written by Exel Jul 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Having puffy curly hair can be a challenge to manage especially if you prefer a sleek and flat look However with the right techniques and products you can achieve the desired result In this article we will share some tips and tricks on how to make puffy curly hair flat

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having puffy curly hair can be a challenge to manage, especially if you prefer a sleek and flat look. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the desired result. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make puffy curly hair flat.

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, it's important to start with clean and conditioned locks. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product build-up and follow it up with a hydrating conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

2. Apply a Smoothing Serum

After washing your hair, apply a smoothing serum or a heat protectant to your damp locks. This will help to reduce frizz and create a sleeker appearance. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When your hair is still damp, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle any knots gently. Start from the ends and work your way up to avoid causing unnecessary breakage. This will help in creating a smooth base for styling.

4. Blow Dry with a Round Brush

To achieve a flat look, blow-dry your hair with a round brush. Start at the roots and slowly work your way down to the ends, pulling the hair straight as you go. This will help in reducing the volume and creating a sleeker appearance.

5. Use a Flat Iron

After blow-drying, use a flat iron to further flatten your hair. Divide your hair into small sections and run the flat iron through each section from root to tip. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using the flat iron to prevent any heat damage.

6. Apply a Lightweight Hair Spray

To keep your hair flat throughout the day, apply a lightweight hair spray. This will help in holding your style in place without weighing it down or making it look greasy. Choose a hairspray specifically designed for a flat finish.

7. Avoid Humidity

Humidity can cause puffy hair, so it's essential to avoid exposing your hair to high humidity environments. If you live in a humid area, consider using an anti-humidity hair product or wearing a scarf or hat to protect your hair.

8. Sleep with a Silk Pillowcase

When you sleep, friction from cotton pillowcases can cause your hair to become frizzy and puffy. To prevent this, sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase. These materials cause less friction, resulting in smoother and flatter hair in the morning.

9. Choose the Right Hairstyles

Opt for hairstyles that help in keeping your hair flat. Sleek ponytails, buns, or braids can help in taming puffy hair. Avoid hairstyles that add volume or make your hair appear bigger.

10. Embrace Natural Fluffiness

While the tips mentioned above will help in making your puffy curly hair flat, it's also important to embrace your natural hair texture. Embracing your natural fluffiness can boost your confidence and make you feel beautiful just the way you are.

Conclusion

With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a sleek and flat look for your puffy curly hair. Remember to start with clean hair, use smoothing serums, blow-dry with a round brush, and use a flat iron. Additionally, avoid humidity, sleep on a silk pillowcase, and choose hairstyles that help in keeping your hair flat. Embrace your natural hair texture and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Now, go flaunt your fabulous hair!
