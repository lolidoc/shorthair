Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it is beautiful and voluminous, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Many people with thick hair struggle to find hairstyles that work for them, especially when it comes to wearing their hair up. Thick hair can be heavy and bulky, making it challenging to create an updo that stays in place and looks elegant. Additionally, thick hair can often overwhelm the face and make it difficult to create a balanced and flattering hairstyle.

If you have thick hair and are looking for ways to wear it up, we have some tips that can help. First and foremost, it is essential to find the right products for your hair. Look for styling products that are specifically formulated for thick hair, as these will provide the hold and control that you need. Additionally, consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra lift to your hair.

When it comes to creating an updo, there are many options to choose from. One popular choice is a messy bun. To create a messy bun, start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape. Secure the bun with bobby pins, and leave a few strands of hair loose to create a relaxed and effortless look.

Another option for wearing thick hair up is a braided updo. To create a braided updo, start by dividing your hair into three sections. Braid each section individually, and then twist the braids together to create a larger braid. Secure the braid with bobby pins, and gently tug on the edges of the braid to create a fuller and more voluminous look.
