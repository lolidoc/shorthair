Short Haircuts for Thick Hair Over 70

Now that we've discussed the benefits and considerations of short haircuts for thick hair over 70, let's dive into some specific hairstyle ideas. Here are a few popular short haircuts for women over 70 with thick hair:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can work well for women of all ages. This cut features short layers that are styled close to the head, creating a sleek and chic look. Pixie cuts can be customized to suit your unique style, whether you prefer a more edgy or feminine look.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another versatile option for women over 70 with thick hair. This style features hair that is cut to just below the chin or at the jawline, creating a flattering and youthful look. Bobs can be styled straight, wavy, or with loose curls, depending on your preference.

3. Shag Cut

The shag cut is a trendy and modern hairstyle that can work well for women over 70 with thick hair. This cut features layers throughout the hair, creating a textured and voluminous look. The shag cut can be styled in various ways, from messy and tousled to sleek and polished.

4. Crop Cut

The crop cut is a short and low-maintenance hairstyle that can be a great option for women over 70. This cut features hair that is cut very short all over, creating a bold and stylish look. The crop cut can be styled with a bit of texture and volume for added flair.

Tips for Short Haircuts for Thick Hair Over 70

If you're considering a short haircut for your thick hair, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Consult with a Professional

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine the best short haircut for your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

2. Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling and maintenance. If you prefer a low-maintenance style, opt for a cut that requires minimal styling and upkeep.

3. Experiment with Styling Products

Short haircuts for thick hair can benefit from the use of styling products, such as volumizing mousse or texturizing spray. These products can help to add volume, texture, and hold to your hair, making it easier to achieve your desired style.

4. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Thick hair has a natural tendency to be voluminous and full-bodied. Instead of fighting against your hair's natural texture, embrace it! Work with your stylist to find a short haircut that enhances and showcases your natural texture.

5. Have Fun with Accessories

Short haircuts provide the perfect canvas for accessorizing. From headbands to hair clips to hats, there are countless ways to add some flair to your short hairstyle. Experiment with different accessories to add some personality to your look.
