Short Haircuts for Naturally Wavy Thick Hair: How to Maintain

Now that you have found the perfect short haircut for your naturally wavy thick hair, it's important to know how to maintain it. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish:

1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for wavy hair. This will help to keep your waves hydrated and frizz-free.

2. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.

3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to enhance the natural texture of your waves. This will help to define and control your curls.

4. Limit the use of heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair. Instead, embrace your natural waves and let them air dry whenever possible.
