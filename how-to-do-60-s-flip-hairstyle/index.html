The 60's flip hairstyle was a popular look during the 1960s and continues to be a classic style that many people love to recreate today. If you're looking to achieve this retro look, you're in the right place! In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to do 60's flip hairstyle, step by step.

Many people struggle with achieving the perfect flip hairstyle, as it requires a certain technique and the right tools. It can be frustrating when your hair just won't cooperate and you end up with a flat or messy flip. But fear not, we're here to help you master the art of the 60's flip!

To achieve the 60's flip hairstyle, you will need a few essential tools: a round brush, a blow dryer, a curling iron, and some hairspray. Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold. Then, blow dry your hair using the round brush to create volume at the roots.

Once your hair is dry, divide it into sections and start curling them with the curling iron. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process for all sections of hair, focusing on the ends to create the flipped effect.

After curling your hair, gently brush through the curls with your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to soften the look. Then, take the front sections of your hair and carefully flip them outwards using the round brush and blow dryer. This will create the signature flip of the 60's hairstyle.

How to Do 60's Flip Hairstyle: A Step-by-Step Guide

Now, let's dive into a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect 60's flip hairstyle:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the roots.

Step 2: Divide and Curl

Divide your hair into sections and start curling them with a curling iron. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process for all sections of hair, focusing on the ends to create the flipped effect.

Step 3: Brush and Flip

Gently brush through the curls with your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to soften the look. Then, take the front sections of your hair and carefully flip them outwards using a round brush and blow dryer.

Step 4: Set with Hairspray

To ensure that your 60's flip hairstyle stays in place all day, finish off with a generous spritz of hairspray. This will help hold the shape and prevent any flyaways.

Conclusion of How to Do 60's Flip Hairstyle

The 60's flip hairstyle is a classic and glamorous look that never goes out of style. By following these steps and using the right tools, you can easily achieve this retro look. Remember to practice and experiment to find the perfect technique for your hair type. So go ahead, rock that 60's flip and embrace your inner vintage vixen!

Question and Answer

Q1: Can I achieve the 60's flip hairstyle with short hair?

A1: While the 60's flip is traditionally done on longer hair, you can still achieve a similar look with shorter hair by using a smaller curling iron and focusing on the ends.

Q2: How long does it take to do the 60's flip hairstyle?

A2: The time it takes to do the 60's flip hairstyle depends on your hair length and thickness. On average, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour.

Q3: Can I achieve the 60's flip hairstyle without heat tools?

A3: While heat tools like curling irons can help create the perfect flip, you can still achieve a similar look by using foam rollers or braiding your hair overnight to create natural waves.

Q4: How do I maintain the 60's flip hairstyle throughout the day?

A4: To maintain the 60's flip hairstyle, carry a small brush or comb with you for touch-ups throughout the day. You can also use a lightweight hairspray to keep the flip in place.
