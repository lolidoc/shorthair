Tips for Good Hairstyles for Round Face Shape

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face shape, there are a few tips to keep in mind. Firstly, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume at the crown of the head, as this helps to elongate the face. Secondly, avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, as this can make the face appear rounder. Finally, consider incorporating layers and angles into your hairstyle, as these can help to create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.
