Understanding Your Skin Tone

Before diving into the quiz, it's essential to understand your skin tone. Your skin tone plays a significant role in determining which hair color will complement your features the most. There are generally three main categories of skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral.

Warm Skin Tone

If you have a warm skin tone, you will notice yellow or golden undertones in your skin. Warm skin tones usually have green, hazel, or amber eyes. People with warm skin tones tend to look best with hair colors that have warm undertones, such as golden blonde, copper, or warm brown shades.

Cool Skin Tone

Cool skin tones have pink or bluish undertones. If you have cool-toned skin, you might have blue, gray, or green eyes. Hair colors that work well with cool skin tones include platinum blonde, ash brown, or cool-toned reds.

Neutral Skin Tone

Neutral skin tones have a balance of warm and cool undertones. This means you can pull off both warm and cool hair colors. Lucky you! Feel free to experiment with a wide range of shades, from warm caramel to cool chocolate brown.
