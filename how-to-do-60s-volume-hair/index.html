How To Achieve 60S Volume Hair Step By Step Guide

Introduction

Are you looking to rock a trendy and glamorous hairstyle from the 60s? The 60s volume hair is a classic look that adds a touch of elegance and sophistication to your overall appearance. In this step-by-step guide, we will walk you through the process of achieving that gorgeous voluminous hair that was so popular in the 60s.

Gather Your Tools

Before you start, make sure you have all the necessary tools at hand. You will need a teasing comb, a round brush, hair clips, a blow dryer, hairspray, and heat protectant spray. These tools will help you create the volume and hold required for the 60s-inspired hairstyle.

Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra body and thickness. Towel-dry your hair gently, and apply a heat protectant spray to shield your locks from the heat of the styling tools.

Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start by creating a part down the middle and then sectioning your hair from ear to ear. This will make it easier to work on each section individually, ensuring that you achieve maximum volume throughout your hair.

Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry each section of your hair starting from the roots. Lift the hair at the roots with the brush and direct the heat of the blow dryer towards the roots to add volume. Continue this process until your hair is completely dry.

Create Teased Sections

Now it's time to add some serious volume to your hair. Take a small section of hair from the crown area and hold it straight up. Using a teasing comb, gently backcomb the section towards the roots. Repeat this process with each section of hair, focusing on the crown and top layers to create height and volume.

Smooth Out Teased Hair

After teasing your hair, gently comb over the top layer to smooth out any tangles or knots. This step will give your voluminous hairstyle a polished and refined look.

Add Finishing Touches

To ensure that your 60s volume hair stays in place all day, spritz some hairspray over your entire head. This will provide the necessary hold and ensure that your hairstyle lasts throughout the day, no matter what activities you have planned.

Optional: Accessorize

If you want to add an extra touch of 60s glamour to your look, consider using accessories such as headbands, scarves, or hair clips. These accessories were popular during the 60s and can elevate your voluminous hairstyle to the next level.

Final Thoughts

With this step-by-step guide, achieving 60s volume hair has never been easier. Whether you're attending a special event or simply want to add some vintage charm to your everyday look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel fabulous. So go ahead, give it a try, and embrace the elegance of the 60s!
