Hairstyles For Guys With Long Thick Hair In 2023

Long thick hair can be a blessing for guys who know how to rock it With the right hairstyle you can turn heads and make a lasting impression In this article we will explore some of the trendiest and most stylish hairstyles for guys with long thick hair in 2023 Whether you prefer a classic or modern look there s something here for everyone

Table of Contents

Introduction

Long thick hair can be a blessing for guys who know how to rock it. With the right hairstyle, you can turn heads and make a lasting impression. In this article, we will explore some of the trendiest and most stylish hairstyles for guys with long thick hair in 2023. Whether you prefer a classic or modern look, there's something here for everyone.

The Classic Pompadour

The classic pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. This look is characterized by short sides and back with long hair on top, which is styled upwards and back. To achieve this look, apply some pomade to your hair and comb it back, creating height at the front. This style works best for guys with thick hair as it adds volume and texture.

The Messy Man Bun

If you want a more relaxed and casual look, the messy man bun is perfect for you. This style involves gathering all your hair into a loose bun at the back of your head. To achieve the messy look, let some strands of hair fall naturally around your face. This hairstyle works well for guys with long thick hair as it keeps your hair out of your face while still looking stylish.

The Undercut

The undercut is a versatile hairstyle that works well for guys with long thick hair. This style involves shaving the sides and back of your head while leaving the top long. You can style the long hair on top in various ways, such as slicking it back or creating a quiff. The undercut adds a modern and edgy touch to your look, making it a popular choice among fashionable guys.

The Surfer Hair

If you want a laid-back and beachy vibe, the surfer hair is the way to go. This style is all about embracing your natural texture and letting your hair flow freely. To achieve this look, apply some sea salt spray to damp hair and scrunch it with your fingers. This will enhance your natural waves and create a tousled, effortless look. Surfer hair works well for guys with long thick hair as it adds movement and dimension.

The Long Ponytail

For a sleek and sophisticated look, consider tying your long thick hair into a ponytail. This style is perfect for formal occasions or when you want to keep your hair out of your face. To achieve the long ponytail, gather all your hair at the back of your head and secure it with a hair tie. You can also add a touch of style by wrapping a small section of hair around the hair tie to conceal it.

The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern and trendy hairstyle that works well for guys with long thick hair. This style involves cutting your hair short on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. The longer hair on top is then styled with texture and movement, creating a messy yet stylish look. To achieve this style, apply some texturizing spray to your hair and use your fingers to create a tousled effect.

The Slicked-Back Look

If you want a polished and sophisticated look, the slicked-back hairstyle is a great choice. This style involves combing your long thick hair back and applying some gel or pomade to keep it in place. This sleek and elegant look is perfect for formal events or when you want to make a statement. The slicked-back look works well for guys with long thick hair as it adds a touch of luxury and refinement.

The Half-Up Half-Down

If you want a versatile and trendy hairstyle, consider the half-up half-down look. This style involves tying the top half of your hair into a small bun or ponytail while leaving the rest down. This look is perfect for guys with long thick hair as it keeps your hair out of your face while still allowing you to showcase your length. You can also add some accessories like a headband or hair clip to elevate the style.

The Braided Man Bun

For a unique and eye-catching hairstyle, try the braided man bun. This style combines the elegance of braids with the practicality of a bun. To achieve this look, start by braiding a small section of hair at the front and secure it with a hair tie. Then, gather the rest of your hair into a bun at the back of your head. This hairstyle works well for guys with long thick hair as it adds a touch of intricacy and style.

Conclusion

With these trendy and stylish hairstyles for guys with long thick hair in 2023, you can confidently embrace your natural texture and make a statement. Whether you prefer a classic or modern look, there's a hairstyle here to suit your taste and personality. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and stylish. Remember to take care of your hair by using quality products and visiting a professional stylist regularly for maintenance. Rock your long thick hair with pride and let your hairstyle reflect your unique personality.
