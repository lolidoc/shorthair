Tips for Finding the Perfect Hairstyle

Having a round face shape can sometimes make it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. However, with long and thick hair, you have a variety of options to choose from. Here are some tips to help you find the ideal long thick hairstyle for your round face:

1. Consider Layers

Adding layers to your long thick hair can help create the illusion of length and add more dimension to your round face. Opt for long layers that start below the chin to elongate your face shape.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can help balance out the width of your round face. Consider getting long, side swept bangs that graze your eyebrows for a stylish and flattering look.

3. Embrace Soft Waves

Soft waves can help soften the angles of a round face and add a touch of femininity. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves that frame your face and draw attention away from the roundness.

4. Avoid Excessive Volume

While volume can add dimension to your hair, too much can make your round face appear even rounder. Opt for hairstyles that have controlled volume rather than excessive poufiness.

Hairstyle Ideas for Long Thick Hair and Round Faces

Now that you know some tips for finding the perfect hairstyle, here are some hairstyle ideas that work well for long thick hair and round faces:

1. Long Layered Haircut

A long layered haircut is versatile and flattering for round faces. The layers help to create movement and add texture to your hair, while the length helps to elongate your face.

2. Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers can draw attention to your best features and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. These layers should start around the cheekbones and gently cascade down, framing the face beautifully.

3. High Ponytail with Face-Framing Pieces

A high ponytail with face-framing pieces can give the appearance of a longer face shape. Pull your hair up into a high ponytail and leave some strands loose around your face to soften the roundness.

4. Half-Up Half-Down Hairstyle

A half-up half-down hairstyle can create the illusion of length and add a touch of elegance to your look. Pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a stylish hair accessory, while leaving the rest of your hair down.

5. Braided Crown

A braided crown hairstyle can add height and elongate your face shape. Create a crown braid by braiding sections of hair from both sides of your head and securing them at the back, creating a beautiful and unique hairstyle.

Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful. These are just a few ideas to get you started on your journey to finding the perfect long thick hairstyle for your round face. Experiment with different styles and consult with a professional hairstylist to find the look that suits you best.
