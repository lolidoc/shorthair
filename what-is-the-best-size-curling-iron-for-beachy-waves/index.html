What is the Best Size Curling Iron for Beachy Waves and How Does it Work?

The best size curling iron for beachy waves is typically around 1.25 inches to 1.5 inches in diameter. This size allows you to create loose, natural-looking waves that resemble those effortless beach waves.

So how does it work? The curling iron heats up the hair, allowing it to bend and hold its shape. When you wrap a section of hair around the barrel and hold it for a few seconds, the heat from the curling iron breaks the hydrogen bonds in the hair, temporarily changing its shape. As the hair cools, it sets in the new shape, creating a curl or wave.

The size of the curling iron barrel determines the size and shape of the curl or wave. A larger barrel size will create bigger, looser waves, while a smaller barrel size will create tighter, more defined curls.

When using a curling iron to create beachy waves, it's important to remember a few key tips. First, always start with dry hair. Wet or damp hair can cause the curl to fall out more quickly. Second, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. Lastly, don't overdo it with the heat. Use a lower heat setting to avoid frying your hair and causing damage.

By following these tips and using the best size curling iron for beachy waves, you can achieve the perfect, effortless look you've been dreaming of.
