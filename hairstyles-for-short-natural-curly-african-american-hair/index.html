Hairstyles For Short Natural Curly African American Hair In 2023

Introduction

Short natural curly African American hair is beautiful and versatile. In 2023, there are numerous trendy hairstyles that can enhance the natural texture of your hair and make a bold fashion statement. Whether you prefer a low-maintenance look or want to experiment with different styles, we have got you covered. Read on to discover some amazing hairstyles that will make heads turn!

1. Tapered Cut

The tapered cut is a popular choice for short natural curly hair. It involves cutting the sides and back shorter while leaving more length on the top. This style creates a bold and edgy look that is easy to maintain. You can enhance your curls by using a curl enhancing gel or cream and scrunching your hair for definition.

2. Afro Puff

If you want a quick and stylish hairstyle, the afro puff is a great option. Simply gather all your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie or a stylish hair accessory. This hairstyle is perfect for both casual and formal occasions and adds volume to your hair.

3. Finger Coils

Finger coils give your short natural curly hair a defined and polished look. To achieve this style, apply a curl enhancing cream to your damp hair and twist small sections around your finger. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser for faster drying. The result is beautiful coils that frame your face.

4. Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are a versatile hairstyle that can be worn both as a protective style and as a statement look. To create Bantu knots, divide your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly. Coil the twisted section around itself to create a knot and secure it with a bobby pin. This hairstyle can be worn overnight to achieve a more defined look or unraveled for a curly and voluminous style.

5. Twist Out

A twist out is a classic and timeless hairstyle for short natural curly hair. To achieve this style, divide your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly. Allow your hair to dry completely or use a hooded dryer for faster drying. Once your hair is dry, unravel the twists to reveal beautiful and defined curls.

6. Mohawk

A Mohawk is a bold and edgy hairstyle that is perfect for those who want to make a statement. To achieve this style, shave or cut the sides of your hair shorter while leaving more length on the top. You can style the top section of your hair into curls, twists, or a sleek updo for a unique and eye-catching look.

7. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a chic and timeless hairstyle that works well with short natural curly hair. This cut involves keeping the hair short all around and adding layers for texture and volume. You can style your pixie cut by adding a side part, using a curl enhancing product, or accessorizing with headbands or hair clips.

8. Faux Hawk

A faux hawk is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that mimics the look of a Mohawk without shaving or cutting the sides of your hair. To achieve a faux hawk, gather the sides of your hair and pin them up towards the center of your head. The result is a voluminous and edgy hairstyle that is perfect for special occasions or a night out.

9. Frohawk

A frohawk is a combination of a fro and a Mohawk, creating a unique and eye-catching hairstyle. To achieve this style, section off the middle portion of your hair and create a high ponytail or multiple ponytails. You can leave the rest of your hair loose or style it into curls or twists for added texture.

10. Short Twist

Short twists are a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in various ways. To achieve this style, divide your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly. You can leave the twists as is for a simple and elegant look or style them into an updo, side-swept style, or a half-up half-down hairstyle.

Conclusion

These are just a few of the many hairstyles that you can try for your short natural curly African American hair in 2023. Remember to experiment with different styles, products, and accessories to find the look that suits your personal style and enhances your natural beauty. Embrace your curls and rock your short hair with confidence!
