Layered Wedge Haircuts for Over 60: What Is It?

A layered wedge haircut is a hairstyle where the hair is cut into multiple layers that gradually increase in length towards the back. This creates a wedge shape, with the shortest layers at the nape of the neck and the longest layers at the crown. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, while the wedge shape adds movement and dimension.

This hairstyle is particularly popular among women over 60 because it can help address common hair concerns such as thinning and lack of volume.
