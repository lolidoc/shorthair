How to Style Short Hairstyles for Fine Thin Hair and Fat Face

Styling short hairstyles for fine thin hair and fat face is easier than you might think. Here are a few simple steps to help you achieve a fabulous look:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Use a lightweight mousse or volumizing spray to add body and volume to your hair.

2. Use a round brush

Use a round brush to create volume and movement in your hair. Blow dry your hair in sections, lifting the roots with the brush to add volume.

3. Add texture

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or a flat iron to add texture to your hair. Curl small sections of your hair away from your face to create soft waves.

4. Finish with a texturizing spray

Finish off your look with a texturizing spray to add definition and hold to your hair. Scrunch the spray into your hair using your fingers to create a tousled and effortless look.
