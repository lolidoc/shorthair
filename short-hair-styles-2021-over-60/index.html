Short Hair Styles 2021 Over 60: The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hair style that has been popular for decades. It is characterized by its short length and cropped layers, which can be styled in a variety of ways. The pixie cut is a great option for women over 60 because it is low-maintenance and can be easily customized to suit your individual features.

If you have a round face shape, a pixie cut with longer layers on top can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. If you have a square face shape, a pixie cut with textured layers can soften your features and add movement to your hair. If you have a heart-shaped face, a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can help to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Short Hair Styles 2021 Over 60: The Bob

The bob is another popular short hair style that is versatile and flattering for women over 60. The bob is characterized by its even length and clean lines, which can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. The bob is a great option for women over 60 because it is timeless and can be styled in a variety of ways.

If you have a round face shape, a bob with longer layers and side-swept bangs can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. If you have a heart-shaped face, a bob with face-framing layers can help to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.
