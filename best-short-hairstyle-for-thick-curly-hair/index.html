Question and Answer

Q: Can I straighten my thick curly hair if I want a different look? A: Yes, you can straighten your thick curly hair if you want to switch up your style. However, it's important to use heat protectant products and avoid excessive heat to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my thick curly hair? A: Washing frequency depends on your hair type and personal preference.
