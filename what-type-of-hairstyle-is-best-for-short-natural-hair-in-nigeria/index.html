The History and Myth of What Type of Hairstyle is Best for Short Natural Hair in Nigeria

In Nigeria, natural hair has a rich history and is deeply rooted in cultural identity. Traditional hairstyles like the Bantu knots, Fulani braids, and Didi hairstyles have been passed down through generations. These hairstyles not only showcase the beauty of natural hair but also serve as a form of self-expression and cultural pride.

However, there are also myths surrounding natural hair in Nigeria. Some people believe that natural hair is unprofessional or unkempt, leading many women to resort to chemical relaxers or weaves to conform to societal beauty standards. It's essential to challenge these myths and embrace the beauty of our natural hair.

The Hidden Secret of What Type of Hairstyle is Best for Short Natural Hair in Nigeria
