Which Hairstyle Suits For Thick Hair 2023 Guide

Written by Michael Dec 23, 2022 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While you have an abundance of hair to work with, finding the right hairstyle that suits your thick locks can be a challenge. In this guide, we will explore the best hairstyles for thick hair in 2023, helping you embrace your natural volume and achieve a stylish and manageable look.

1. Layered Haircuts

Layered haircuts are perfect for thick hair as they help reduce the bulk and weight while adding movement and texture. Opt for long layers for a softer look or short layers for a more edgy style. This versatile haircut suits various face shapes and can be customized to your preferences.

2. Bob Hairstyles

A bob haircut is a timeless choice for thick hair. Whether you prefer a classic bob or a more modern variation like an inverted or stacked bob, this hairstyle provides structure and shape to your thick locks. Bobs are low maintenance and can be styled in various ways, making them a popular choice among women with thick hair.

3. Pixie Cuts

If you're feeling bold and want to embrace a shorter hairstyle, a pixie cut is an excellent option for thick hair. This hairstyle adds volume and dimension while keeping your hair manageable. You can opt for a textured pixie cut or a sleek and polished look, depending on your personal style.

4. Shoulder-Length Hairstyles

Shoulder-length hairstyles are a great choice for thick hair as they provide a balance between length and manageability. Whether you prefer a blunt cut or layered ends, this hairstyle allows you to showcase your thick locks without overwhelming your face. Add some face-framing highlights or layers for added dimension.

5. Updos and Ponytails

Thick hair is perfect for creating elegant updos and voluminous ponytails. Experiment with various updo styles like braids, buns, or twists to showcase the fullness of your hair. A high ponytail with some loose tendrils or a messy bun can instantly elevate your look and add a touch of sophistication.

6. Beachy Waves

For a relaxed and effortless look, beachy waves are an ideal choice for thick hair. This hairstyle adds movement and texture, making your hair appear voluminous and full of life. You can achieve beachy waves using a curling iron or by braiding your damp hair overnight for a more natural look.

7. Bangs

Bangs are a great way to change up your look and suit various face shapes. For thick hair, opt for side-swept bangs or curtain bangs that blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair. Bangs can help frame your face and add a touch of style without sacrificing the volume of your thick locks.

8. Half-Up Half-Down Hairstyles

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a perfect choice for thick hair as it allows you to showcase your natural volume while keeping your hair off your face. You can experiment with braids, twists, or top knots to create different half-up half-down looks that suit various occasions.

9. Shag Haircuts

A shag haircut is a trendy choice for thick hair in 2023. This hairstyle features layers throughout the hair, creating a textured and tousled look. Shag haircuts add movement and dimension to your locks, making them perfect for showcasing the natural volume of thick hair.

10. Sleek and Straight

If you prefer a sleek and polished look, straightening your thick hair can give you a sophisticated and elegant appearance. Use a high-quality flat iron and a heat protectant to achieve a smooth and straight finish. You can also add some shine serum or hair oil to enhance the sleekness of your hair.

Conclusion

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for thick hair, the options are endless. Whether you prefer a short pixie cut, a long layered style, or an elegant updo, embracing your natural volume can lead to stunning and versatile looks. Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits your personal style and enhances the beauty of your thick hair.
