Best Long Hairstyles for 60 Year Old Woman: How To

If you're interested in trying out a long hairstyle, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture, then apply a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to the roots.
  3. Using a round brush, blow dry your hair while lifting the roots to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to the ends for a more youthful and dynamic look.
  5. Finish off the look with a spritz of hairspray to hold everything in place.
