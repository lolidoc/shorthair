2. Bob

A bob is another excellent choice for fine wavy hair and an oval face. It adds volume and body to your hair while also creating a sleek and polished look. 3. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling adventurous, a pixie cut can be a great choice for fine wavy hair and an oval face. It adds volume and texture to your hair while also creating a bold and edgy look. A pixie cut can be styled in various ways, from tousled and messy to sleek and polished. It's a versatile haircut that allows you to play with different styles and looks.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Adding side-swept bangs to your haircut is a great way to change up your look and enhance your natural waves. Side-swept bangs create a soft and feminine frame for your face and bring out your best features. They also add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous. To style side-swept bangs, you can blow dry them with a round brush or use a flat iron for a sleek and polished finish.
