So, how exactly do you cut your own ladies short hair at home? The first step is to gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp haircutting shears, a comb, and a handheld mirror. It's also helpful to have some hair clips or hair ties to help section off your hair. Once you have your tools ready, you can start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. This will make it easier to see and work with.

Now that your hair is clean and dry, it's time to start cutting. The key to a successful DIY haircut is to take it slow and work in small sections. Start by combing your hair straight down and using the comb to create a clean, horizontal parting. Use your fingers or a hair clip to hold the rest of your hair out of the way. Then, using the handheld mirror, carefully trim the hair below the parting to your desired length. Remember to cut small amounts at a time and regularly check your progress in the mirror.
