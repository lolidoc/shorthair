Recommendation of How to Put Long Thick Hair Up

When it comes to putting long thick hair up, there are several hairstyles that work well for this hair type. Here are some recommendations:

1. The Classic Bun: This is a timeless hairstyle that works well for long thick hair. Simply gather your hair into a ponytail, twist it around its base, and secure it with bobby pins.

2. The French Braid: This is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for both casual and formal occasions. Start by dividing your hair into three sections and cross the outer sections over the middle section, adding in more hair as you go.

3. The Half-Up Half-Down: This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep some of their hair down while still putting it up. Simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip, leaving the rest of your hair down.
