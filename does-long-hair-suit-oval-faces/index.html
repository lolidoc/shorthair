Recommendations for long hair suiting oval faces

If you have an oval face and are considering growing your hair out, here are some recommendations to keep in mind:

1. Consider face-framing layers:

Face-framing layers can help to accentuate your features and add movement to your hair. Opt for soft, blended layers that start around the cheekbones and frame your face.

2. Experiment with different partings:

Changing your parting can instantly transform your look and highlight different aspects of your face. Try a deep side part for a dramatic effect or a middle part for a more symmetrical look.

3. Embrace natural texture:

Oval faces are well-suited to a variety of hair textures, so embrace your natural texture and work with it. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, let your natural texture shine through.

4. Don't be afraid to try new styles:

Long hair offers endless possibilities when it comes to styling. Don't be afraid to try new styles and experiment with different looks. From braids to updos, the options are limitless.
