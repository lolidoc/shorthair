How to Point Cut Thick Hair

Point cutting thick hair requires precision and skill. Here are some steps to follow when point cutting thick hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see the natural texture and movement of the hair.
  2. Section off the hair into manageable sections. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any areas.
  3. Hold the scissors at a slight angle and cut into the hair at different lengths. This will create texture and movement.
  4. Continue point cutting throughout the hair, making sure to cut at different angles and lengths.
  5. Once you have finished point cutting, style your hair as desired. You can use styling products to enhance the texture and movement created by the point cutting technique.

What If Point Cutting is Not Good for Thick Hair?

If point cutting is not good for your thick hair, there are other cutting techniques that may work better for you. These include:

  • Blunt cutting: This technique involves cutting the hair straight across, creating a blunt and uniform look.
  • Layering: This technique involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create layers and add dimension.
  • Thinning shears: This tool can be used to remove bulk and reduce the weight of thick hair.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I get a point cut?

A: The frequency of point cuts will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. On average, it is recommended to get a point cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the desired shape and texture.

Q: Can point cutting make my hair look thinner?

A: While point cutting can remove bulk and reduce the weight of the hair, it does not actually make the hair thinner. Instead, it creates texture and movement, making the hair look more lively and vibrant.

Q: Can I point cut my own hair at home?

A: It is generally not recommended to point cut your own hair at home, especially if you are not experienced with this cutting technique. It is best to consult with a professional hairstylist who has experience with point cutting to ensure the best results.

Q: Can I use point cutting on any length of hair?

A: Yes, point cutting can be done on any length of hair, from short to long. It can be used to add texture and movement to a pixie cut or to create layers and dimension in long hair.
