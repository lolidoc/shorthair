Introduction

Having thick wavy hair can be a blessing, but it can also be a challenge to find the right hairstyle that suits your hair type. Medium length hairstyles are a great option for those with thick wavy hair as they provide a balance between manageable length and enough weight to tame the waves. In this article, we will explore some of the best medium length hairstyles for thick wavy hair in 2023.

The Bob

One of the most popular medium length hairstyles for thick wavy hair is the bob. This classic haircut is versatile and can be tailored to suit different face shapes and hair textures. For thick wavy hair, a layered bob can add movement and reduce bulkiness. You can opt for a chin-length bob or go slightly longer for a more dramatic look.

The Shag

If you're looking for a hairstyle that embraces the natural texture of your thick wavy hair, the shag is a great choice. This trendy hairstyle is all about layers and choppy ends, which work well with the volume of your hair. The shag can be styled in various ways, from messy and tousled to sleek and polished, making it a versatile option for any occasion.

The Lob

The lob, or long bob, is another fantastic option for those with thick wavy hair. This hairstyle falls just above the shoulders and offers a chic and sophisticated look. The lob works well with thick wavy hair as it provides enough length to showcase the natural waves while still being manageable. You can add layers to add movement and dimension to your hair.

Layered Hairstyles

Layered hairstyles are ideal for thick wavy hair as they help to reduce bulkiness and add movement. You can opt for long layers or short layers, depending on your preference and face shape. Layered hairstyles can be styled in various ways, from loose beachy waves to sleek straight hair, giving you the flexibility to switch up your look.

Updos and Half-Up Styles

When it comes to special occasions or simply wanting to switch up your everyday look, updos and half-up styles are a great option for those with thick wavy hair. Updos can range from elegant chignons to messy buns, while half-up styles offer a combination of sophistication and playfulness. These hairstyles allow you to showcase your natural waves while keeping your hair out of your face.

Conclusion

Choosing the right medium length hairstyle for your thick wavy hair can enhance your natural beauty and make styling a breeze. Whether you opt for a bob, shag, lob, layered hairstyle, or an updo, there are plenty of options to suit your preferences and lifestyle. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
