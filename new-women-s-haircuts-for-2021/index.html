New Women's Haircuts for 2021: The Layered Shag

The layered shag haircut is one of the hottest trends for women in 2021. This haircut features layers throughout the hair, creating a textured and voluminous look. The shag haircut is perfect for women with medium to long hair and works well with all hair textures.

The layered shag haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can wear it straight for a sleek and polished look, or you can add some waves or curls for a more relaxed and beachy vibe. This haircut is low-maintenance and easy to style, making it a great option for busy women.

The layered shag haircut has a long history and has been popular since the 1970s. It is often associated with rock and roll and has a cool and edgy vibe. Many celebrities, such as Jane Fonda and Farrah Fawcett, have sported this iconic haircut.

One hidden secret of the layered shag haircut is that it can help to add volume and texture to fine or thin hair. The layers create movement and body, making your hair appear fuller and thicker. This is why the layered shag haircut is a popular choice for women with fine or thin hair.

If you're considering getting a layered shag haircut, I highly recommend consulting with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine if this haircut is right for you and give you personalized advice on how to style and maintain your new look.

New Women's Haircuts for 2021: The Bob

The bob haircut is a classic and timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. In 2021, the bob is experiencing a resurgence in popularity, with many women opting for shorter and more modern versions of this iconic haircut.

The bob haircut is versatile and can be customized to suit your individual style and preferences. You can choose a blunt bob for a sleek and polished look, or you can opt for a layered bob for added texture and movement. The bob works well with all face shapes and hair textures, making it a versatile and flattering choice.

One fun fact about the bob haircut is that it was first popularized in the 1920s by the iconic flapper girls. This short and chic hairstyle became a symbol of the Roaring Twenties and the liberation of women. Since then, the bob has remained a popular choice for women who want a stylish and low-maintenance haircut.

If you're thinking about getting a bob haircut, I recommend considering your face shape and hair texture. A professional hairstylist can help you determine the best length and style of bob for your individual features. They can also give you tips on how to style and maintain your new haircut.

New Women's Haircuts for 2021: The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and daring haircut that is perfect for women who want to make a statement. This short and chic hairstyle is characterized by its cropped length and textured layers. The pixie cut works well with all face shapes and hair textures.

If you're considering getting a pixie cut, it's important to understand that this haircut requires regular maintenance. You'll need to visit the salon every 4-6 weeks for trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and stylish. Additionally, you may need to invest in styling products to help you achieve your desired look.

One tip for styling a pixie cut is to use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and texture to your hair. This will help to enhance the layers and create a more edgy and voluminous look. You can also experiment with different styling techniques, such as tousling your hair with your fingers or using a flat iron to create sleek and smooth strands.
