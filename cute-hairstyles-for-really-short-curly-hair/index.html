Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair

If you have short curly hair, you know that finding cute and manageable hairstyles can be a challenge. But fear not, because we've got you covered! In this article, we will share some tips and ideas for cute hairstyles for really short curly hair that will make you look fabulous.

1. Embrace your natural texture

One of the best things about having short curly hair is that you can embrace your natural texture. Instead of trying to straighten or tame your curls, let them shine! Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and add some volume.

2. Pixie cut with curly bangs

A pixie cut is a great option for short curly hair. Add some curly bangs to soften the look and give it a playful touch. This hairstyle is low-maintenance and perfect for those who want to rock a short and sassy look.

3. Curly bob

If you want a classic and timeless look, go for a curly bob. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in various ways. You can wear it sleek and polished for a formal event or tousled and messy for a casual day out.

4. Half-up half-down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still showing off your curls. Gather the top section of your hair and secure it with a cute hair accessory or a bobby pin. This hairstyle is simple yet stylish.

5. Curly ponytail

A curly ponytail is a quick and easy hairstyle that looks effortlessly chic. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and let your curls cascade down. You can also wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to add a polished touch.

6. Side swept curls

If you want to add some drama to your short curly hair, try a side swept hairstyle. Part your hair to one side and let your curls fall naturally. This hairstyle is perfect for a night out or a special occasion.

7. Curly updo

For a more formal look, consider a curly updo. Gather your hair into a loose bun or twist and secure it with bobby pins. Leave a few curls loose to frame your face. This hairstyle is elegant and sophisticated.

8. Accessorize with a headband

Add some flair to your short curly hair by accessorizing with a headband. Choose a headband that complements your outfit and adds a pop of color or texture to your hairstyle. This is a simple and stylish way to switch up your look.

9. Curly faux hawk

If you're feeling bold and adventurous, try a curly faux hawk. This edgy hairstyle is created by pinning the sides of your hair towards the center, creating a faux hawk shape. Use bobby pins to secure the style and add some hairspray for extra hold.

10. Curly side braid

A curly side braid is a romantic and feminine hairstyle that works well with short curly hair. Sweep your hair to one side and create a loose braid. Pull out a few strands to frame your face and add some softness to the look.

With these cute hairstyles for really short curly hair, you can embrace your natural texture and rock a fabulous look. Experiment with different styles and find the ones that make you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to use products that enhance your curls and keep them hydrated for the best results. Have fun styling your short curly hair!
