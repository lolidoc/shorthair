What Are Short Bangs and How Do They Look Good on Round Faces

Short bangs refer to bangs that are cut above the eyebrows, typically ending just below the hairline. They can be blunt or textured, depending on the desired look. On round faces, short bangs create the illusion of length and add angles to the face, helping to balance out the roundness.

When styled correctly, short bangs can frame the face, draw attention to the eyes, and create a trendy and fashion-forward look. They can also be versatile, allowing for different styling options such as side-swept bangs or a center part.

The History and Myth of Short Bangs Looking Good on Round Faces

Short bangs have been a popular hairstyle choice for centuries, with various cultures and time periods embracing this look. In ancient Egypt, both men and women wore short bangs as a symbol of beauty and status. In the 1920s, the flapper style popularized short bangs, and they have continued to be a fashionable choice ever since.

There is a common myth that short bangs should be avoided by those with round faces. However, this myth is unfounded. While it's true that not all bangs styles suit round faces, short bangs can actually be flattering if chosen and styled correctly.

The Hidden Secret of Short Bangs Looking Good on Round Faces

The secret to making short bangs look good on round faces lies in finding the right length and texture. A slightly longer bang that ends just below the hairline can help to elongate the face and create a more angular appearance. Textured bangs can also add dimension and movement, making them a great choice for round faces.

Another secret is to style the bangs with a slight side-sweep or a center part. This helps to create the illusion of length and draws attention away from the roundness of the face. Adding volume to the crown of the head can also help to balance out the proportions and create a more flattering look.
