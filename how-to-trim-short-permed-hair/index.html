How to Trim Short Permed Hair

When it comes to trimming short permed hair, it is important to follow the proper steps to achieve the best results. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to trim short permed hair:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you begin trimming your short permed hair, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will allow you to get a more accurate cut and prevent any damage to your hair.

Step 2: Section Off Your Hair

Next, section off your hair into manageable sections and secure them with clips. This will make it easier to trim your hair evenly and prevent any missed spots.

Step 3: Trim Small Sections at a Time

Using sharp hairdressing scissors, trim small sections of hair at a time. It is important to only trim a small amount at a time to avoid cutting off too much hair. Start with the longer sections and work your way up to the shorter sections.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After trimming each section, check for evenness by combing through your hair and comparing the lengths. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and trim them until you achieve a balanced look.

Step 5: Finish with Styling

Once you have finished trimming your short permed hair, style it as desired. You can use a straightening iron or curling wand to add more definition to your curls or use a styling product to control frizz.

By following these steps and practicing regularly, you will become more confident in your ability to trim your own short permed hair and maintain a stunning style.
