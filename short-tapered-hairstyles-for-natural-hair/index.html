Short Tapered Hairstyles For Natural Hair

Written by Michael Jan 04, 2023 · 5 min read

Short tapered hairstyles for natural hair have become increasingly popular in recent years These versatile and stylish haircuts are perfect for those who want a low maintenance hairstyle that still looks chic and trendy Whether you have curly coily or kinky hair a short tapered cut can enhance your natural texture and bring out your unique beauty In this article we will explore some of the best short tapered hairstyles for natural hair in 2023

Table of Contents

Introduction

Short tapered hairstyles for natural hair have become increasingly popular in recent years. These versatile and stylish haircuts are perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks chic and trendy. Whether you have curly, coily, or kinky hair, a short tapered cut can enhance your natural texture and bring out your unique beauty. In this article, we will explore some of the best short tapered hairstyles for natural hair in 2023.

1. The Classic Tapered Cut

The classic tapered cut is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. This cut features short sides and a slightly longer length on top, creating a tapered effect. It is a great option for those who want a versatile and easy-to-maintain hairstyle. You can style the top in various ways, such as wearing it curly, straight, or in a twist-out.

2. The Textured Tapered Cut

If you want to enhance your natural texture, the textured tapered cut is the perfect choice. This cut adds more definition and volume to your curls or coils, making them the focal point of your hairstyle. To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist to add layers and texture to the top while keeping the sides short.

3. The Frohawk

The frohawk is a fun and edgy hairstyle that combines the elements of a mohawk and an afro. To create this look, the sides are shaved or cut short, while the center is left longer and styled into a faux hawk. This style allows you to showcase your natural hair while adding a touch of sassiness to your overall look.

4. The Buzz Cut

If you're looking for a bold and daring hairstyle, the buzz cut is an excellent option. This ultra-short style requires little to no maintenance and can be a liberating experience. The buzz cut accentuates your facial features and highlights your natural beauty. It's a perfect choice for those who want to make a statement with their natural hair.

5. The Asymmetrical Tapered Cut

The asymmetrical tapered cut is a modern and stylish take on the classic tapered cut. This hairstyle features one side of the hair cut shorter than the other, creating an asymmetrical look. It adds a touch of uniqueness and flair to your overall appearance. You can also experiment with different lengths and textures on each side to make this style truly your own.

6. The Mohawk

The mohawk is a bold and fierce hairstyle that never fails to make a statement. This style involves shaving or cutting the sides short while leaving a strip of longer hair in the center. You can style the longer hair in various ways, such as wearing it curly, braided, or straightened. The mohawk allows you to showcase your natural hair while embracing your inner rebel.

7. The Twist-Out Tapered Cut

The twist-out tapered cut is a versatile and elegant hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. This style involves twisting small sections of the hair and leaving them overnight to create defined curls or coils. The next day, you can unravel the twists and enjoy a beautiful twist-out. The tapered cut enhances the shape and volume of the twist-out, giving you a stunning and effortless look.

8. The Curly Pixie Cut

The curly pixie cut is a chic and feminine hairstyle that is easy to maintain. This cut features short sides and a slightly longer length on top, creating a pixie-like effect. It allows you to embrace your natural curls while showcasing your facial features. You can also add some styling products to enhance the definition and shine of your curls.

9. The Faux Locs Tapered Cut

If you want to try something different and unique, the faux locs tapered cut is a fantastic choice. This style involves creating faux locs on the top while keeping the sides short. It gives you the look of real locs without the commitment. The faux locs add texture and dimension to your hairstyle, making it a head-turning look.

10. The Bantu Knot-Out Tapered Cut

The Bantu knot-out tapered cut is a playful and stylish hairstyle that is perfect for those with natural hair. This style involves twisting small sections of the hair into Bantu knots and leaving them overnight. The next day, you can unravel the knots and enjoy defined curls or coils. The tapered cut enhances the shape and volume of the Bantu knot-out, creating a stunning and eye-catching look.

Conclusion

Short tapered hairstyles for natural hair offer a wide range of options to suit every individual's style and preference. Whether you prefer a classic, textured, or edgy look, there is a short tapered cut that can enhance your natural beauty. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and empowered. Embrace your natural hair and rock your short tapered cut with pride!
