Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair and Square Face: Exploring the Options

When it comes to short hairstyles for thick hair and square face shapes, there are several options to consider. Let's take a closer look at some of the most popular styles:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for thick hair and square face shapes. This style involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving a bit more length on top. The longer layers on top help to soften the angles of the face and create a more feminine look. This hairstyle is low-maintenance and perfect for those who want a bold and edgy look.

2. Bob

The bob is another versatile option for those with thick hair and square face shapes. This hairstyle involves cutting the hair to chin-length or slightly longer, with or without layers. The bob helps to frame the face and draw attention to your best features. It can be styled straight, wavy, or with curls, depending on your preference. This hairstyle is chic, timeless, and perfect for those who want a more sophisticated look.

3. Lob

If you're not ready to fully commit to a short hairstyle, the lob is a great choice. The lob, or long bob, is a slightly longer version of the bob and falls just above the shoulders. This hairstyle is incredibly versatile and can be styled in many different ways. The lob helps to create balance and soften the angles of the face, making it a great option for those with square face shapes. Whether you prefer sleek and straight or tousled and wavy, the lob is a stylish and trendy option.

4. Shag Cut

The shag cut is a fun and edgy hairstyle that works well for those with thick hair and square face shapes. This style involves cutting the hair into layers of varying lengths, creating a textured and tousled look. The shag cut adds movement and dimension to your hair, and the layers help to soften the angles of the face. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a bold and unique look.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I pull off a pixie cut with thick hair and a square face shape?

A: Absolutely! The pixie cut is a great option for those with thick hair and square face shapes. The shorter length helps to soften the angles of the face and draw attention to your best features. Talk to your hairstylist to find the perfect pixie cut that flatters your face shape.

Q: Will a bob make my face look more angular?

A: Not necessarily. The bob is a versatile hairstyle that can be tailored to your face shape. Adding layers or styling the hair in a certain way can help to soften the angles of the face and create a more flattering look.

Q: How do I style a lob for a square face shape?

A: Styling a lob for a square face shape is all about creating balance. Adding soft waves or curls can help to soften the angles of the face, while straightening the hair can create a sleek and sophisticated look. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that suits you best.

Q: Will a shag cut work for thick hair?

A: Yes! The shag cut is a great option for those with thick hair. The layers help to add movement and texture to your hair, making it easier to style and manage. Talk to your hairstylist to find the perfect shag cut for your thick hair and square face shape.
