Teenagers in the punk scene embraced ripped clothing, leather jackets, and safety pin accessories as a way of expressing their individuality and challenging societal norms.

In the 1990s, the rise of Britpop brought a new wave of fashion trends to the UK. Bands such as Oasis and Blur influenced the fashion choices of many teenagers, with parka jackets, bucket hats, and platform shoes becoming popular.
