Hairstyles For Guys With Short Thick Hair

Table of Contents

Introduction

Are you a guy with short and thick hair? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will explore some trendy and stylish hairstyles that are perfect for guys with short thick hair. Whether you want a sleek and sophisticated look or a more rugged and casual style, there's something here for everyone. So, let's dive in and find the perfect hairstyle for you!

The Classic Pompadour

The classic pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. It works exceptionally well for guys with short and thick hair, as it adds height and volume to the crown. To achieve this look, you'll need to have your hair cut short on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. Use a strong-hold pomade or wax to style your hair upwards and slightly back, creating a pompadour effect.

The Textured Crop

If you prefer a more contemporary and low-maintenance hairstyle, the textured crop is an excellent choice. This hairstyle involves cutting your hair short all around and adding texture to the top. You can achieve this texture by using a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray. Simply apply the product to your damp hair and use your fingers to create a tousled and textured look.

The Undercut

The undercut is a popular hairstyle that works well for guys with short thick hair. This style involves shaving or cutting the sides and back very short while leaving the top longer. The contrast between the shorter sides and the longer top creates a stylish and edgy look. You can style the top in various ways, such as slicking it back or creating a messy, textured look.

The Buzz Cut

If you're looking for a no-fuss and low-maintenance hairstyle, the buzz cut is the way to go. This hairstyle involves cutting your hair very short all over, creating a uniform and clean look. The buzz cut is perfect for guys with short thick hair, as it showcases the natural thickness and texture of the hair. Plus, it requires minimal styling and maintenance.

The Quiff

The quiff is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that can be adapted to suit different hair types, including short thick hair. To achieve the quiff, you'll need to have your hair cut shorter on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. Use a styling product with strong hold to create height and volume at the front of your hair, sweeping it upwards and slightly back.

The Messy Bedhead

If you prefer a more effortless and relaxed look, the messy bedhead hairstyle is perfect for you. This style involves embracing the natural thickness and texture of your hair by keeping it slightly longer all around. Simply apply a small amount of styling product, such as a matte paste or clay, to your hair and tousle it with your fingers to create a messy and undone look.

The Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a bold and trendy hairstyle that can add an element of edginess to your look. This style involves keeping the hair shorter on the sides and longer in the middle. Use a strong-hold product to push the hair in the middle upwards, creating a mohawk-like effect. The faux hawk is a great option for guys with short thick hair, as it adds height and dimension to the hair.

The Side Part

The side part is a classic and sophisticated hairstyle that can elevate your overall appearance. This style involves parting your hair on one side and keeping it shorter on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. Use a comb and some styling product to create a clean and defined part, sweeping the hair to one side. The side part is perfect for formal occasions and professional settings.

The Taper Fade

The taper fade is a popular hairstyle that involves gradually fading the hair from short to shorter or bald on the sides and back. This style works well for guys with short thick hair, as it adds a modern and stylish touch to the overall look. You can pair the taper fade with various hairstyles on top, such as a pompadour or a textured crop, to create a unique and personalized style.

Conclusion

Having short thick hair doesn't mean you have limited hairstyle options. There are plenty of trendy and stylish hairstyles that can enhance your overall look. From the classic pompadour to the textured crop and the side part to the taper fade, there's a hairstyle for every preference and occasion. Experiment with different styles and find the one that suits your personality and lifestyle the best. Remember to use the right styling products and techniques to achieve the desired look and maintain your hairstyle throughout the day. Now go ahead and rock your short thick hair with confidence!
