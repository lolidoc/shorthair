60 Year Old Short Haircuts: How to Style

Styling your 60 year old short haircut can be quick and easy. Here are some tips on how to style your short haircut:

- Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, use a towel to gently blot out excess moisture.

- Apply a styling product to your hair. This can be a mousse, gel, or styling cream, depending on your hair type and desired look.

- Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly through your hair.

- Use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.

- If desired, use a curling iron or flat iron to add some waves or curls to your hair.

- Finish off with a hairspray to set your style and add some hold.
