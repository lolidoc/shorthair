Short Hairstyles For Naturally Curly Hair Over 70

As we age our hair naturally changes and for those with naturally curly hair over 70 finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge However there are plenty of short hairstyles that can enhance your natural curls and make you look fabulous In this article we will explore some of the best short hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 70

Table of Contents

Introduction

As we age, our hair naturally changes, and for those with naturally curly hair over 70, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. However, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can enhance your natural curls and make you look fabulous. In this article, we will explore some of the best short hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 70.

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well with naturally curly hair. It is low maintenance and can give you a youthful and stylish look. The short length helps to enhance your natural curls and adds volume to your hair. You can also add some layers to create more texture and movement.

2. Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is another great option for those with naturally curly hair over 70. This hairstyle is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. The bangs can help to frame your face and create a more youthful appearance. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob depending on your preference.

3. Curly Shag

A curly shag is a trendy and fun hairstyle that can add a modern touch to your look. This hairstyle works well with naturally curly hair and can help to enhance your curls and add volume. The layers in the shag haircut can also help to reduce the weight of your hair and make it easier to manage.

4. Short Afro

If you have naturally tight curls, embracing your natural texture with a short afro can be a great option. This hairstyle is bold and fashionable and can give you a confident and stylish look. With the right products and styling techniques, you can keep your afro looking voluminous and well-defined.

5. Curly Mohawk

A curly mohawk is a bold and edgy hairstyle that can make a statement. This hairstyle involves shaving or cutting the sides of your hair short while leaving the center section longer. You can enhance your natural curls in the center section and style it in various ways, such as by adding volume or creating twists.

6. Tapered Cut

A tapered cut is a short hairstyle that involves gradually cutting the hair shorter towards the nape of the neck. This style works well with naturally curly hair and can provide a neat and polished look. The tapered cut can help to enhance your curls and create a more defined shape.

7. Curly Bob

A curly bob is a timeless and elegant hairstyle that can work well for those with naturally curly hair over 70. This hairstyle is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. The bob can be cut to chin-length or slightly longer, and the curls can be enhanced with the right styling products.

8. Side-Swept Curls

If you prefer to keep your hair longer, you can opt for side-swept curls. This hairstyle involves parting your hair to the side and sweeping the curls to one side. This creates a soft and romantic look and can help to frame your face. You can also add some layers to add movement and texture to your curls.

9. Curly Updo

For special occasions or when you want to change up your look, a curly updo can be a great choice. This hairstyle involves pulling your hair up and securing it in an elegant and stylish updo. You can leave some curls loose to frame your face and add a soft touch to the overall look.

10. Half Up, Half Down

If you want to show off your curls while keeping your hair away from your face, a half up, half down hairstyle is a great option. This style involves pulling the top half of your hair up and leaving the bottom half down. You can secure it with a clip or create a small bun at the crown of your head.

Conclusion

Getting older doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style. With the right short hairstyle for naturally curly hair over 70, you can embrace your natural texture and look fabulous. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, bob, shag, or afro, there are plenty of options to suit your personal style and enhance your natural beauty. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
