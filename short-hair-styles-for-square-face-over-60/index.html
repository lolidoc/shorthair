Tips for Maintaining Short Hair Styles for Square Face Over 60

Once you've found the perfect short hair style for your square face over 60, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hair style:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for aging hair. These products will nourish and hydrate your hair, keeping it healthy and shiny.

- Avoid excessive heat styling, as this can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle. Instead, opt for air-drying or using low heat settings on your styling tools.

- Regularly trim your hair to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. A short hair style should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and vibrant.

- Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and desired look. A mousse or volumizing spray can add lift and body to your hair, while a smoothing serum can tame frizz and flyaways.

How to Style Short Hair Styles for Square Face Over 60

Styling short hair is quick and easy, especially if you choose a low-maintenance style. Here are some simple steps to style your short hair:

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or gel, to add volume and hold to your hair.

2. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to lift and shape your hair. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends, using the brush to add volume and create movement.

3. If desired, use a flat iron or curling wand to add texture or create waves. This will give your hair a more polished and finished look.

4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place without weighing it down.
