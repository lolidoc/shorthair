Long layers can be worn straight and sleek, or styled with loose waves for a more relaxed and bohemian feel. They can also be easily maintained and styled, making them a great choice for women who prefer a low-maintenance option.

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I'm over 60 with thick hair?

Q: What products should I use on my thick hair?

A: When it comes to styling thick hair, it's important to use products that will help to control and manage your hair without weighing it down. Look for lightweight products that are designed for thick or coarse hair, such as volumizing mousses, texturizing sprays, and lightweight oils. Q: How often should I cut my thick hair?

A: The frequency of haircuts will depend on your individual hair growth and style preferences. However, most hairstylists recommend getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the health and shape of your hair. Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage, and can also help to keep your hair looking fresh and polished.
