Short Hairstyles for Boys/Kids 2021: The Classic Crew Cut

The classic crew cut is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is characterized by short hair on the sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. The crew cut is perfect for boys/kids with straight or wavy hair, as it adds structure and definition to their hair. It is also easy to maintain and can be styled with a little bit of gel or pomade for a polished look.

Short Hairstyles for Boys/Kids 2021: The Modern Pompadour

The modern pompadour is a trendy and stylish short hairstyle for boys/kids in 2021. This style features short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that is styled to create height and volume. The modern pompadour can be customized to suit different hair types and face shapes. It can be styled with a matte pomade or wax for a textured and edgy look. This hairstyle is perfect for boys/kids who want to make a statement and stand out from the crowd.

Short Hairstyles for Boys/Kids 2021: The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a popular short hairstyle for boys/kids in 2021. This style features short hair all over, with the hair on top styled to create texture and movement. The textured crop is perfect for boys/kids with thick and wavy hair, as it allows the natural texture of their hair to shine through. It can be styled with a little bit of texturizing spray or paste for a tousled and effortless look. This hairstyle is perfect for boys/kids who want a low-maintenance and stylish look.

Short Hairstyles for Boys/Kids 2021: The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic and timeless short hairstyle for boys/kids in 2021. This style features very short hair all over, giving a clean and polished look. The buzz cut is perfect for boys/kids who want a low-maintenance and fuss-free hairstyle. It is also a great option for boys/kids with thinning hair or receding hairlines, as it can create the illusion of thicker and fuller hair. This hairstyle is versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.
