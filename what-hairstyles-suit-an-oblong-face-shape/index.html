What Hairstyles Suit an Oblong Face Shape?

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face shape, there are a few factors to consider. The length, texture, and volume of the hair can all play a role in finding the perfect style. Here are some recommendations:

Short Hairstyles:

Short hairstyles can work well for oblong face shapes as they add width and balance to the face. Pixie cuts, layered bobs, or short shaggy hairstyles can all be flattering options. Adding texture or curls to short hair can also help create the illusion of width.

Medium Length Hairstyles:

Medium length hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides of the face can be a great choice for oblong face shapes. Consider shoulder-length cuts, layered styles, or beachy waves to add softness and balance.

Long Hairstyles:

For those with long hair, adding layers, waves, or curls can help break up the length of the face and add volume to the sides. Avoid straight, sleek styles that can accentuate the length of the face.

Bangs:

Bangs can be a game-changer for oblong face shapes. Adding bangs can help shorten the face and create the illusion of width. Consider side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, or wispy bangs to soften the forehead and balance out the face.

Updos and Ponytails:

When it comes to updos and ponytails, opt for styles that add volume and width to the sides of the face. Messy buns, voluminous ponytails, or half-up, half-down styles can all be flattering options for oblong face shapes.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have an oblong face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have an oblong face shape. However, it's important to consider adding volume and width to the sides of your face to balance out the length.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have an oblong face shape?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules, it's generally recommended to avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head, as this can elongate the face further. Additionally, straight, sleek styles without any volume or texture can accentuate the length of the face.

Q: Can bangs flatter an oblong face shape?

A: Yes, bangs can be a great way to flatter an oblong face shape. Side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, or wispy bangs can help shorten the face and create the illusion of width.

Q: How can I add volume to my hair if I have an oblong face shape?

A: To add volume to your hair if you have an oblong face shape, consider using volumizing products, such as mousse or dry shampoo. Additionally, adding layers or opting for styles with curls or waves can also help create the illusion of volume.
