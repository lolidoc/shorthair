How to Grow Hair Longer and Faster: Explained

So, how exactly does hair grow? Hair growth occurs in cycles, with each hair follicle going through a growth phase, rest phase, and shedding phase. The length of each phase can vary depending on several factors, including genetics and overall health.

During the growth phase, new hair cells are formed at the base of the hair follicle. These cells push older cells up through the follicle, where they eventually harden and form the hair shaft. The rate at which hair grows is determined by the rate of cell division at the base of the follicle.
