Are you looking to achieve that iconic 60s hairstyle? Teased hair was a popular trend in the 1960s and continues to be a favorite among fashion enthusiasts today. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of teasing your hair 60s style, so you can achieve that voluminous and glamorous look.

Teasing your hair can be a tricky task, especially if you're new to the technique. It may feel overwhelming to achieve the perfect amount of volume without making your hair look frizzy or tangled. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can master the art of teasing your hair and create a stunning 60s-inspired hairstyle.

To tease your hair 60s style, start by sectioning your hair and backcombing each section from the root to the mid-length. This will create the desired volume and texture. Then, gently smooth out the top layer of your hair to give it a polished finish. Finally, spritz some hairspray to hold the teased hair in place throughout the day.

In summary, to achieve a 60s-inspired teased hair look, follow these steps:

Step 1: Section your hair

Divide your hair into small sections, starting from the crown of your head. Use clips or hair ties to keep the sections separated.

Step 2: Backcomb each section

Take one section at a time and hold it taut. Using a fine-tooth comb, backcomb the hair from the root to the mid-length. Repeat this process for each section, focusing on the areas where you want to create volume.

Step 3: Smooth out the top layer

After teasing your hair, gently brush the top layer to smooth out any tangles or bumps. Be careful not to brush too aggressively, as this can undo the teasing. Use a boar bristle brush or a wide-tooth comb for best results.

Step 4: Set with hairspray

To ensure that your teased hair stays in place, spritz some hairspray all over your hair. This will provide hold and prevent the teased sections from falling flat throughout the day.

Tips for Teasing Hair 60s Style:

1. Use a rat-tail comb for precise backcombing.

2. Start with clean, dry hair for better hold.

3. Don't be afraid to tease vigorously for maximum volume.

4. Use hair accessories like headbands or scarves to enhance your 60s look.

Conclusion of how to tease hair 60s style:

Teasing your hair 60s style is a fun and fashionable way to add volume and texture to your hairstyle. By following the steps outlined above and incorporating some helpful tips, you can achieve a glamorous and retro look that pays homage to the iconic 60s era. So go ahead, grab your comb and hairspray, and embrace the teased hair trend!
