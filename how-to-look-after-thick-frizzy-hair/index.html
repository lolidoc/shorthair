Question and Answer: How to Look After Thick Frizzy Hair

Q: Can I use oils to moisturize my frizzy hair?

A: While oils can provide some moisture to the hair, it's important to use them sparingly and choose lightweight oils that won't weigh your hair down. Heavy oils can make your hair appear greasy and contribute to frizz.

Q: How often should I deep condition my thick frizzy hair?

A: It's recommended to deep condition your hair once a week or every two weeks, depending on the condition of your hair. This will help to nourish your hair and provide it with the moisture it needs to reduce frizz.

Q: Is it necessary to use a diffuser when air-drying my frizzy hair?

A: Using a diffuser can help to enhance your natural hair texture and minimize frizz. However, if you prefer to air-dry your hair without a diffuser, you can simply scrunch your hair with your hands while it's drying to encourage your natural curls or waves.
