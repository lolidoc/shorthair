Do you have thick curly hair that you struggle to wear up? If so, you're not alone. Many people with thick curly hair find it challenging to find hairstyles that work well with their natural texture. But don't worry, we're here to help! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for how to wear thick curly hair up, so you can rock a stylish updo with confidence.

One of the main pain points for people with thick curly hair is that it can be difficult to manage and style. The volume and texture of curly hair can make it hard to create sleek and polished updos. Additionally, curly hair tends to be more prone to frizz and can easily become tangled. These factors can make it frustrating to wear your hair up, especially for special occasions or formal events.

So, how can you wear your thick curly hair up in a way that looks stylish and keeps your curls under control? The key is to find hairstyles that work with your natural texture and embrace the volume and bounce of your curls. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect updo:

1. Use the right products

Before styling your hair, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that will help keep your curls hydrated and reduce frizz. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to define your curls and add extra moisture. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.

2. Start with a good foundation

When wearing your hair up, it's essential to start with a good foundation. This means having a well-prepared base that will help your updo stay in place all day. If your hair tends to be slippery or hard to manage, try using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add grip and hold. You can also use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your curls and create a sturdy base for your updo.

3. Choose the right hairstyle

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for your thick curly hair, the possibilities are endless. However, some styles tend to work better with curly hair than others. For a simple and elegant look, try a low bun or a side-swept updo. These styles will showcase your curls while keeping them off your face. If you're looking for a more glamorous option, consider a high ponytail or a half-up, half-down style. These hairstyles will add height and volume to your hair, creating a stunning look.

4. Embrace accessories

Accessorizing your updo can be a great way to add a personal touch and make your hairstyle stand out. Consider adding a sparkly hairpin or a decorative headband to your updo. These accessories will not only add a touch of glamour but also help to keep your curls in place. You can also experiment with different types of hair ties, such as scrunchies or silk ribbons, to create unique and stylish looks.

Conclusion

Wearing thick curly hair up can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a stunning updo that showcases your natural texture. By using the right products, starting with a good foundation, choosing the right hairstyle, and embracing accessories, you can create a stylish and sophisticated look that will turn heads wherever you go. So, embrace your curls and get ready to rock that updo!
