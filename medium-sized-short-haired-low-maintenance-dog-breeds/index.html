Medium-Sized Short-Haired Low Maintenance Dog Breeds and Exercise

Exercise is an important aspect of owning a dog, regardless of their size or coat type. While medium-sized short-haired low maintenance dog breeds have moderate exercise requirements, it is still important to provide them with regular physical and mental stimulation.

Aim for at least 30 minutes to an hour of exercise each day, depending on your dog's age and energy level. This can include walks, playtime in the backyard, or visits to the dog park. Additionally, mental stimulation is just as important as physical exercise. Consider incorporating puzzle toys, obedience training, or interactive games into your dog's daily routine to keep their minds sharp and engaged.
