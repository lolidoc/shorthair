Q: Can using the wrong comb cause damage to thin hair?

A: Yes, using the wrong comb can cause damage to thin hair. Combs with narrow teeth or harsh bristles can cause breakage and tangling, leading to further damage to already thin strands.

Q: How often should I comb my thin hair?

A: It is recommended to comb your thin hair gently once or twice a day. Be sure to start at the ends and work your way up to prevent unnecessary pulling and breakage.

Q: Can I use a brush instead of a comb for thin hair?

A: While brushes can be used on thin hair, combs are generally more gentle and less likely to cause breakage. If you prefer using a brush, opt for one with wide, flexible bristles to minimize damage.
