Short Hairstyles for Round Faces and Thick Hair Over 50

Introduction

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and lose its volume. If you have a round face and thick hair, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. However, there are plenty of stylish and flattering short hairstyles that can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident. In this article, we will explore some trendy and age-appropriate hairstyles for women over 50 with round faces and thick hair.

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can work wonders for women with round faces and thick hair. This short haircut adds dimension and texture, making your hair look voluminous and stylish. It also helps to elongate the face, giving the illusion of a slimmer appearance. Consider adding layers or side-swept bangs to enhance the overall look.

2. Bob Hairstyle

A bob hairstyle is another excellent choice for women over 50 with round faces and thick hair. Opt for a chin-length or slightly longer bob to create a more balanced and elongated look. This hairstyle can be customized with layers or bangs to suit your personal style. It's low-maintenance and can be easily styled for any occasion.

3. Layered Bob

If you want to add more movement and texture to your hair, consider a layered bob. This hairstyle is perfect for women with round faces and thick hair as it helps to create a flattering frame around the face. The layers add depth and dimension, making your hair appear lighter and more voluminous. You can also play with different lengths to find the perfect balance.

4. Shaggy Hairstyle

A shaggy hairstyle is a great option for women over 50 with round faces and thick hair. This edgy and modern haircut adds texture and movement to your hair. The layers are strategically placed to create a tousled and carefree look. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that can be effortlessly styled with some mousse or texturizing spray.

5. Short Curly Hair

If you have natural curls, embrace them and go for a short curly hairstyle. Curly hair adds volume and dimension, making it ideal for round faces and thick hair. Opt for a cut that falls just above the shoulders to prevent your hair from overpowering your face. Emphasize your curls with a curl-enhancing product for a stunning and youthful look.

6. Side-Swept Bangs

Adding side-swept bangs to any short hairstyle can instantly transform your look. Bangs help to create angles and draw attention away from the roundness of your face. They also add a touch of sophistication and can make you look more youthful. Whether you have a pixie cut, bob, or shaggy hairstyle, side-swept bangs can be a game-changer.

7. Soft Waves

Soft waves can add a touch of glamour and femininity to any short hairstyle. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves that frame your face. This styling technique can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face and add volume to your hair. Finish off with a lightweight hairspray to hold the waves in place throughout the day.

8. Highlighting and Lowlights

Adding highlights and lowlights to your hair can enhance the dimension and texture. Opt for subtle and natural-looking shades that complement your skin tone. Highlights can draw attention away from the roundness of your face, while lowlights can create depth and volume. Consult with a professional hairstylist to find the perfect color combination for your short hairstyle.

9. Accessorize

Don't be afraid to accessorize your short hairstyle. Headbands, clips, and hairpins can add a touch of style and personality to your look. Experiment with different accessories to find the ones that suit your taste and complement your hairstyle. They can also help to keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling flat.

10. Confidence is Key

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, remember that confidence is key. Embrace your natural beauty and rock your short hairstyle with pride. The right hairstyle can enhance your features and make you feel fabulous at any age. Be open to trying new styles and consult with a professional hairstylist who can guide you in finding the perfect short hairstyle for your round face and thick hair.
