So, how do you go about cutting the top of your hair yourself? Here's a step-by-step guide to get you started:

1. Start by dividing your hair into sections. Use hair clips or hair ties to separate the top section from the rest of your hair.

2. Dampen your hair slightly to make it easier to cut. Use a spray bottle filled with water or a leave-in conditioner.

3. Comb through the section of hair that you want to cut. Make sure it is free from tangles and knots.

4. Hold the hair between your fingers at the desired length. Be sure to leave some extra length to account for any mistakes.

5. Using sharp hair cutting scissors, trim the hair between your fingers in a straight line. Take your time and make small cuts.

6. Continue this process until you have cut the entire top section of your hair.

7. Once you have finished cutting, style your hair as desired. Use a blow dryer, straightener, or curling iron to achieve your desired look.
