Short Choppy Haircuts for Thick Wavy Hair: How to Achieve the Perfect Style

Achieving the perfect short choppy haircut for thick wavy hair starts with finding the right hairstylist. Look for someone who specializes in cutting and styling thick, wavy hair, as they will have the expertise and knowledge to create a haircut that suits your unique texture.

During your consultation, be sure to communicate your desired style and any specific concerns or preferences you may have. Your hairstylist can then work with you to determine the best haircut for your face shape and lifestyle.

Once you have your new haircut, styling your short choppy hair is all about enhancing your natural waves. Start by applying a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to damp hair, then scrunch the hair with your hands to encourage the waves. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry the hair, or let it air dry for a more natural look.

To enhance the texture and add definition to your waves, you can use a curling iron or wand to curl random sections of the hair. Alternatively, you can use a flat iron to create loose waves by twisting the iron as you glide it down the hair shaft.
