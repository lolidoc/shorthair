What Makeup to Wear with Grey Hair: How-To

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to create a makeup look for grey hair:

1. Start with a clean and moisturized face.

2. Choose a foundation shade that matches your skin tone and apply it evenly all over your face.

3. Apply a neutral eyeshadow shade on your eyelids and blend it outwards.

4. Line your upper lash line with a thin line of eyeliner.

5. Curl your lashes and apply mascara to add volume and length.

6. Use a cream blush to add a natural flush to your cheeks.

7. Finish off the look with a bold lipstick shade that complements your skin tone.
