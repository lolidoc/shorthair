Tips for Using the Best Conditioner for Hair over 60

Here are some tips for using the best conditioner for hair over 60:

1. Use a small amount of conditioner.

A little goes a long way when it comes to conditioner. Use a small amount and focus on the ends of your hair, where it tends to be the driest and most damaged.

2. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes.

Allow the conditioner to penetrate your hair by leaving it on for a few minutes before rinsing. This will help to maximize its benefits and leave your hair feeling soft and hydrated.

3. Rinse with cool water.

After conditioning, rinse your hair with cool water to help seal the cuticles and lock in moisture. This will also help to add shine to your hair.

4. Use a wide-tooth comb.

Gently detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb after conditioning to prevent breakage. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots.
