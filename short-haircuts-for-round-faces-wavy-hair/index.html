How to Style Short Haircuts for Round Faces with Wavy Hair

Styling short haircuts for round faces with wavy hair can be a breeze with the right tools and techniques. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

1. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from damage. This is especially important if you plan to use heat styling tools.

2. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair and remove any knots or tangles.

3. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or a curl-enhancing cream, to your damp hair. This will help to define your waves and add texture to your haircut.

4. Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves and prevent frizz. Focus on lifting the roots to create volume and shape.

5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or a curling wand to add more definition to your waves. Wrap small sections of hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

6. Finish off with a hairspray to set your style and keep your waves in place throughout the day.
