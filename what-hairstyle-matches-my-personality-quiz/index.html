What Hairstyle Matches My Personality - Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I determine my face shape?

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face using a washable marker or lipstick. Step back and observe the shape you've drawn to determine if it is round, oval, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped.

2. Can I change my hairstyle to match my personality?
