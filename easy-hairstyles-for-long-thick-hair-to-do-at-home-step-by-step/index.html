Easy Hairstyles for Long Thick Hair to Do at Home Step by Step: Sleek Ponytail

One of the easiest hairstyles for long thick hair is a sleek ponytail. This timeless look is perfect for any occasion and can be done in just a few simple steps.

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Then, gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. To make the ponytail look sleek and polished, take a small section of hair from the underside of the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band. Secure the end of the section with a bobby pin.

For added shine and hold, you can apply a small amount of hair gel or spray to smooth down any flyaways. And that's it! You now have a sleek and stylish ponytail that is perfect for any occasion.

Easy Hairstyles for Long Thick Hair to Do at Home Step by Step: Messy Bun

If you prefer a more relaxed and effortless look, a messy bun is the way to go. This hairstyle is great for those days when you want to look put together but don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair.

To create a messy bun, start by gathering your hair into a high ponytail and securing it with an elastic band. Then, twist the ponytail around the base to form a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Don't worry about making it perfect - the messier, the better!

You can also pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a soft and romantic look. And voila! You now have a stylish and effortless messy bun.

Easy Hairstyles for Long Thick Hair to Do at Home Step by Step: Half-Up Half-Down

If you want to show off your long, thick hair while keeping it out of your face, a half-up half-down hairstyle is the perfect choice. This versatile look can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

To create a half-up half-down hairstyle, start by gathering the top section of your hair and securing it with an elastic band or hair clip. You can leave it as is for a simple and casual look, or you can add some curls or waves for a more polished style.

For added flair, you can also braid the top section of your hair or twist it into a knot. The possibilities are endless! And there you have it - a chic and easy half-up half-down hairstyle for your long thick hair.

Whether you prefer a sleek ponytail, a messy bun, or a half-up half-down look, there is a hairstyle for every occasion.
