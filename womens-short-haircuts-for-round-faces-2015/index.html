Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the top womens short haircuts for round faces in 2015 and how they can enhance your features and boost your confidence.

Having a round face can sometimes make it challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your facial shape. Many women with round faces struggle with finding a haircut that adds angles and definition to their face, rather than accentuating its roundness. This can lead to frustration and a lack of confidence in one's appearance.

If you have a round face, the key to finding the right haircut is to create the illusion of length and angles. You want to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of your head while keeping the sides of your hair sleek and close to your face. By doing so, you can create the appearance of a more elongated face shape.

In summary, womens short haircuts for round faces in 2015 should aim to add height and volume to the crown of the head while keeping the sides sleek and close to the face. This creates the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape. Now, let's dive deeper into the topic and explore some specific hairstyles that are perfect for round faces.

Womens Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2015: The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic and timeless hairstyle that works well for women with round faces. It is short on the sides and back, while the top is left longer to add height and volume. This creates the illusion of a longer face shape and draws attention to the cheekbones and eyes. The pixie cut is low maintenance and versatile, allowing you to style it in various ways to suit your personal style.

Womens Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2015: The Bob

The bob is another great option for women with round faces. Opt for a chin-length or slightly longer bob that is angled to create the appearance of length and angles. By keeping the hair shorter in the back and longer in the front, you can add dimension and definition to your face shape. The bob is a versatile haircut that can be styled straight, wavy, or curly to suit your preferences.

Womens Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2015: The Shag

The shag haircut is a trendy and chic option for women with round faces. It is a layered haircut with feathered ends that adds texture and movement to the hair. The shag is perfect for adding volume to the crown of the head, creating the illusion of a longer face shape. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in various ways to suit your personal style.

Womens Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2015: The Pixie Bob

The pixie bob is a hybrid of the pixie cut and the bob, making it a perfect choice for women with round faces. It is shorter in the back and longer in the front, creating the illusion of length and angles. The pixie bob is a low maintenance and versatile haircut that can be styled in various ways to suit your personal style.

Womens Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2015 and Related Keywords

Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful!

Conclusion of Womens Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2015

Choosing the right haircut for your round face can make a world of difference in how you feel about your appearance. Question and Answer

Q: Can women with round faces pull off a short haircut?

A: Absolutely! Women with round faces can rock a short haircut as long as they choose a style that adds height and volume to the crown of the head while keeping the sides sleek and close to the face.

A: While there are no hard and fast rules, some short haircuts may not be as flattering for round faces. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face or make the face appear rounder.

A: Regular trims are essential for maintaining the shape and style of a short haircut. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
