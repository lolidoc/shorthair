How to Use the Best Flat Iron for Curling Fine Straight Hair

Using the best flat iron for curling fine, straight hair is relatively simple. Start by plugging in the flat iron and allowing it to heat up to the desired temperature. It's important to use the appropriate heat setting for your hair type to minimize damage.

Once the flat iron is heated, section your hair and start with a small section at a time. Clamp the flat iron at the roots of the hair and twist it as you glide it down the length of the hair. This twisting motion helps to create natural-looking curls.

Continue this process throughout your entire head of hair, working in small sections. Once all of your hair is curled, you can use your fingers to tousle the curls and create a more relaxed look. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to set the curls in place.
