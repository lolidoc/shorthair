Recommendation for Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50

If you have a round face and are over 50, here are some recommended shoulder length hairstyles that can flatter your face shape:

  1. Layered Bob: This hairstyle features layers that add volume and movement to your hair, while creating a slimming effect.
  2. Angled Lob: An angled lob is a longer version of a bob, with the front pieces slightly longer than the back. This hairstyle can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look.
  3. Shoulder-Length Curls: If you have naturally curly hair, embracing your curls can be a great option. Shoulder-length curls can add volume and texture to your hair, while flattering your face shape.
  4. Blunt Cut with Bangs: A blunt cut with bangs can help to frame your face and draw attention to your features. This hairstyle can also create a more balanced and symmetrical look.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50

Here are some tips to help you style your shoulder length hairstyle for a round face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and lift to your hair.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and volume.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying your hair to add lift at the roots.
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to create volume and hold.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.
