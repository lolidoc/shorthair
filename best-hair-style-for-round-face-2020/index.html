Q: Are bangs a good option for round faces?

A: Bangs can be a good option for round faces, but it is important to choose the right style. Side-swept bangs or layered bangs can help to create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round face. However, it is best to avoid pulling the hair back too tightly, as this can accentuate the roundness of the face. Instead, opt for a loose and relaxed ponytail.
