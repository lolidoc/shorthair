Short Haircuts for Guys with Round Faces

When it comes to short haircuts for guys with round faces, there are several options to choose from. One popular style is the classic crew cut. This haircut features short sides and back with slightly longer hair on top. The top can be styled with a bit of texture or left slightly messy for a more relaxed look.

Another great option is the undercut. This haircut involves shaving the sides and back very short while leaving the top longer. The longer hair on top can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back or styled into a pompadour. The undercut adds a modern and edgy touch to your look.

A buzz cut is also a popular choice for guys with round faces. This haircut involves cutting the hair very short all over the head. The buzz cut is low maintenance and provides a clean and polished look. It can help create a more angular appearance and highlight your facial features.

The high fade is another trendy option for guys with round faces. This haircut features a gradual fade from the sides and back to longer hair on top. The high fade adds a stylish and modern touch to your look and helps create height and volume on top.
