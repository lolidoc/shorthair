Short Pixie Cut - Bold and Edgy

A short pixie cut is a bold and edgy hairstyle that can make a statement. This style is characterized by short hair all around the head, with longer layers on top for added volume and texture. The short pixie cut can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. For example, if you have a round face, a pixie cut with volume at the crown can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. If you have a square face, a pixie cut with soft, asymmetrical layers can help to soften your features and add femininity.

Short Bob - Sophisticated and Elegant

A short bob is a classic and timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. This style is characterized by hair that is cut to chin or shoulder-length, with soft layers for added texture and movement. The short bob can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. For example, if you have a round face, a bob with side-swept bangs can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. If you have a square face, a bob with soft, face-framing layers can help to soften your features and add femininity.

Short Afro - Natural and Beautiful

A short afro is a natural and beautiful hairstyle that celebrates your natural hair texture. This style is characterized by hair that is cut very short all over the head, with the natural curl pattern on display. The short afro can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. For example, if you have a round face, a slightly longer afro with volume at the crown can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. If you have a square face, a shorter afro with soft, face-framing layers can help to soften your features and add femininity.

Short Cornrows - Trendy and Chic

Short cornrows are a trendy and chic hairstyle that can be customized to suit your individual style. This style is characterized by small, tight braids that are close to the scalp. The length of the cornrows can vary, depending on your preference. Short cornrows can be worn in various patterns and styles, such as straight back, zigzag, or curved. They can also be accessorized with beads, cuffs, or other hair accessories to add a personal touch. Short cornrows are a great option if you want a low-maintenance hairstyle that looks stylish and on-trend.
