Short African Hairstyles for Natural Hair

Introduction

Short African hairstyles are a popular choice for those with natural hair, as they offer versatility, low maintenance, and a stylish look. In this article, we will explore some trendy and chic short African hairstyles that you can try in 2023. Whether you have curly, coily, or kinky hair, there are plenty of options to suit your unique style and personality.

1. TWA (Teeny Weeny Afro)

The Teeny Weeny Afro, also known as TWA, is a classic short hairstyle for natural hair. It is perfect for those who want to embrace their natural texture and showcase their curls. This hairstyle requires minimal maintenance and can be styled in various ways, such as adding accessories or using different hair products for a defined or voluminous look.

2. Mohawk

The Mohawk hairstyle is a bold and edgy choice for those who want to make a statement. It involves shaving or cutting the sides of the hair short while leaving a strip of longer hair in the center. You can style the longer section in various ways, such as braiding, twisting, or adding curls for added texture and dimension.

3. Afro Puff

The Afro puff is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that works well for both casual and formal occasions. It involves gathering the hair into a puff at the crown of the head. You can leave the edges sleek for a polished look or add some texture by using a comb to lift the hair at the roots. This hairstyle can be accessorized with headbands, scarves, or hairpins to add a personal touch.

4. Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are a traditional African hairstyle that involves sectioning the hair and twisting it into small knots. This style not only looks trendy but also helps in protecting the hair and promoting growth. You can leave the knots as they are or unravel them for a beautiful twist-out or bantu knot-out hairstyle.

5. Frohawk

The Frohawk is a fun and stylish hairstyle that combines the elements of a fro and a mohawk. It involves creating two or more sections of hair and styling them in an upward direction, similar to a mohawk. This hairstyle allows you to showcase your natural curls while adding a unique twist.

6. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and chic hairstyle that works well for natural hair. It involves cutting the hair short all over, creating a sleek and sophisticated look. You can add some texture by using a texturizing spray or gel for a more defined and voluminous pixie cut.

7. Finger Coils

Finger coils are a great way to enhance your natural curl pattern and create a defined and elongated hairstyle. This technique involves twisting small sections of hair around your finger to create coils. You can leave the coils as they are or separate them slightly for a fuller look.

8. Twist-Out

The twist-out is a popular hairstyle for natural hair that involves twisting small sections of hair and unraveling them after they are dry. This creates defined and bouncy curls or waves. You can customize the size of the twists and the direction of the twist to achieve different looks.

9. Protective Styles

Protective styles, such as braids, twists, or cornrows, are a great option for short natural hair. They not only protect your hair from damage and breakage but also allow for low maintenance and versatility. You can experiment with different braid patterns, sizes, and lengths to find a style that suits you.

10. Head Wraps

If you want to switch up your look or protect your hair, head wraps are a stylish and practical option. They come in various colors, patterns, and fabrics, allowing you to express your individuality. You can wrap your hair in different styles, such as a turban or a headband, to add a fashionable touch to your short African hairstyle.
